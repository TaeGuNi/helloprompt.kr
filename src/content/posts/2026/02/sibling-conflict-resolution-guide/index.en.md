### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version

Use this when you need a structured, step-by-step mediation process for recurring conflicts.

> **Role (Role):** You are a fair, empathetic child psychologist and expert family mediator.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: An older sibling (8 years old) and a younger sibling (5 years old) are fighting over who gets to choose the TV channel.
> - Goal: To resolve the current conflict peacefully, make both children feel heard, and teach them how to negotiate for the future.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Write an opening script for a "Fairness Council" that invites both children to share their side of the story without interruption.
> 2. Ask guiding, age-appropriate questions that prompt them to acknowledge what the other person wants.
> 3. Propose three fun, neutral ways to find a compromise (e.g., Rock-Paper-Scissors, setting a 10-minute timer for each, or finding a show they both like).
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Strictly prohibit any hierarchical logic such as "You must yield because you are older" or "Let them have it because they are younger."
> - Ensure the tone is warm, objective, and engaging for young children.
> - Conclude the mediation with a fun, mandatory reconciliation mission (e.g., a secret handshake or a big hug).

## ğŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: What if my children are too young to understand complex questions?**
  - A: You can easily adjust the prompt by changing the ages in the `[Context]` section and adding a constraint like "Use simple vocabulary suitable for toddlers."

- **Q: Does this work for physical fights as well?**
  - A: Physical safety always comes first. Separate the children immediately if things get physical. Once they have calmed down completely, you can use this prompt to process their emotions and prevent future occurrences.

- **Q: Can I use the free version of ChatGPT for this?**
  - A: Absolutely. The Pro Version prompt is structured clearly enough that even free models like ChatGPT-3.5 or Gemini Flash will generate excellent, highly usable mediation scripts.

## ğŸ§¬ Prompt Dissection (Why it works?)

1. **Role Playing:** Assigning the AI the role of a "fair child psychologist" ensures the output uses empathetic, developmentally appropriate language.
