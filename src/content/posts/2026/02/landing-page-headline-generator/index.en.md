## üöÄ The Solution: "Hero Section Maker"

### ü•â Basic Version

Use this when you need quick inspiration or a rapid draft.

> **Role:** You are a world-class `[Web Copywriter]` and `[UX Writer]` specializing in high-converting landing pages.
> **Request:** Write a landing page Hero Section copy for `[My Product/Service]` that instantly hooks the target audience in 3 seconds.

<br>

### ü•á Pro Version (Expert)

Use this for production-ready, highly tailored copy that directly addresses your specific market.

> **Role (Role):** You are an elite `[Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Copywriter]` and `[UX Writer]`. Your expertise lies in crafting irresistible hooks that capture a user's attention within 3 seconds.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I need to design the Hero Section for our new landing page.
> - Goal: Maximize the click-through rate (CTR) on the primary Call-To-Action button.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> Please generate 3 distinct versions of the Hero Section based on the following product details:
>
> 1. Product Name: `[Insert Product Name]`
> 2. Target Audience's Pain Point: `[Describe the primary problem your customer faces]`
> 3. Our Solution/Differentiator: `[Explain how your product uniquely solves the problem]`
>
> For each version, provide the following 3 elements:
>
> - **Headline:** A bold, powerful hook. Summarize the core value in a single, punchy sentence.
> - **Sub-headline:** A supporting sentence that adds context, builds trust, and provides concrete details to back up the headline.
> - **CTA Button:** A highly actionable, low-friction button text that compels the user to click.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Keep the Headline strictly under 15 words. Make every word count.
> - The Sub-headline must not exceed 2 lines of text.
> - Use concrete, quantifiable benefits (e.g., "Save 10 hours," "Start for $0") instead of vague buzzwords like "Innovative," "Best," or "Synergy."
> - Present the output clearly in a Markdown format.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not invent features or statistics that are not provided in the prompt. Stick strictly to the provided context.

## üí° Writer's Insight (Insight)

Creating landing page copy often falls into the trap of "we-centric" writing‚Äîfocusing too much on what the company does rather than what the user gets. This prompt is engineered to force the AI into a "you-centric" mindset. By explicitly requiring the _Customer's Pain Point_ and demanding _concrete benefits_, the AI bypasses generic marketing fluff.

_Pro Tip:_ I highly recommend testing the "Problem-Solving Type" output first. Agitating a specific pain point in the headline ("Still doing X manually?") almost always outperforms generic benefit statements in initial A/B tests.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this work well with the free version of ChatGPT (GPT-3.5/GPT-4o-mini)?**
  - A: Yes, the basic structure will work fine. However, for nuanced, emotionally resonant copy that truly sounds like a seasoned UX writer, Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o will yield significantly better, more natural-sounding results.

- **Q: What if the generated copy feels too "salesy"?**
  - A: Simply add a constraint to the prompt: `Use a conversational, empathetic tone. Avoid aggressive sales jargon.` You can also specify the exact brand voice you want (e.g., `Tone: Playful and witty like Mailchimp`).

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Playing (CRO Expert):** Instructing the AI to act as a Conversion Rate Optimization expert shifts its output from generic text generation to performance-driven copywriting.
2. **Structural Enforcement:** By forcing the output into a specific trio (Headline + Sub-headline + CTA), you ensure the AI delivers a complete, structurally sound Hero Section rather than a disjointed paragraph.
3. **Anti-Buzzword Constraint:** The specific instruction to avoid words like "Innovative" and instead use concrete numbers is the secret sauce here. AI models love using fluffy adjectives; this constraint forces them to be precise and persuasive.
