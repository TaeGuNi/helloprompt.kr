### ğŸ” Explanation

- `^`: Start of string
- `01([0|1|6|7|8|9])`: Starts with 01 followed by one of 0,1,6,7,8,9 (Identifier)
- `-?`: Hyphen (-) exists 0 or 1 time (Optional)
- `([0-9]{3,4})`: Numbers 0-9 appear 3 or 4 times (Middle digits)
- `([0-9]{4})`: Numbers 0-9 appear 4 times (Last digits)
- `$`: End of string
