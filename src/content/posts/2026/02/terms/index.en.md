## 💡 Writer's Insight

When we hear "Terms of Service," we typically envision a massive, intimidating wall of legal text. However, Hello Prompt's terms are designed differently—they act as a brief, mutual promise detailing **"how we will protect and nurture this shared space of knowledge."**

In particular, **Article 3 (Limitation of Liability)** is a crucial principle we must all keep in mind during the AI era. Think of AI as an incredibly capable, tireless Copilot. However, never forget that **you are the Pilot**—the final decision-maker who ultimately bears the responsibility for the output. Rather than blindly trusting the generated results, developing the habit of aggressively tweaking, verifying, and validating the AI's responses to fit your specific context is the true beginning of **'Prompt Engineering.'**

If the bizarre output persists, it might be an AI hallucination—so rigorous fact-checking is always mandatory.
