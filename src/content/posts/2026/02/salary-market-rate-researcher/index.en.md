### ü•á Pro Version (Expert Strategy)

Use this when you need highly detailed, data-backed evidence for a formal negotiation.

> **Role (Role):** You are an expert salary compensation analyst and career strategist.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Job Title: `[e.g., Backend Developer]`
> - Years of Experience: `[e.g., 5 years]`
> - Industry: `[e.g., Fintech]`
> - Location: `[e.g., San Francisco, CA]`
> - Current Salary: `[e.g., $110,000]`
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Search reliable salary information platforms (such as Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, Payscale, etc.) to investigate the precise salary range for professionals with my exact profile.
> 2. Present the data clearly categorized into **'Low (Bottom 25%) - Median - High (Top 25%)'** tiers.
> 3. Based on my `[Current Salary]`, suggest a logical and strategic **'Target Negotiation Amount'** and provide three compelling arguments I can use in the meeting.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - You MUST cite the sources of your data (e.g., specific websites or reports) to ensure the negotiation grounds are credible.
> - Output the salary breakdown in a clear Markdown format.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not hallucinate or invent salary figures. If accurate data for this specific niche is unavailable, state clearly that the data is limited and provide the closest related industry benchmark.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this work for non-tech industries?**
  - A: Absolutely. While sites like Levels.fyi are tech-focused, the AI will pull from broader databases like Glassdoor, Payscale, or local job boards if you specify your industry clearly in the prompt.

- **Q: What if the AI's median salary is lower than my current salary?**
  - A: That's incredibly valuable information, too! It means you are currently paid above the average market rate. In this case, pivot your negotiation strategy away from a "market adjustment" and focus entirely on your unique impact, recent wins, and the specific ROI you bring to the company.

- **Q: Can I use this for a new job offer I just received?**
  - A: Yes! Just change the `[Current Salary]` field to the `[Offered Salary]` and ask the AI to formulate a counter-offer email based on the market gap it discovers.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Assignment:** Positioning the AI as a "compensation analyst" ensures the tone is professional, objective, and analytical rather than conversational.
2. **Specific Context:** By breaking down the user profile (Industry, Location, Years of Experience), the AI filters out irrelevant data (e.g., a junior salary in Ohio vs. a senior salary in New York).
3. **Data Source Mandate:** Forcing the AI to cite sources (Constraints) ensures you aren't bringing hallucinated numbers to your HR department.
