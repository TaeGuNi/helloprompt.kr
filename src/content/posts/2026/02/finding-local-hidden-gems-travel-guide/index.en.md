- **Q: Does this work well with free AI models?**
  - A: It works, but you might still get slightly generic answers. For niche, hyper-local recommendations, models with broader real-time web search capabilities or advanced reasoning (like GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, or Perplexity) yield significantly better results.

  - A: AI models can sometimes rely on outdated data. Always double-check the suggested locations on Google Maps to confirm their current operating hours before visiting.

- **Q: How can I adjust this prompt for a family trip?**
  - A: Simply add a parameter to the Constraints: "Must be family-friendly and accessible with a stroller," and change the Context to "Traveling with young children."
