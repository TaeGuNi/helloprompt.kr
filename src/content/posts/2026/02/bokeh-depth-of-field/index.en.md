> _"I want the subject to pop with crystal clarity while the background melts away into a dreamy haze."_

**Bokeh** refers to the aesthetic quality of the out-of-focus blur in a photograph. By mastering **Shallow Depth of Field** prompts, you can instantly direct the viewer's gaze to your subject and infuse your AI-generated images with a cinematic, romantic atmosphere.

---

## ‚ö°Ô∏è 3-Line Summary (TL;DR)

1. **Keywords are Key:** Terms like `Bokeh` and `Blurry background` are non-negotiable for this effect.
2. **Camera Settings Matter:** Explicitly commanding aperture values such as `Shallow depth of field` or `f/1.8` forces the AI to simulate a real DSLR lens.
3. **Environment Selection:** Nighttime city streets with scattered light sources yield the most dramatic and beautiful bokeh light orbs.

## üöÄ The Solution: "Cinematic Bokeh Prompt"

### ü•â Basic Version

Use this when you need a quick, beautiful blur without overthinking the technical details.

> **Role:** You are an expert photographer.
> **Request:** Create an image of a `[Subject]` with a beautifully blurred background featuring emotional, glowing light orbs (Bokeh). Make the subject stand out sharply against the soft background.

<br>

### ü•á Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need absolute control over the lighting, focus, and atmospheric depth.

> **Role:** You are an award-winning cinematic portrait photographer.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: A bustling cyberpunk city street at night, raining slightly.
> - Goal: To capture a deeply emotional portrait where the subject is in razor-sharp focus while the background dissolves into a masterpiece of bokeh.
>
> **Request:**
>
> 1. Subject: `[Describe your subject, e.g., A jazz musician playing the saxophone]`.
> 2. Camera: `[Camera settings, e.g., 85mm lens, f/1.2 aperture, shallow depth of field, sharp focus on the subject's face]`.
> 3. Environment: `[Background details, e.g., Colorful neon city lights reflecting off wet pavement, blurred into soft glowing orbs (Bokeh)]`.
> 4. Style: Cinematic mood, hyper-realistic, volumetric lighting, 8k resolution.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Ensure the transition between the sharp subject and the blurred background is realistic and smooth.
> - Do not make the background elements recognizable; they must be completely out of focus.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Avoid over-sharpening the entire image. The contrast between sharp and soft focus is crucial.

## üí° Writer's Insight

This prompt structure is incredibly powerful because it bridges the gap between AI generation and actual photography physics. AI models like Midjourney and DALL-E 3 have been trained on millions of tagged photographs. When you feed them specific lens terminology like "f/1.2 aperture" or "85mm lens," they stop rendering the image as a flat illustration and start simulating the optical physics of a high-end camera.

In my experience, simply saying "blur the background" often results in an unnatural, artificial smudge. However, using the word "Bokeh" triggers the AI to render those specific, beautiful, circular light artifacts that make professional photography so captivating. It's the ultimate "cheat code" for making AI images look authentically photographic.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this work the same across all AI image generators?**
  - A: While the core concept works everywhere, Midjourney tends to understand camera specifications (like f-stops and focal lengths) much better than DALL-E 3. For DALL-E 3, you might need to emphasize descriptive words like "dreamy background light orbs" more heavily.

- **Q: Why are my subjects sometimes blurry too?**
  - A: This happens when the AI misunderstands the focal point. Always explicitly state "sharp focus on the subject's face" or "crisp detail on the main subject" to ensure the blur is confined strictly to the background.

- **Q: Can I get bokeh effects during the daytime?**
  - A: Absolutely! While night scenes with neon lights produce the most obvious glowing orbs, daytime shots with dappled sunlight coming through tree leaves will create stunning, soft-edged daylight bokeh.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1.  **Role Assignment:** By declaring the AI an "award-winning cinematic portrait photographer," you set a high baseline for the aesthetic quality of the output.
2.  **Optical Terminology:** Words like "shallow depth of field" and "f/1.2" constrain the AI to specific physical rendering rules, preventing a flat depth map.
3.  **Specific Focus Directions:** The prompt doesn't just ask for a blur; it explicitly dictates _what_ should be sharp and _how_ the background should behave (melting into orbs).
