> _"If you want to escape the 9-to-5 by your 40s, exactly how much do you need to start saving today?"_

Have you heard of the '4% Rule', the core principle of the FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) movement? It's the theory that if you invest your retirement funds in a diversified portfolio (like US index funds) and withdraw just 4% annually, your nest egg can sustain you indefinitely without depleting the principal. This prompt acts as your personal financial planner, pinpointing the exact year you can confidently hand in your resignation letter based on your current savings rate.

## ‚ö°Ô∏è 3-Line Summary (TL;DR)

1. Determine your FIRE Number by multiplying your annual living expenses by 25.
2. Calculate your exact retirement timeline based on your current savings and investment rate.
3. Simulate how accelerating your savings can drastically bring your retirement date forward.

> - **Current Net Worth/Investments:** `[$100,000]`
> - **Annual Savings Contribution:** `[$30,000]` (approx. $2,500/month)
> - **Expected Investment Return:** Assume an annual return of `[8%]`
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Calculate my **Target FIRE Asset (FIRE Number)** based on the 25x annual expense rule.
> 2. Assuming I continue saving and investing at my current rate, **at what exact age will I reach my goal** and be able to retire?
> 3. Provide a simulation: If I increase my monthly savings by `[$500]`, how many years earlier can I retire?
> 4. Briefly explain the **'Sequence of Returns Risk'** and how I should prepare for it during the early years of my retirement.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Assume an average inflation rate of 3% and calculate using the real return rate (5%).
> - Format the final output cleanly using bullet points and bold text for key numbers.

## üí° Writer's Insight (Insight)

This prompt transforms overwhelming financial math into an actionable, highly motivating roadmap. The true power of this specific prompt lies in the **"Acceleration Simulation" (Task 3)**. By asking the AI to show the impact of just a slight increase in monthly savings, it changes your mindset from "retirement is decades away" to "if I skip a few luxury purchases a month, I buy back two full years of my life."

Furthermore, including the **Sequence of Returns Risk** ensures the AI doesn't just give you a fantasy number, but provides a realistic, bulletproof strategy that accounts for potential market downturns right after you officially retire.

---

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this account for inflation?**
  - A: Yes! In the Pro Version, the constraints explicitly instruct the AI to calculate your trajectory using a "real return rate" (expected return minus inflation), giving you a much more accurate timeline based on future purchasing power.

- **Q: Can I use this if I have debt?**
  - A: Absolutely. Just subtract your total debt from your "Current Net Worth/Investments" variable. If the number is negative, the AI will factor in your debt payoff period before calculating your wealth accumulation.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Explicit Variable Mapping:** By breaking down age, expenses, net worth, and savings into clear bullet points, the AI doesn't have to guess your financial state and avoids hallucinating numbers.
2. **Realistic Constraints:** Forcing the AI to use a 5% real return (8% market return - 3% inflation) ensures the projection is conservative and grounded in reality, rather than overly optimistic.
3. **Risk Awareness:** Prompting the AI to address the "Sequence of Returns Risk" adds a layer of professional financial advisory, ensuring you don't retire blindly into a bear market.
