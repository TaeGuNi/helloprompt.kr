The most difficult step in writing is 'starting'. Because thoughts are tangled in the head about what to write. Don't try to write sentences blindly, but set up an Outline first. This prompt draws a solid blueprint by gathering your idea pieces.

## ‚ö°Ô∏è 3-Line Summary (TL;DR)

2. Recommend sub-topics and evidence materials to go into each paragraph
3. Propose 'Hook' sentences to catch reader's interest and 'Impact' of conclusion

### ü•á Pro Version

Use this when you need detailed quality.

Copy the **PROMPT** below and use it.

> **Role:** You are a `[Bestselling Author and Essay Professional Coach]`.
>
> **Context:** I have to write an essay/report on `[Topic]`. My argument is `[Core Argument]`, but I don't know how to develop the writing logically and persuasively.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **[Outline Creation]**: Set up a standard outline of Introduction-Body (3 steps)-Conclusion.
> 2. **[Paragraph Guide]**: Give specific keywords on what content to write and what examples to cite for each paragraph.
> 3. **[Hook & Closing]**: Recommend the first sentence of introduction (Hook to catch reader's eye) and the last sentence of conclusion (Comment leaving lingering imagery).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Logical Flow must be natural.
> - Include one Counter-argument against the opposing opinion in the body.
>
> **Topic:** `[e.g.: Will AI replace human jobs?]`
> **My thought:** `[e.g.: It will replace but new opportunities will also arise.]`

## üìä Proof: Before & After

### ‚ùå Before (Stream of Consciousness)

AI is dangerous. But convenient. Will jobs decrease? Maybe not. Anyway we have to use AI well. (Incoherent)

### ‚úÖ After (Prompt Result)

**[Outline]**

1. **Introduction**: Start with story of people destroying textile machines during Industrial Revolution (Luddite movement) -> Connect to current AI fear.
2. **Body 1 (Crisis)**: Extinction of simple repetitive tasks is inevitable (Present data).
3. **Body 2 (Rebuttal)**: However, areas of emotion, creativity, ethical judgment are irreplaceable.
4. **Body 3 (Opportunity)**: Birth of new job group called 'AI Utilization Capable Person' who utilizes AI as a tool.
5. **Conclusion**: Don't compete with AI but command AI.

"10 years later, will your boss be AI, or a person who handles AI?"

"It becomes a disaster to those who fear change, but wings to those who ride it."
