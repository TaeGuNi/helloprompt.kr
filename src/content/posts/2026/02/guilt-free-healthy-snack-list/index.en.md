## üöÄ Solution: "The Healthy Snack Vending Machine"

### ü•â Basic Version

Use this when you need immediate recommendations without a complex setup.

> **Role:** You are a gourmet dessert critic and a nutritionist specializing in healthy eating. You have a refined palate and absolutely refuse to eat anything bland or tasteless.
> **Request:** Fake hunger just hit me at 3 PM. Generate an endless list of healthy snack ideas that won't ruin my diet but will effectively relieve my stress and satisfy my cravings.

<br>

### ü•á Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need highly targeted, specific recommendations and actionable recipes.

> **Role:** You are a gourmet dessert critic and a nutritionist specializing in healthy eating. You have a refined palate and absolutely refuse to eat anything bland or tasteless.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: It's 3 PM and I am intensely craving `[something sweet and crunchy / something salty and spicy]`.
> - Goal: I want to satisfy my craving while strictly limiting my intake of `[sugar / refined flour]`.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Provide **5 alternative snack ideas** that completely satisfy my current craving without compromising my health goals.
> 2. Recommend **3 commercial products** that I can buy right now at a typical **convenience store or supermarket**, specifying the brand or product type.
> 3. Provide **1 ultra-simple recipe** that I can make at home in under 5 minutes using just an **air fryer or microwave**.
> 4. Briefly list the estimated calories and sugar content for each recommendation.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Output the recommendations using a clear, easy-to-read list format.
> - Exclude overly generic diet foods like konjac jelly, plain chicken breast, or raw tomatoes.
> - The recommendations must closely mimic the taste and texture of real "junk food" or popular treats.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - If you are unsure about a commercial product's nutritional information or availability, clearly state that it is an estimate. Do not invent or hallucinate brand details.

## üí° Writer's Insight

This prompt is an absolute lifesaver when you're teetering on the edge of a diet-ruining binge. The genius of this approach lies in its constraints: by explicitly banning "generic diet foods like konjac jelly," you force the AI to think outside the box and find genuinely satisfying alternatives.

I personally use this whenever I'm craving salty potato chips. The AI suggested microwaving a slice of low-fat cheese on parchment paper for 90 seconds‚Äîit turns into a perfectly crunchy, salty cheese crisp that hits exactly the same spot as a Dorito, but with zero carbs and high protein. - **Q: Does this work well with the free version of ChatGPT?**
  - A: Yes, the free version of ChatGPT (GPT-3.5 or GPT-4o-mini) handles this perfectly. Since it doesn't require complex data analysis or logic, any standard conversational AI can generate excellent snack recommendations.

- **Q: What if I have specific dietary restrictions, like being vegan or gluten-free?**
  - A: Simply add your restriction to the **Context** section of the prompt! For example: _"I am strictly limiting my intake of [sugar], and I am also [vegan / gluten-free]."_ The AI will instantly filter its recommendations to match your specific dietary needs.
