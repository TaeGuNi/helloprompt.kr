_"If you can't explain it, you don't understand it." - Richard Feynman_

If you only read books with your eyes, it's easy to mistake that you 'know' (illusion of fluency). But when you actually try to explain, words don't come out. The Feynman technique is a study method of teaching assuming "explaining to an imaginary elementary school student". Now, don't mumble to the wall, teach an AI student.
