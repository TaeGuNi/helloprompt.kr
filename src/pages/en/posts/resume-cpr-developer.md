### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version (STAR Method Converter)

This is the real deal. Turns plain experience into 'Achievement'.

> **Role:** You are a Professional Resume Consultant for Google/Facebook candidates.
>
> **Input (My Experience):**
> `[Images on shopping mall were slow, so I improved loading speed. Added resizing and caching.]`
>
> **Task:**
> Reconstruct this into **STAR Method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)**.
> And summarize into a one-line **Bullet Point**.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> 1.  **Quantification:** Result MUST have numbers (%, ms, sec). (If you don't know exact numbers, estimate 'approx XX% improvement')
> 2.  **Tech Stack:** Specify what you used (AWS S3, Lambda, Redis, etc.).
> 3.  **Action Verbs:** End with strong verbs like "Achieved", "Improved", "Reduced".
