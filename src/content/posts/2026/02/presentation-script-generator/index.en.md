### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need a highly polished, engaging script with seamless transitions and audience interaction.

Copy the **PROMPT** below and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

> **Role (Role):** You are an expert TED Talk Presentation Coach and Master Storyteller.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I am going to give a presentation about `[Topic]` to `[Target Audience]`.
> - Goal: To deliver a compelling, easy-to-understand presentation that keeps the audience engaged from start to finish.
> - Slide Details: `[Insert brief summary or bullet points of your slide contents here]`
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Write a script for each slide that sounds natural when spoken aloud.
> 2. Insert seamless transition phrases (Transition) when moving from one slide to the next.
> 3. Strategically place rhetorical questions or light humor points between slides to capture and retain the audience's attention.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Use a conversational, spoken tone (Avoid overly formal or written-style language).
> - Keep the length of each slide's script to approximately 1 to 2 minutes of speaking time.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not invent any facts or statistics that are not provided in the slide details. If you lack information, simply focus on the flow and structure based on what is given.

## ğŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Do I need to provide the entire text of my presentation to the AI?**
  - A: Not necessarily. Providing bullet points or the core message of each slide is usually enough. The AI will flesh out the spoken narrative based on those key points.

- **Q: What if the generated script sounds too artificial or not like my own voice?**
  - A: You can add an extra constraint to the prompt, such as: "Match the tone of a casual, energetic startup founder" or "Keep the tone strictly professional and corporate." Adjusting the persona will help tailor the voice to your personal style.

## ğŸ§¬ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Assignment:** Giving the AI the "TED Talk Coach" persona ensures the output prioritizes engagement, clear articulation, and storytelling over dry data recitation.
2. **Focus on Transitions:** Explicitly asking for "connecting comments" prevents the presentation from feeling disjointed, guiding the audience smoothly through your narrative arc.
3. **Conversational Constraints:** By enforcing a "spoken language" constraint, the prompt prevents the AI from generating dense, academic text that is difficult to read aloud naturally.
