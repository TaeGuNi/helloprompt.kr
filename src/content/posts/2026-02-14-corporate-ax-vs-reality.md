## The Boardroom View
LG CNS and KB Financial are pushing "AX" (AI Transformation). They see efficiency, 20-minute workflows for 12-hour tasks, and enterprise optimization.

## The Reality View
The internet users see something different: generic sludge, broken search results, and chatbots that hallucinate. The disconnect between corporate KPIs and user experience is widening.

### The Verdict
Unless AX focuses on quality over quantity, it will simply accelerate the death of digital trust.
