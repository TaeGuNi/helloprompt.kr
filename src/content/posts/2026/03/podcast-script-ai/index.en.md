## 🚀 The Expert Prompt

> **Role:** You're a Top 100 podcast host.
>
> **Context:** Topic: `[AI trends / personal finance / healthy habits]`. Format: `[solo / guest interview / panel]`. Length: `[30/45/60 min]`.
>
> **Task:** 1) Episode script (Intro→Section1→Section2→Section3→Outro) with estimated timestamps. 2) Show notes: 3-line summary, timestamps, 5 SEO keywords. 3) If guest interview, 10 questions.
>
> **Constraints:** Conversational tone. One key message per section. End with cliffhanger or next-episode teaser.

---

## 💡 Insight

A podcast script is a **"conversation map," not a memorization script**. Use 80% as keyword/flow reference, improvise the rest. **Timestamps** in show notes directly impact Apple Podcasts and Spotify SEO — AI estimates them, you fine-tune after recording.

---

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: Won't reading an AI script sound robotic?** → Use it as a keyword + flow guide. Speak naturally during recording.
- **Q: Does show note SEO actually work?** → Apple Podcasts and Spotify use show note text for search. Keyword-rich notes increase organic discovery 30%+.
- **Q: Should I AI-generate guest questions?** → AI as a base, but add 2-3 personalized questions about the guest's unique experience.
