## ğŸ§¬ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **"Watercolor sketch, loose brushstrokes"**: Instructs the AI to avoid filling every pixel, leaving "breathing room" (negative space) in the composition.
2. **"Delicate graphite lines"**: Acts as the structural skeleton of the image. Without this, AI watercolors often melt into an unrecognizable, mushy blob.
3. **"Cold-pressed white paper texture"**: Grounds the image in physical reality. The AI will render the subtle bumps and grain of real watercolor paper, drastically elevating the analog feel.
