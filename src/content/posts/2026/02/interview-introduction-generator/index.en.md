> _"Take a deep breath. Let's start with a 1-minute self-introduction."_

Recruiters endure dozens of identical introductions every single day. Vague adjectives like "diligent" or "passionate" instantly evaporate from their memory. It is time to eliminate the fluff and deliver a commanding hook forged from **hard numbers and proven impact**. 

Leverage an AI copywriter to transform your seemingly ordinary background into a **"Headline News"** pitch that immediately seizes the interview panel's attention.

1. Feed the AI your core experiences alongside the target Job Description (JD).
2. Command the AI to generate a script driven by a **"Bottom-Line First Hook"** and **"Quantified Achievements"**.
3. Refine the output to match your natural speaking cadence and practice until it flows effortlessly.

## 🚀 The Solution: "Interview Script Writer"

### 🥉 Basic Version (For Entry-Level & New Grads)

Use this when you need to convey your passion and potential with maximum impact.

> **Role (Persona):** You are an HR Director at a top-tier enterprise and a lead interviewer with 10 years of experience.
>
> **Context:**
> - Applicant's Background: `[Business Administration major, President of a university marketing club, 1 year of part-time experience at a convenience store]`
> - Target Position: `[Entry-level B2C Consumer Goods Marketer]`
>
> **Task:**
> Craft a 1-minute self-introduction script designed to immediately capture the interview panel's attention.
>
> 1. Highlight "hands-on experience identifying customer needs in the field" as the core competitive advantage.
> 2. Eliminate abstract fluff (e.g., "hard-working," "passionate") and replace it with a brief, concrete anecdote.
> 3. Structure the script using short, conversational sentences so it sounds completely natural when spoken aloud.

### 🥇 Pro Version (For Experienced Professionals)

Use this to logically and confidently project yourself as a "plug-and-play" asset ready to deliver immediate value.

> **Role (Persona):** You are a top-tier headhunter and executive career coach specializing in C-level recruitment.
>
> **Context:**
> - Target Role: `[Performance Marketer / Growth Hacker at a Series B Tech Startup]`
> - Core Competencies: `[1. Reduced CAC by 30% via A/B test optimization, 2. SQL-based cohort data analysis, 3. Fearless execution of rapid hypothesis testing]`
>
> **Task:**
> Write an unforgettable 1-minute self-introduction script that permanently etches the candidate into the interviewer's memory.
>
> 1. **Hook:** Open with an intuitive, bold one-liner (e.g., "I am `[Your Name]`, a marketer who translates data into revenue.").
> 2. **Body:** Logically weave in the '30% CAC reduction' achievement, strictly backing it up with the provided hard numbers.
> 3. **Closing:** Conclude with a concrete vision detailing exactly how my proven expertise will accelerate the company's explosive growth.
>
> **Constraints:**
> - Strictly limit the word count to fit a 50-second delivery at a normal speaking pace (approximately 130–150 words).
> - Tone & Manner: Professional, highly confident, yet grounded and approachable (Confident but Humble).
>
> **Warning:**
> - Do not hallucinate or fabricate any numbers, metrics, or experiences outside of the provided context.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

An AI-generated introduction is a **first draft**, not a finished masterpiece. The most detrimental mistake you can make is memorizing this script word-for-word and reciting it like a robot in the interview room.

The true objective of a 1-minute introduction is to cast a strategic bait that screams, **"Ask me about this!"**—empowering you to seize control of the entire interview narrative. Instead of memorizing sentences, memorize the three most critical keywords from your generated script. Furthermore, always prompt the AI with a follow-up: **"Based on this script, predict 3 aggressive pressure questions the interviewers might ask, and provide model answers for each."** This ensures you are bulletproof against any unexpected follow-up attacks.

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: The script feels stiff and unnatural when I read it aloud.**
  - A: AI models default to a formal, written literary style. Once the initial script is generated, use this follow-up prompt: **"Rewrite this script in a highly conversational tone, as if I am speaking naturally to a senior colleague. Vary the sentence lengths and endings to eliminate any robotic rigidity."**

- **Q: What if I have virtually no professional experience to highlight?**
  - A: For entry-level positions, you do not need grandiose, world-changing achievements. In the core competencies section, simply detail **the specific attitude and process you utilized to solve a minor problem** (e.g., mediating a conflict during a university team project, or executing meticulous research for an assignment). AI is remarkably adept at extracting compelling "job fit" narratives from seemingly trivial daily experiences.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works)

1. **Persona Optimization:** By assigning the AI the role of a "top-tier headhunter" rather than a generic writing assistant, we force it to utilize sophisticated phrasing that directly aligns with current hiring trends and recruiter psychology.
2. **Framework Enforcement:** Mandating the proven speech framework of 'Hook -> Body (Quantified Achievements) -> Closing' guarantees a logical, high-impact narrative arc with a distinct beginning, middle, and end.
3. **Timing Control:** Setting a strict constraint of '50 seconds' prevents the script from rambling, ensuring you lock in the precise word count required for an authoritative, unhurried speaking tempo.
