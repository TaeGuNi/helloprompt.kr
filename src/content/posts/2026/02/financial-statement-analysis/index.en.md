## üöÄ The Solution: "Financial Statement X-Ray"

### ü•â Basic Version (Quick Scan)

Use this when you need a rapid, high-level summary of a company's financials.

> **Role:** You are a `[Senior Equity Analyst]` with 20 years of experience on Wall Street.
> **Request:** I will provide the recent financial statement data for `[Company Name]`. Summarize the key takeaways in 1 minute, focusing only on the metrics that matter most for a retail stock investor.

### ü•á Pro Version (Deep Dive)

Use this for a comprehensive, professional-grade fundamental analysis. Paste the raw financial statement text or earnings report data along with this prompt.

> **Role (Role):** You are a `[Senior Equity Analyst]` with 20 years of experience on Wall Street.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I need to analyze the fundamental health, profitability, and growth potential of `[Company Name]`.
> - Goal: To extract actionable insights from highly complex financial statements without getting lost in the noise.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Analyze the 3-year historical trends for **Revenue, Operating Profit, and Net Income**.
> 2. Calculate or extract key solvency and profitability metrics: **Debt-to-Equity Ratio, Current Ratio, and ROE (Return on Equity)**.
> 3. Based on the data, rate the **"Fundamental Investment Attractiveness"** as High, Medium, or Low, and provide 3 concrete reasons for your rating.
> 4. Translate all complex accounting jargon into plain English so a beginner investor can easily understand the company's true financial standing.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Format the core metrics in a clear Markdown table.
> - Base all your analysis strictly on the provided objective figures.
> - Do not provide direct financial advice or definitive buy/sell recommendations; offer an objective analytical perspective instead.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - If any critical data points are missing from the provided text, do not hallucinate them. Explicitly state that the data is missing.

It forces the AI to act strictly as an analytical engine rather than a search engine, guaranteeing high-fidelity insights.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I use the free version of ChatGPT for this?**
  - A: Yes, but it's highly recommended to use advanced models like GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, or Gemini 2.5 Pro. Financial analysis requires rigorous logic and large context windows, which the free tiers sometimes struggle with when handling long documents.

- **Q: How do I get the financial statement text to paste?**
  - A: You can visit Yahoo Finance, go to the 'Financials' tab of your target stock, and simply copy the text from the Income Statement and Balance Sheet. Alternatively, you can upload the official SEC 10-K PDF directly if your AI model supports file uploads.

- **Q: Will this prompt tell me exactly what stock to buy?**
  - A: No. The prompt is strictly designed to evaluate _fundamental health_, not to give financial advice. It will tell you if the company is bleeding cash or growing profitably, but you must make the final investment decision yourself.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Playing:** By assigning the persona of a "Wall Street Senior Equity Analyst," the AI automatically adopts a highly analytical, precise, and professional tone.
2. **Structured Outputs:** Forcing the AI to use Markdown tables and clear lists prevents the output from becoming a massive, unreadable wall of text.
3. **Anti-Hallucination Constraints:** The strict directive to analyze _only_ objective figures and to admit when missing data ("Warning" section) drastically reduces the risk of the AI making up financial numbers.
