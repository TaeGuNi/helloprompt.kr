**[10-Minute Running Recovery Routine]**

**1. Immediate Cool-down Standing (Using Park Bench)**

- **Calf Stretch:** Push a bench or wall and press the heel of the back leg to the ground. (30 sec each side)
- **Hamstring Stretch:** Put one leg on the bench and bend your upper body. (30 sec each side)

**2. Foam Roller Care at Home (Focus on Outer Knee Pain)**

- **Iliotibial Band Massage:** Lie on your side, place the foam roller on the outside of your thigh, and roll up and down. (It might hurt a lot, but endure for 1 minute!)
- _Tip:_ If it hurts too much, support the floor with the opposite leg to distribute weight.
