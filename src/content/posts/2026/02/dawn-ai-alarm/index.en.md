## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: How do I actually use this script as a morning alarm?**
  - A: The easiest way is to read the script aloud and record it using your phone's Voice Memos app, then set that audio file as your alarm tone. For a more advanced setup, use an iOS Shortcut (Personal Automation) or Android Tasker to read the text using Siri or Google Assistant right when your alarm goes off.

- **Q: Won't I get used to the script and start ignoring it after a few days?**
  - A: That's a valid concern known as "alarm fatigue." To prevent this, I highly recommend generating a fresh script every 1 to 2 weeks. Updating it with your latest micro-goals and immediate daily tasks keeps the message highly relevant and impactful.

## üß¨ Anatomy of the Prompt (Why it works?)

1.  **Emotional Anchoring:** By referencing your `[Long-term Goal]`, the prompt connects an immediate, painful action (waking up early) to a deeply desired outcome.
