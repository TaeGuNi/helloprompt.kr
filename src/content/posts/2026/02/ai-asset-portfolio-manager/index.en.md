## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can it provide detailed analysis down to individual Korean stocks?**
  - A: AI is excellent at grasping the macro trends of global top-tier large-cap stocks like Samsung Electronics or Apple. However, for small and mid-cap themed stocks on the KOSDAQ or deep financial information on individual companies, there is a high probability of **hallucination (generating false information)** due to a lack of training data. Therefore, asking questions at the 'Sector' or 'Asset Class' level, such as "20% weight in the Korean semiconductor sector," will lead to much more accurate and meaningful feedback.

- **Q: Can I get advice on investment weights for cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin?**
  - A: Yes, it is definitely possible. However, cryptocurrencies have extremely high volatility compared to traditional financial assets. For this reason, AI tends to give conservative advice, basically limiting the weight to **around 1–5%** of the total portfolio. If you explicitly add a condition to the 'Warning' section of the prompt to "consider Bitcoin as an alternative investment asset like digital gold," you can obtain a more flexible and aggressive rebalancing answer.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **The Power of Downside Rigidity (MDD) Conditions:** By giving the goals of "8% annual return" and "defend MDD within -10%" simultaneously, these two conflicting indicators act as the most powerful key triggers. They systematically block the AI from the risk of going 'all-in' on a single asset and force it to precisely combine **defensive assets with low price correlation**.
2. **The Strength of a Proven Persona:** By inputting specific and authoritative keywords like "Senior Wall Street Hedge Fund Manager" and "Ray Dalio," the AI generates **professional institutional-level answers** based strictly on 'Modern Portfolio Theory,' rather than the light, unfounded opinions found in internet communities.
3. **Blocking Individual Stock Predictions:** The Warning section strictly prohibits meaningless short-term price predictions. By controlling the AI to focus only on macroeconomic flows and correlations at the asset class level, the objectivity and quality of the facts in the output are overwhelmingly increased.
