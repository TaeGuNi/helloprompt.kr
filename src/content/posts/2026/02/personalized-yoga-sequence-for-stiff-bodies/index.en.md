## 🚀 Solution: "Personal Yoga Teacher"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Deploy this prompt when you need a frictionless, no-nonsense routine to instantly loosen up.

> **Role:** You are an expert yoga instructor specializing in rehabilitation therapy and absolute beginners.
>
> **Request:** Please create a gentle, exceptionally easy-to-follow, 15-minute home yoga sequence for someone with severe physical stiffness who feels too intimidated to attend a public yoga class.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this advanced framework to generate a hyper-personalized, meticulously detailed sequence engineered for your exact physical condition.

> **Role:** You are a senior yoga instructor with profound expertise in anatomical rehabilitation therapy. You excel at crafting gentle, restorative sequences that highly inflexible individuals can follow without experiencing pain or strain.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I am an office worker who remains seated at a desk for over 8 hours a day. My `[shoulders and neck]` are chronically stiff, and my `[hip flexors]` feel locked and sore. My current flexibility level is so restricted that `[my hands can only reach my knees when I fold forward]`.
> - Goal: I want to safely restore my mobility, incrementally improve my flexibility, and melt away daily physical tension.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Design a **15-minute home yoga sequence** explicitly formulated to release tension in my specified problem areas.
> 2. Structure the session with a logical, professional flow: Introduction (Centering/Breathwork) → Warm-up → Main Sequence → Cool-down (Savasana).
> 3. Provide crystal-clear **'Posture Tips'** and precise **'Breathing Cues (Inhale/Exhale)'** for every individual movement.
> 4. For any pose that presents a challenge, you must provide a highly accessible **modification** tailored for absolute beginners.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Eliminate esoteric jargon. If Sanskrit terms are used, they must be immediately followed by plain English translations (e.g., Adho Mukha Svanasana -> Downward-Facing Dog).
> - Format the routine clearly using clean, scannable Markdown.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Under no circumstances should you recommend advanced poses like headstands, deep backbends, or intense hamstring stretches that risk injury. Strictly prioritize foundational safety and gentle, restorative stretching.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

This prompt is a game-changer because it strategically neutralizes the **fear of failure** that plagues most yoga novices. By brutally declaring your physical limitations upfront (e.g., "my hands can only reach my knees"), you force the AI to completely recalibrate its baseline definition of a "beginner," preventing it from suggesting overly ambitious contortions.

In practical application, meticulously detailing your lifestyle habits—like being chained to a chair for eight hours—empowers the AI to prescribe targeted antidote poses. It acts as a digital physical therapist, specifically counteracting the rounded shoulders and shortened hip flexors caused by modern sedentary life. Furthermore, you hold the ultimate **constraint control**: if a prescribed movement feels too intense, simply hit reply with, _"Pose #3 is still too aggressive for my lower back. Give me a regression."_ The AI will instantly pivot, adapting the routine in real-time so your practice remains a source of relief, never frustration.

- **Q: Do I need a premium AI subscription to generate these routines?**

- **Q: What is the optimal frequency for practicing this custom sequence?**
  - A: Consistency triumphs over intensity. Commit to this specific 15-minute routine every evening for one week. As your neuro-muscular pathways adapt and loosen, simply prompt the AI to "incrementally increase the stretch depth" for next week's evolution.
- **Q: How do I proceed if I cannot visualize the pose the AI is describing?**
  - A: You have two rapid solutions: prompt the AI to "Describe the biomechanics of this pose step-by-step as if explaining it to a five-year-old," or quickly drop the English name of the pose into YouTube for a five-second visual reference.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Strategic Vulnerability:** By brutally quantifying your physical constraints (`hands only reach knees`), you strip the AI of its generic, templated assumptions, forcing it to construct a routine anchored in your actual biomechanical reality.
2. **Ironclad Safety Constraints:** The explicit directive banning advanced inversions or deep backbends acts as a vital guardrail, ensuring the AI operates with a "do no harm" philosophy while you practice unmonitored at home.
3. **Professional Sequencing:** Mandating a strict chronological architecture (Centering → Warm-up → Peak Flow → Savasana) elevates the output from a randomized list of disjointed stretches into a cohesive, deeply restorative mind-body experience.

1. **Supported Child's Pose (Balasana) - Modification:** (2 mins)
   - _Breathing:_ Inhale deeply expanding your back ribs, exhale to let your hips melt toward your heels.
   - _Posture Tip:_ If your hips hover far above your heels, that is perfectly fine. Widen your knees to create space for your torso and allow your neck to completely disengage.
2. **Seated Neck & Shoulder Releases:** (2 mins)
   - _Breathing:_ Inhale smoothly as you shrug your shoulders to your ears, exhale completely as you glide them down your spine.
   - _Posture Tip:_ Maintain a tall, neutral spine; never force your neck beyond a gentle, comfortable stretch.
3. **Spinal Articulation (Cat-Cow):** (10 Repetitions)
   - _Breathing:_ Inhale as you lower your belly and broaden your collarbones (Cow), exhale as you dome your upper back toward the ceiling (Cat).
   - _Posture Tip:_ Move with extreme intention, visualizing the articulation of each individual vertebra.
