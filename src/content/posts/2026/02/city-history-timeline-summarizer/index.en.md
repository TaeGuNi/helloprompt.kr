> **Task:**
>

> 3. Synthesize a single, powerful "Core Keyword" that penetrates and summarizes the entire history of this city (e.g., "Coexistence," "Resilience," "Rebirth").
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Format the output precisely as: `- [Year] Event Name : Extremely concise explanation (max 2 sentences) -> Related Modern Landmark/Cultural Trace`.
> - Do not use bullet points inside the explanation. Keep it punchy and highly readable.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Ensure all dates and historical facts are 100% accurate. If the connection between an event and a modern trace is speculative, state that clearly. Avoid hallucinating historical connections.
