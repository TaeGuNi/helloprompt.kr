## üöÄ Solution: "The Anti-Slop Content Refiner"

### ü•â Basic Version

Use this when you need a quick polish to remove obvious AI buzzwords and robotic phrasing.

> **Role:** You are an expert human copywriter.
> **Task:** Rewrite the following `[AI-generated text]` to sound 100% natural and human. Remove all typical AI buzzwords (e.g., "delve," "tapestry," "realm") and write in a conversational, engaging tone.

### ü•á Pro Version

Use this for crucial content where authenticity, emotional resonance, and a unique voice are non-negotiable.

> **Role (Role):** You are a senior editorial director and expert copywriter known for a punchy, authentic, and deeply human voice.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: We have an initial draft `[AI-Generated Draft]` that feels too generic, robotic, and suffers from "AI Slop."
> - Goal: Transform this text into a compelling piece that resonates emotionally with `[Target Audience]`, ensuring it passes as completely human-written.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Completely rewrite the draft to eliminate all AI clich√©s, predictable sentence structures, and soulless corporate jargon.
> 2. Introduce varied sentence lengths (short, punchy sentences mixed with longer, flowing ones) to create a natural rhythm.
> 3. Inject subtle humor, relatable analogies, or a specific tone: `[Desired Tone, e.g., witty, empathetic, authoritative]`.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Do NOT use words like "delve," "testament," "tapestry," "foster," or "seamless."
> - Maintain the original core message and facts, but elevate the storytelling.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - If the original draft contains hallucinations or unsubstantiated claims, flag them before rewriting. Do not invent facts to make the text sound better.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Banning Buzzwords (Constraints):** By explicitly blacklisting words that AI models overuse, we force the AI to search for more creative, natural vocabulary.

3. **Targeted Tone (Context):** Defining the audience and desired emotional resonance ensures the text connects on a human level, transforming a mere information dump into compelling storytelling.
