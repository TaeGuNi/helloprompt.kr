> _"Tired of agonizing over every penny in a complex budgeting app? Just remember three simple numbers."_

Budgeting doesn't have to mean tracking every single coffee purchase. The **50/30/20 Rule** offers a highly effective, low-stress alternative. It simply divides your after-tax income into three buckets: 50% for Needs, 30% for Wants, and 20% for Savings and Debt Repayment. This prompt acts as your personal financial coach, automatically calculating these buckets and tailoring them to your unique living situation.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this work with any currency?**
  - A: Absolutely. Whether you input USD, EUR, KRW, or any other currency, the AI perfectly calculates the percentages. Just make sure to input your _net_ (after-tax) income.

- **Q: What if I have a lot of high-interest debt?**
  - A: You can instruct the AI in the Pro Version's context: "I have $5,000 in credit card debt." The AI will typically advise allocating the entire 20% 'Savings' portion strictly toward aggressive debt repayment until it is cleared.

- **Q: Is the 50/30/20 rule realistic for low-income earners?**
  - A: It can be challenging. If your basic needs exceed 50%, you can ask the AI to generate a modified ratio, such as 60/20/20 or even 70/20/10, prioritizing immediate survival while still building the long-term habit of saving.
