_"Others write cool posts well, but I have nothing to write about..."_

It's not just grand success stories that become posts. Rather, **'small failures and learnings'** gain greater empathy. If you throw a trivial episode you experienced at work today to AI, it cooks it into an 'insight post' that calls for likes.

### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need detailed quality.

Copy the **PROMPT** below.

> **Role (Role):** You are a LinkedIn influencer and content editor.
>
> **Context (Context):** I am going to post on LinkedIn based on my work experience. I want to write a post that smells professional yet human.
>
> **Material (Topic):**
>
> - **Incident:** `[e.g., My opinion was rejected in the meeting today]`
> - **Thought/Learning:** `[e.g., Realized lack of preparation. Thought I should persuade with data]`
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1.  **Hook:** Start the first sentence with a question or a shocking sentence that stops people from scrolling.
> 2.  **Storytelling:** Unravel my experience frankly and plainly. (Attitude of admitting failure)
> 3.  **Insight:** So give a lesson on what I learned and how I will apply it.
> 4.  **Readability:** Divide paragraphs frequently to make it comfortable to view on mobile, and use emojis appropriately.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Avoid trying to teach too much (condescending) or boasting too much.
> - End with a question asking for people's opinions at the end (inducing comments).
