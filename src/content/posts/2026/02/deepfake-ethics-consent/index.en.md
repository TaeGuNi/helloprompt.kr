## 🚀 The Solution: "Deepfake Ethics & Defense Policy Generator"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this to quickly draft the core principles of an ethical policy or a community announcement.

> **Role:** You are an AI Ethics Expert at an IT company.
> **Task:** Draft the "5 Core Principles Prohibiting the Creation and Distribution of Non-Consensual Deepfakes" to protect our users.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you require a comprehensive, practical guideline that encompasses your organization's actual response manual alongside technical and legal defense mechanisms.

> **Role:** You are the AI Ethics Officer and Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: We are currently facing a 'technological gap' where the malicious use of generative AI for non-consensual deepfake pornography and fake news is escalating rapidly.
> - Goal: To establish robust ethical guidelines and a crisis response manual that completely blocks the malicious use of deepfakes within `[Organization/Service Name]` and enables an immediate response if an incident occurs.
>
> **Task:**
>
> Write a comprehensive guideline that must include the following:
>
> 1. Core Principles: State the principles of 'Consent'-based content generation and the absolute protection of human dignity.
> 2. Technical Response: Propose technical control measures such as watermarking, metadata insertion, and the adoption of detection algorithms.
> 3. Sanctions & Legal Action: Establish a zero-tolerance policy for violations (creation and distribution) and outline strict disciplinary and reporting procedures.
> 4. Victim Relief Protocol: Create a 3-step crisis response scenario ranging from report reception to content blinding and connection to legal/psychological support.
> 5. Keep the `[Organization/Service Name]` placeholder in brackets `[ ]` exactly as is, so the user can easily insert their own company name.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Format the output using Markdown headings (`###`) and bullet points (`-`) to ensure a highly readable manual structure.
> - Strictly avoid an academic or theoretical tone. Maintain a firm, authoritative Corporate Policy Tone that the operations team can immediately adopt as an internal regulation.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Align the guidelines with universal human rights and global ethical standards that do not conflict with current laws in specific countries. Do not make definitive statements regarding specific national laws. (Prevent hallucination)

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The key to defending against deepfake crimes lies not within the AI technology itself, but in the **'agile processes of the organization'**. In a technological vacuum, the presence or absence of clear guidelines dictates the ultimate scale of the damage.

This prompt translates vague AI ethics into concrete, **'actionable guidelines'**. Specifically, proactively systematizing the **'Victim Relief Protocol (Report-Delete-Support)'** allows your platform to drastically mitigate risks while building immense trust with your user base. Even without a flawless detection system in place today, simply declaring a zero-tolerance policy serves as a powerful psychological deterrent.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Do guidelines generated by AI hold legal validity?**
  - A: They serve effectively as internal regulations and terms of service. In the event of a dispute, they provide compelling evidence that the organization 'fulfilled its duty of care'. However, we strongly advise having your internal legal counsel review the final draft before official publication.

- **Q: Can't we simply use AI to detect the deepfake videos themselves?**
  - A: Relying solely on Text-Based Large Language Model (LLM) prompts is fundamentally limited for this task. For actual detection, it is far more effective and accurate to adopt dedicated AI detection solutions specialized in analyzing video frequencies and pixels, such as Intel's FakeCatcher or DeepBrain AI.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Assigning Complementary Personas:** By merging the roles of 'Ethics Officer (Value Guardian)' and 'CISO (System Defender)', the prompt forces the output to transcend mere moral declarations, delivering practical and technical defense mechanisms.
2. **Enforcing Zero-Tolerance & Relief Principles (Constraints):** By explicitly mandating a 3-step protocol for victim relief in the Task, the spectrum of ethical responsibility effectively extends beyond mere sanctions to comprehensive post-incident management.
3. **Controlling Tone & Manner:** The constraints to 'avoid academic tone' and 'maintain a firm policy tone' guarantee that the resulting output is instantly ready to be deployed to internal notice boards without further editing.
