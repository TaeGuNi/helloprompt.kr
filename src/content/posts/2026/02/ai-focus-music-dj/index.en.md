## 🚀 The Solution: "AI Focus DJ"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you need a quick playlist without overthinking the details.

> **Role:** You are a focus and productivity music expert.
> **Task:** Recommend a YouTube or Spotify search query for background music that will help me focus on `[your specific task, e.g., coding, writing, studying]`. I need something strictly without lyrics.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this for deep, extended flow states tailored precisely to your current neuro-rhythm.

> **Role:** You are an elite audio-psychologist and productivity DJ. You specialize in curating soundscapes that optimize brainwave states (Alpha, Beta, Gamma) for peak cognitive performance.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Current State: I am feeling `[your current energy level, e.g., sluggish, anxious, hyperactive]`.
> - Task: I need to do `[specific task, e.g., debug complex React code, write a strategic marketing plan]`.
> - Duration: I need to focus intensely for `[duration, e.g., 90 minutes]`.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Analyze the cognitive requirements of my task and my current energy level.
> 2. Recommend 3 specific music genres or soundscape styles (e.g., 40Hz Binaural Beats, Lo-Fi Chillhop, Cinematic Ambient) that will transition my brain into the optimal state for this task.
> 3. Provide exact, copy-pasteable search queries for YouTube and Spotify for each recommendation.
> 4. Suggest an optimal volume level (e.g., 30%, 50%) for the recommended audio.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Absolutely NO tracks with vocals or distracting sudden beat drops.
> - Output the recommendations using a Markdown list, not a table.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not recommend generic pop or highly stimulating EDM unless it explicitly fits the cognitive profile of the task. Keep it strictly focused on productivity enhancement.
