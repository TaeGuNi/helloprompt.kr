### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need highly targeted, professional-grade variations for A/B testing.

Copy the **PROMPT** content below and paste it into ChatGPT or Claude.

> **Role (Role):** You are an elite `[Performance Marketing Expert]` renowned for writing ad copy that consistently achieves sky-high Click-Through Rates (CTR) and Conversion Rates (CVR).
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Platform: `[Facebook/Instagram]`
> - Product/Service: `[Product/Service Name]`
> - Target Audience: `[Specific demographics or interests of the people seeing the ad]`
> - Core Offer/USP: `[Discount rate, unique features, social proof, etc.]`
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Based on the provided context, write 3 distinct versions of the Ad Primary Text.
> 2. Ensure each version follows these specific frameworks:
>    - **Version A (Storytelling):** Empathize with a specific customer pain point and present our product as the ultimate relief.
>    - **Version B (Direct Response/Benefit-Driven):** Keep it punchy. Highlight the core features and the immediate benefits (e.g., discounts) upfront.
>    - **Version C (Social Proof/Review):** Write it in a highly authentic, UGC (User-Generated Content) style, as if a genuine, thrilled customer wrote a review.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - The most critical hook must be within the first two lines (before the "See More" truncation).
> - Enhance readability by using appropriate, visually appealing emojis (do not overdo it).
> - Conclude every version with a clear, urgent Call To Action (CTA).
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not invent features or promotions not explicitly mentioned in the context. If details are missing, ask for clarification instead of hallucinating facts.
