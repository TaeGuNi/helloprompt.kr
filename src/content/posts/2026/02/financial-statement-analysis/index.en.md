Corporate fundamental analysis is the bedrock of sound investing, yet slogging through hundreds of pages of dense financial statements is a nightmare for most. ## ⚡️ 3-Line Summary (TL;DR)

1. **Instant Distillation:** Condenses overwhelming financial data into bite-sized, core performance indicators.
2. **Trend Analysis:** Compares three years of historical data to expose hidden growth trajectories and red flags.
3. **Actionable Output:** Delivers a clear, beginner-friendly investment perspective completely free of Wall Street jargon.

## 🚀 The Solution: "Financial Statement X-Ray"

### 🥉 Basic Version (Quick Scan)

Use this when you need a rapid, high-level summary of a company's financials.

> **Role:** You are a `[Senior Equity Analyst]` with 20 years of experience on Wall Street.
>
> **Request:** I will provide recent financial statement data for `[Company Name]`. Summarize the key takeaways in under a minute, focusing strictly on the metrics that matter most to a retail stock investor.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Deep Dive)

Use this for a comprehensive, professional-grade fundamental analysis. Paste the raw financial statement text or earnings report data along with this prompt.

> **Role:** You are a `[Senior Equity Analyst]` with 20 years of experience on Wall Street.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I need to analyze the fundamental health, profitability, and growth potential of `[Company Name]`.
> - Goal: To extract actionable insights from highly complex financial statements without getting lost in the noise.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Analyze the 3-year historical trends for **Revenue, Operating Profit, and Net Income**.
> 2. Calculate or extract key solvency and profitability metrics: **Debt-to-Equity Ratio, Current Ratio, and ROE (Return on Equity)**.
> 3. Based on the data, rate the **"Fundamental Investment Attractiveness"** as High, Medium, or Low, and provide 3 concrete reasons for your rating.
> 4. Translate all complex accounting jargon into plain English so a beginner investor can easily understand the company's true financial standing.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Format the core metrics in a clear Markdown table.
> - Base all your analysis strictly on the provided objective figures.
> - Do not provide direct financial advice or definitive buy/sell recommendations; offer an objective analytical perspective instead.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - If any critical data points are missing from the provided text, do not hallucinate them. Explicitly state that the data is missing.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

This prompt is an absolute **game-changer** during earnings season. When a company drops its quarterly earnings report, the market reacts in seconds. Instead of manually digging through the SEC EDGAR database or parsing dense PDFs, simply paste the raw 10-Q or 10-K text into an AI model built for large context windows (like **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** or **ChatGPT-4o**) and run the Pro Version prompt.

_Pro Tip:_ For the most accurate results, never just ask the AI to "look up" the data—it may hallucinate or pull outdated numbers. **Always copy and paste** the actual income statement and balance sheet text directly below your prompt. This forces the AI to operate strictly as an analytical engine rather than a search engine, guaranteeing high-fidelity, data-backed insights.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I use the free version of ChatGPT for this?**
  - A: Yes, but we highly recommend leveraging advanced models like **GPT-4o**, **Claude 3.5 Sonnet**, or **Gemini 2.5 Pro**. Financial analysis requires rigorous logic and expansive context windows—areas where free tiers often struggle when processing lengthy documents.

- **Q: Where do I find the financial statement text to paste?**
  - A: You can visit Yahoo Finance, navigate to the **'Financials'** tab of your target stock, and copy the text directly from the Income Statement and Balance Sheet. Alternatively, if your AI model supports file uploads, you can directly attach the official SEC 10-K PDF.

- **Q: Will this prompt tell me exactly which stock to buy?**
  - A: No. This prompt is strictly designed to evaluate **fundamental health**, not to dispense financial advice. It will clearly show whether a company is bleeding cash or growing profitably, but the final investment decision remains entirely yours.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Playing:** By assigning the persona of a "Wall Street Senior Equity Analyst," the AI automatically adopts a highly analytical, precise, and professional tone.
2. **Structured Outputs:** Forcing the AI to format its response using Markdown tables and clear lists prevents the output from devolving into a massive, unreadable wall of text.
3. **Anti-Hallucination Constraints:** The strict directive to analyze _only_ the provided objective figures—and to openly admit when data is missing—drastically reduces the risk of the AI inventing false financial numbers.
