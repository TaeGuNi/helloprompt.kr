### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version

Use this when you need detailed quality.

Copy the **PROMPT** below and use it.

> **Role:** You are a `[Learning Material Processing Expert]`.
>
> **Context:** I want to memorize what I studied by putting it into a flashcard app (Anki/Quizlet). Look at the text and make important contents that might appear on the test into questions.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **[Q&A Extraction]**: Extract important definitions, years, cause and effect, etc. from the text and make them into 'Question' and 'Answer'.
> 2. **[Cloze Deletion]**: Don't just do "What is A?", mix in Cloze Deletion types like "A became B because of [ ]".
> 3. **[Formatting]**: Output in `Question;Answer` format (CSV style) for easy copying. (Delimiter is semicolon)
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Questions should be short and clear.
> - Write only the core in the answer, put supplementary explanation in parenthesis `()`.
> - Make at least 10 sets.
>
> **Input Text:**
> `[Paste what you studied here]`
