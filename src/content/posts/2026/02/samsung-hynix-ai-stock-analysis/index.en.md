## 🚀 The Solution: "Semiconductor Stock Analysis Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this prompt when you just need a rapid, high-level forecast of the core market trends.

> **Role:** You are a `[Senior Semiconductor Analyst]`.
> **Task:** Based on the latest news, summarize the 6-month stock outlook for `[Samsung Electronics]` and `[SK Hynix]`, focusing strictly on the most critical price drivers.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Deploy this advanced prompt when you require deep business diagnostics, valuation comparisons, and actionable investment points.

> **Role:** You are a `[Lead Semiconductor Sector Analyst at a Top Tier Brokerage]` with 20 years of institutional experience.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: `[Current global AI semiconductor demand status and position in the memory cycle]`
> - Goal: `[Write a detailed analysis report that is easy for retail investors to understand]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Comparatively analyze the current valuation metrics (PER, PBR, etc.) of Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix.
> 2. Summarize the fundamental strengths and weaknesses of both companies' future growth catalysts (e.g., HBM, DDR5) in a table.
> 3. Provide 3 highly rational, data-backed reasons whether an investor should Buy, Hold, or Sell `[Target Company, e.g., Samsung Electronics]` at this exact market juncture.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Use a mix of Markdown tables and bullet points to ensure the output is visually clean and highly readable.
> - Break down complex technical jargon using intuitive, everyday analogies so absolute beginners can effortlessly grasp the concepts.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Never hallucinate or fabricate uncertain financial figures like recent earnings releases. If you lack real-time data, explicitly state: "Requires checking latest search."

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: Does this prompt work on the free version of ChatGPT?**
  - A: Yes, it functions perfectly. However, because stock market analysis relies heavily on up-to-the-minute data, we strongly recommend using models equipped with real-time web search capabilities (such as Perplexity or ChatGPT Plus).

- **Q: Can I use this effectively even if I don't understand complex terms like 'HBM' or 'Foundry'?**
  - A: Absolutely! We specifically engineered the constraints to force the AI to "explain technical jargon using everyday analogies." The AI will automatically break down intimidating semiconductor concepts into highly intuitive, easy-to-digest comparisons.

## 🧬 Why it works?

1. **Role Playing:** By assigning the persona of a 'Lead Analyst,' we force the AI to abandon simple knowledge regurgitation and adopt a highly professional, authoritative tone packed with institutional investment insights.
2. **Context Specification:** Explicitly framing the current market constraints—such as global AI semiconductor demand and the exact phase of the memory cycle—compels the AI to generate a nuanced, contextually aware analysis rather than generic advice.
3. **Constraints:** Mandating the simplification of technical terms and strict formatting guarantees a highly readable, mobile-friendly report that cuts straight to the actionable takeaways.
