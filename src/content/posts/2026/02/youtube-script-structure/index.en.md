### ü•á Pro Version (Expert Craftsmanship)

Use this when you need a detailed, production-ready script with exact pacing.

> **Role:** You are an `[Expert YouTube Producer for a 1M+ Subscriber Channel]`.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I am planning to shoot a YouTube video about `[Topic]`.
> - Goal: I need a dynamic, zero-fluff script that hooks the audience immediately and keeps them glued to the screen until the very last second.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **The Hook (0~30 sec):** Write a high-impact opening statement that instantly spikes curiosity, addresses the viewer's pain point, and guarantees a payoff.
> 2. **The Meat (Main Body):** Divide the core message into 3 distinct, escalating sub-topics. Ensure logical flow and use storytelling techniques to maintain high energy.
> 3. **The Outro & CTA:** Conclude with a smooth, non-pushy Call-to-Action (CTA) that naturally drives likes, subscriptions, and binge-watching (pointing to another video).
>
> **Variables:**
>
> - `[Topic]`: How to master English conversation alone at home without spending a dime.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Output the script in a clear Markdown format, separating audio (what to say) and visual cues (B-roll/text overlays).
> - Do not use clich√© transitions like "Without further ado" or "Let's dive right in."
