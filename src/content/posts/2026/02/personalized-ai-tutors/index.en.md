## ğŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can younger children use this safely?**
  - A: Yes, but adult supervision is recommended. You can add a constraint like _"Use child-friendly language and strictly avoid any inappropriate content"_ to ensure a completely safe environment.

- **Q: Does this framework work for language learning?**
  - A: Absolutely! Just change the Role to _"Native ESL Teacher"_ and the Goal to _"Practicing conversational fluency and correcting grammar mistakes."_

- **Q: How do I prevent the AI from just giving the student the answers?**
  - A: The "Socratic method" instruction in the Pro Version specifically prevents this. If the AI still slips up, you can add a hard constraint: _"NEVER provide the final answer until the student has attempted to solve it at least twice."_
