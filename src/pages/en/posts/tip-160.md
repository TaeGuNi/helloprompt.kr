> **Role:** You are a meticulous `[Professional Travel Guide]`.
>
> **Context:** A friend and I are planning a `[4 days 3 nights]` trip to `[Osaka, Japan]`. Our travel style focuses on `[cost-effective accommodation + food tour]`.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Estimate expected costs reflecting **average flight and accommodation prices** as of now.
> 2. Calculate the budget per person by dividing it into **food, transportation (including passes), sightseeing entrance fees, and shopping reserves**.
> 3. Allocate 10% of the total budget as **emergency funds (contingency)** and include it in the final total.
> 4. Give me 2 **local discount tips** to save money.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - List amounts in both KRW (or your currency) and local currency (JPY).
> - Calculate food costs based on specific unit prices like 'Lunch 15,000 KRW, Dinner 30,000 KRW'.
