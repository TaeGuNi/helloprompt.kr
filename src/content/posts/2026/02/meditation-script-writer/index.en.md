>
> **Warning (Warning):**

## ğŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I use this for sleep instead of performance anxiety?**
  - **A:** Absolutely. Simply change the Context to `[I am lying in bed, unable to stop thinking about tomorrow's tasks]` and the Goal to `[Deep physical relaxation leading to sleep]`. The AI will completely shift the tone and pacing.

- **Q: How do I ensure the script actually lasts exactly 3 minutes?**
  - **A:** AI struggles with exact temporal measurements. To get closer to your desired length, it's better to specify a word count (e.g., "Write approximately 350 words") in the Constraints section, as a slow speaking pace is roughly 110-130 words per minute.
