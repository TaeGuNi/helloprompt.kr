<br>

### ü•á Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need detailed, realistic, and highly tailored nutritional quality.

> **Role (Role):** You are a Sports Nutrition Expert with 15 years of experience and a Professional Diet Consultant.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I am an office worker. My profile is: `[Height: 175cm, Weight: 80kg, Target Weight: 70kg]`. My activity level is `[Light exercise twice a week]`, and my allergies or disliked foods are `[Cucumbers, Nuts]`.
> - Goal: To achieve healthy, sustainable weight loss without starving.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Calculate my daily recommended caloric intake based on my estimated Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).
> 2. Create a one-week (7 days) meal plan that perfectly balances macronutrient ratios (Carbohydrates, Protein, Fat).
> 3. Since I am an office worker, please account for regular cafeteria or restaurant meals for lunch (assuming portion control, e.g., eating half a portion). Keep the suggestions realistic and practical for a busy schedule.
> 4. Display the estimated calorie count for every single meal.
> 5. Finally, compile a comprehensive 'Weekly Grocery List' categorized by food type to help me buy all the necessary ingredients efficiently.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Exclude recipes that require complex cooking methods or excessive prep time.
> - Output the final 7-day meal plan strictly in a beautifully formatted Markdown table.
> - Prioritize a sustainable, long-term diet approach rather than extreme calorie restriction or fasting.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not invent nutritional facts. If you cannot accurately estimate the calories for a specific meal, state "Calorie estimate unavailable."

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Do I need a paid version like ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro for this?**
  - A: Not at all! Free models like ChatGPT (GPT-4o-mini) or standard Claude 3 Haiku handle this prompt perfectly. However, more advanced models might provide slightly more creative recipes.

- **Q: What if I am vegan or doing Keto?**
  - A: Simply modify the `[Context]` section! Just add "I am strictly vegan" or "I am following a Ketogenic diet" to the background information, and the AI will completely restructure the macros and ingredients to fit your specific dietary restrictions.

- **Q: Can it create meal plans for muscle gain (bulking) instead of weight loss?**
  - A: Absolutely. Just change your `[Target Weight]` to be higher than your current weight, and adjust the `[Goal]` to "Lean muscle gain (bulking)." The AI will automatically calculate a caloric surplus instead of a deficit.
