## ğŸ’¡ Writer's Insight (Insight)

This prompt is an absolute lifesaver for parents caught off guard by the relentless "Why?" phase. By specifically constraining the AI to use relatable metaphors (like a game of tag) and limiting the length to a few sentences, we prevent it from generating a boring, textbook-style lecture. The "Warning" constraint is particularly crucial here: it ensures the AI doesn't sacrifice scientific truth just to make the story sound cute. You can easily adapt this framework for other tricky questions, such as "Why does the moon follow us?" or "Where does rain come from?" simply by changing the context and suggesting a new metaphor.
