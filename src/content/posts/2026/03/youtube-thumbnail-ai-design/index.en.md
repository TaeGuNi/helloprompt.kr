## 🚀 The Expert-Level Prompt

> **Role:** You are a professional YouTube thumbnail graphic designer.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I need a thumbnail background for a `[tech/cooking/investing/beauty]` YouTube channel
> - Goal: Create a high-contrast thumbnail background that instantly grabs attention in mobile feeds
>
> **Task:**
>
> - Generate a YouTube thumbnail background image
> - Aspect ratio: 16:9 (optimized for 1280×720px)
> - Composition: `[Rule of Thirds / Center-focused]` — reserve negative space on the left or top for text overlay
> - Main object: Place one `[laptop screen / food dish / stock chart / cosmetic product]` center-to-right
> - Colors: `[Neon blue+purple gradient / Warm orange+red / Clean white+mint]`
> - Style: 3D rendering, hyper-realistic, soft lighting
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Do NOT include any text or letters in the image (text will be added separately)
> - Keep the background clean — one focal object + clean background is the rule
> - Do NOT generate human faces (real creator photos will be composited)
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not include specific brand logos or copyrighted characters

## 💡 Insight & How to Use

### Why Negative Space is Everything

Think about the actual use case: users scroll on **mobile**. On that tiny screen, you need to overlay **title text** on the thumbnail. If the background is packed with complex illustrations, text **readability drops to zero**.

Analyzing pro YouTubers' thumbnails reveals that **30-40% of the image is empty space for text**. Telling the AI "negative space on the left for text overlay" naturally creates this clean zone.

### Color Psychology

- **Red + Yellow:** Urgency, "must watch now" → News/trending channels
- **Blue + Purple:** Trust, tech, futuristic → Tech/education channels
- **Green + White:** Natural, healthy, clean → Cooking/lifestyle channels
- **Black + Neon:** Premium, luxury → Finance/investment channels

### The "No Text" Constraint

AI image generators (especially Midjourney, DALL-E) still render text **unreliably** — broken letters, typos are common. Remove text from the image entirely and add it with **Canva or Phonto** using clean fonts. This is the professional workflow.

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: How do I set 16:9 aspect ratio in Midjourney?**
  - A: Add `--ar 16:9` at the end of your prompt. DALL-E 3 takes it as text: "landscape orientation, 16:9 aspect ratio." Ideogram has a ratio selector in the UI.

- **Q: Easiest way to add text over the generated image?**
  - A: Use **Canva** (free), select "YouTube Thumbnail" template, upload the AI background, add bold text. Done in 3 minutes. On mobile, **Phonto** app works great.

- **Q: Can I use these commercially?**
  - A: Midjourney paid subscribers can use images commercially. DALL-E 3 (ChatGPT Plus) also allows commercial use. Always check the latest terms of service.
