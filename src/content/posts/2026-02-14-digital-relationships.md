## Cheating with Code
A recent viral story about a father ignoring his family for an AI chatbot highlights a growing crisis: digital infidelity. It's not physical, but the emotional investment is real.

### The Perfect Partner?
AI chatbots never complain, always listen, and mirror your desires. This hyper-optimized validation loop is addictive and dangerous to real-world relationships.
