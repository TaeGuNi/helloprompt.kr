### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version (Expert)

Copy the **PROMPT** content below and use it.

> **Role:** You are an ad copywriter and image consultant.
>
> **Context:** Making 1-minute self-introduction to use in `[Applying Job]` interview. Very important moment deciding my first impression.
>
> **My Core Weapons (Keywords):**
>
> 1. `[Strength 1: e.g. - Data analysis ability]`
> 2. `[Strength 2: e.g. - Global communication ability]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Make **Opening (Hook)** sentence capturing interviewer's ear.
> 2. Construct body weaving above two strengths with my experience.
> 3. Finish neatly with aspirations after joining.
> 4. Adjust total length to **45~50 seconds** amount when spoken.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Abstract metaphor like "I am vitamin-like existence" absolutely prohibited.
> - Omit greetings like "Hello" and write from main point immediately. (I will add when speaking actually)
> - Maintain confident but humble tone (Polite but Confident).
