## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: How can I block personalized ads (cookies) if I don't want to see them?**
  - A: You can opt out of providing cookies at any time. Visit [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) to disable personalized advertising, or go to [www.aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info) to immediately stop third-party vendors from using cookies.

- **Q: When are my access logs (like my IP address) completely deleted?**
  - A: They are anonymized immediately after statistical analysis (via Google Analytics). The original logs are securely retained for a maximum of 3 months in accordance with relevant laws, after which they are permanently and irreversibly destroyed.

- **Q: I live in Europe or California. How are my data rights protected?**
  - A: Hello Prompt strictly complies with global user regulations. Under the **GDPR (Europe)**, you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time. Under the **CCPA (California)**, we strictly abide by the "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" rule and never sell your data to third parties.
