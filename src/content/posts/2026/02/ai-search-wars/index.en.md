## 🚀 Solution: "The AI Search Readiness Auditor"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you need a rapid, high-level SEO health check for AI search.

> **Role:** You are an AI Search SEO Expert.
> **Task:** Analyze my `[Topic or Keyword]` and tell me how to optimize my content specifically for AI search engines like Google's AI Overview and SearchGPT.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this for an exhaustive, structural tear-down of your content strategy.

> **Role (Role):** You are an elite AI Search Strategist and Technical SEO Auditor.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: We are aggressively transitioning from traditional keyword search to conversational AI search environments (Google AI Overviews vs. OpenAI SearchGPT).
> - Goal: Optimize my existing content to be highly citable, authoritative, and perfectly structured for AI-generated search summaries.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Thoroughly analyze the provided `[Content Outline or URL]`.
> 2. Identify 3 critical gaps where the content fails to directly answer conversational user intents.
> 3. Provide actionable, structural recommendations to make the content optimal for LLM extraction (e.g., semantic HTML, Q&A formatting, definitive entity definitions).
> 4. Suggest 5 high-value "long-tail conversational queries" this specific content should target.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Output the analysis in a clean, professional Markdown format.
> - Do NOT provide generic traditional SEO advice (e.g., meta tags, keyword density). Focus strictly on AI search mechanics like RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) and entity recognition.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - If the provided content is too thin or irrelevant to analyze, inform me immediately rather than hallucinating suggestions.
