Stop writing from scratch. That YouTube script you poured your heart into already contains all your core material. ## 🚀 The Solution: "Content Recycler Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you need to quickly refine a raw video script into a highly readable article.

> **Role:** You are a `[Veteran Editor with 10 Years of Experience]`.
> **Task:** Convert the following `[YouTube Video Script]` into an easy-to-read blog post. Refine the conversational, spoken language into natural written prose, and improve readability by structuring the paragraphs logically.
>
> `[Insert your YouTube video script here]`

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you want to simultaneously capture blog search traffic, trigger visual virality on Instagram, and drive rapid distribution on X—all from a single source file.

> **Role:** You are a trendsetting `[Content Marketing Expert and Tech Copywriter with 10 Years of Experience]`.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I have just uploaded a highly informative YouTube video. I want to use its script to drive distributed traffic across multiple platforms.
> - Goal: Maintain the core message of the original script while creating 3 distinct, derivative content pieces flawlessly optimized for the specific characteristics of each platform.
>
> **Source:**
>
> - Original Script: `[Paste the entire YouTube script here]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **SEO-Optimized Blog Post:** Structure it as an informative article using <H2> and <H3> tags for high search engine ranking. Actively use bullet points to maximize readability.
> 2. **Instagram Carousel Text:** Extract 5 key insights and summarize them into short, impactful phrases suitable for 5 slide images. The first slide must feature a powerful, attention-grabbing hook.
> 3. **Twitter (X) Viral Thread:** Start with an intriguing, dopamine-inducing opening sentence, followed by a thread of 5 to 7 short, punchy sentences (under 140 characters each).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Never invent facts that are not present in the original text (strictly no hallucinations).
> - Present the output clearly separated into 3 sections (Blog, Instagram, Twitter) using Markdown format.
> - Include appropriate emojis tailored to the specific vibe of each platform.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The true power of this prompt lies in **strict formatting control**. Video scripts are inherently conversational and long-winded, prioritizing verbal flow to keep viewers engaged. However, audiences on text-first platforms demand scannable, high-density information.

If you simply tell the AI to "turn this into an article," you'll end up with a tedious, unreadable wall of text. You must enforce **precise structural constraints**—mandating <H2> and <H3> tags, bullet-point summaries, and platform-specific layouts like Instagram carousels. Since integrating this exact pipeline into my own workflow, I can schedule an entire week's worth of social media content from a single video concept in just 10 minutes. This is, without a doubt, the highest-leverage way to convert your time into tangible value.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: What is the easiest way to extract a YouTube script or subtitles?**
  - A: I highly recommend the "YouTube Summary with ChatGPT & Claude" Chrome extension. It allows you to cleanly copy the entire transcript—without timestamps—in a single click. If it's your own video, downloading the .txt file directly from the "Subtitles" tab in YouTube Studio is also a flawless method.

- **Q: Won't posting the same content across multiple platforms be penalized as duplicate content?**
  - A: Not at all. YouTube (Google), Instagram (Meta), and X (Twitter) are isolated ecosystems driven by entirely different algorithms and user search intents. Because you are tailoring the format and tone to match native platform expectations, this acts as a brilliant cross-media strategy, not spam. If you are worried about SEO conflicts, giving your blog post a slightly different title than the video is more than enough.

- **Q: My script is too long, and the AI cuts off or throws an error halfway through. What should I do?**
  - A: While models like ChatGPT-4o and Claude 3.5 Sonnet boast massive context windows, it is safer to feed scripts for videos over 30 minutes in chunks. Prompting the AI with "Summarize Part 1 first," and then following up with "Now summarize Part 2," ensures zero loss of critical detail.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Persona-Based Prompting (Role):** By assigning the AI the identity of a seasoned "Content Marketing Expert" instead of a generic summarization bot, we force it to apply core copywriting principles—like engaging hooks, viral structures, and optimal readability—to the final output.
2. **Multi-Dimensional Formatting (Task):** Instead of vaguely asking to "repurpose this," we outline strict, platform-specific deliverables (an SEO-driven blog post, a swipeable Instagram carousel, a punchy X thread) to guarantee assets that are instantly ready to publish.
3. **Safety Through Constraints (Constraints):** Explicitly commanding the AI to "never invent facts" effectively neutralizes its tendency to hallucinate. Bulletproof reliability is the lifeblood of successful content marketing.
