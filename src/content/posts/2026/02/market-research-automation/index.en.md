- **⏱️ Time Saved:** 5 hours → 10 minutes (96% reduction)

> *"A competitor’s 1-star review is the most perfect product brief for your next launch."*

The fatal flaws in your competitor’s products are the clearest breakthroughs for your brand to dominate the market. However, copy-pasting thousands of reviews from Amazon, Shopify, or App Stores into a spreadsheet and reading them one by one is an agonizing waste of human resources. Stop this tedious, soul-crushing manual labor and hand it over to AI. All you need to do is harvest the **'razor-sharp business insights'** refined from the raw data.

## ⚡️ 3-Line Summary (TL;DR)

1. **Data Collection:** Secure customer review data from competitors using web scraping tools or simple copy-pasting.
2. **AI Categorization:** Use targeted prompts to automatically categorize thousands of reviews into 'Pain Points' and 'Wow Factors'.
3. **Insight Extraction:** Formulate a powerful Unique Selling Proposition (USP) for your product based on the discovered 'Unmet Needs'.

## 🚀 The Solution: "Review Mine Excavator" Prompt

### 🥉 Basic Version

Perfect for when you need a quick pulse check on a small batch of about 30 reviews to get an intuitive feel of the market sentiment.

> **Role:** You are a `[Senior Marketer with 10 years of experience]`.
> **Task:** Analyze the provided review data and summarize the following two points:
>
> 1. Top 3 features customers are most enthusiastic about.
> 2. Top 3 fatal flaws that frustrate customers the most.
> 
> **Review Data:** `[Paste your copied review text here]`


### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when conducting an in-depth analysis of hundreds or thousands of reviews to establish a robust, data-driven foundation for new product development.

> **Role:** You are a `[Data Analyst and Lead Product Manager (PM) with 20 years of experience]`.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I am planning to launch a highly differentiated new product that exploits market gaps by analyzing the cumulative review data of `[Insert Competitor and Product Name]`.
> - Goal: To accurately identify the 'Unmet Needs' felt by customers in the existing market and convert them into actionable business opportunities.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Perform sentiment analysis (Positive/Negative/Neutral) based on the `[Review Data]` below and extract key keywords.
> 2. Group the most frequently recurring 'Pain Points' from negative reviews into 3 distinct categories.
> 3. Propose a 'Unique Selling Proposition (USP)' that we must highlight to perfectly resolve each analyzed Pain Point.
> 4. Quote actual sentences from customer reviews to increase the credibility of each analysis result.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - The output must be neatly organized in a Markdown table format. (Columns: Category | Key Pain Point | Frequency | Actual Quote | Business Insight / USP)
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Never fabricate information not present in the data. If evidence is lacking or ambiguous, explicitly state "Insufficient Data." (Prevent hallucination)
>
> **[Review Data]**
> `[Paste the review text here, or if uploading a file, type "Refer to the attached Excel file"]`

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The true value of this prompt explodes when combined with **Raw Excel Data**. Use a Chrome extension (e.g., Web Scraper, Listly) to download reviews from Amazon or any e-commerce platform as a `.csv` or Excel file. 

Next, upload that file to ChatGPT using 'Advanced Data Analysis' or Claude 3.5 Sonnet, and run the **Pro Version prompt** above. You will witness the magic of transforming a massive qualitative data analysis—which normally takes days of all-nighters—into a high-quality product brief in just 10 minutes. AI heavily outperforms humans, especially when catching recurring nuanced phrases hidden inside negative feedback (e.g., "Shipping was fast, but the stitching falls apart easily").

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: What is the maximum number of Excel files (or file size) I can upload?**
  - A: For ChatGPT Plus (GPT-4o), you can upload up to 10 files at once (each up to 512MB). Claude Pro also supports similar large-scale data processing, making it fully capable of digesting tens of thousands of rows of review data effortlessly.

- **Q: Can I use this to analyze App Store or Google Play reviews instead of e-commerce?**
  - A: Absolutely! Just tweak the background in the `[Context]` section to "App Store review data of a competitor's mobile app," and you will receive a flawless bug-tracking and feature-improvement report tailored for IT product enhancements.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Specific Persona and Context Assignment:** By setting the roleplay to a "Lead PM with 20 years of experience" and establishing a clear objective of "new product planning," the tone and depth of the output are elevated to an expert level.
2. **Mandatory Real Quotes (Grounding):** To prevent AI's chronic issue of hallucination, the prompt forces the AI to verbatim quote actual customer wording in the results. This drastically boosts the persuasive power and reliability of the analysis report.
3. **Actionable Output:** Instead of merely summarizing complaints, the prompt is engineered to derive "Our USP" to solve those exact issues, instantly bridging the gap between raw data analysis and practical business planning.
