## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this work equally well on Midjourney and DALL-E 3?**
  - A: Yes, but the results vary slightly. Midjourney tends to handle precise millimeter measurements (like `14mm lens`) better for cinematic realism, whereas DALL-E 3 responds exceptionally well to plain English descriptors like `extreme wide-angle` or `GoPro perspective`.

- **Q: My fisheye images look too distorted and unrecognizable. How do I fix this?**
  - A: If the fisheye effect is too strong, remove the `fisheye distortion` keyword and stick to `ultra-wide shot` or `16mm lens`. You can also dial back the stylization parameter in Midjourney (`--s`) to keep the structural integrity intact.

- **Q: Can I use this for character portraits?**
  - A: Absolutely! Using a wide-angle lens for close-up portraits creates a fun, stylized, hip-hop music video vibe (think 90s fisheye rap videos). Just add `extreme close-up wide-angle portrait`.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Specific Focal Lengths (`14mm lens`):** Providing exact millimeter measurements overrides the AI's default lens choice, forcing an extreme field of view.
2. **Perspective Keywords (`panoramic view`, `fisheye distortion`):** These dictate the geometric warping of the image, stretching the edges to emphasize scale and depth.
3. **Immersive Context (`immersive perspective`):** This tells the AI to place the "camera" in a position that makes the viewer feel like they are inside the scene, rather than just observing it from a distance.
