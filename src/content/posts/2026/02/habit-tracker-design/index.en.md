>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I set a goal to `[study English every day]` but keep failing. When I come home after work, `[I feel exhausted and end up just watching YouTube]`.
> - Goal: To build a sustainable, daily routine without relying on sheer willpower.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Break down my goal into ridiculously small actions so it's impossible to fail (Micro Habits). (e.g., "Just open the textbook.")
> 2. Create a 'Habit Stacking' formula, anchoring the new habit to an existing daily routine. (e.g., "Right after I finish dinner, I will...")
> 3. Generate a 'Weekly O/X Tracker' table so I can easily visually check off my daily practice.
> 4. Establish an 'Emergency Recovery Rule' to help me get back on track without self-blame if I miss a day.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Do not suggest unrealistic starting plans, like studying for 1 hour immediately. The starting point must be exactly 5 minutes a day.
> - Output the tracker in a Markdown table format.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not invent arbitrary psychological terms. If a concept isn't grounded in established behavioral science, do not use it.
