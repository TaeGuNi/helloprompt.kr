## üöÄ Solution: "Auto-Scheduler Prompt"

### ü•â Basic Version (Simple List Sort)

Using Notion AI feature (`Space` key).

> **Brain Dump (Input):**
>
> - Write report
> - Call Mom
> - Buy toothpaste
> - Dev team meeting prep
> - 3pm Dentist
>
> **Prompt:**
> "Classify above into **[Urgent/Important]**, **[Chores]**, **[Work]**, and rewrite as a Checkbox List in execution order."

<br>

### ü•á Pro Version (Eisenhower Matrix & Timetable)

Using ChatGPT to make a perfect daily plan.

- **Role:** You are the best Time Management Coach.
  > **Input (My Tasks):**
  > `[List random tasks here]`
  >
  > **Task:**
  >
  > 1. Classify into **Eisenhower Matrix** (4 Quadrants).
  >    - Do Now (Important/Urgent)
  >    - Schedule (Important/Not Urgent)
  >    - Delegate (Urgent/Not Important)
  >    - Delete (Not Important/Not Urgent)
- 2. Based on this, create a **Timeline for Today**. (Include Lunch 12-1pm)
     > 3. Add estimated time and a **'Focus Tip'** for each task.

## üß¨ Anatomy of Prompt (Why it works?)

1.  **Eisenhower Matrix:** Let AI judge the weight of tasks. We often mistake 'Buy toothpaste' as equal to 'Write report'.
2.  **Timeline Visualization:** A list is not enough. You need to stamp "14:00~15:00 Write Report" to move your body.
