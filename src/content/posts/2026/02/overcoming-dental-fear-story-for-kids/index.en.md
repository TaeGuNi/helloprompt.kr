## ğŸ’¡ Writer's Insight

This prompt leverages a well-known psychological technique called "cognitive reframing." Children often fear the unknown and the loud noises associated with dental tools. By pre-emptively giving those sounds a fun, narrative purpose (like a laser zapping bugs), you strip away the terror. I highly recommend reading the generated story together the night before, and even mimicking the "whirring" sounds playfully. When the actual dentist turns on the drill, your child will associate the sound with the story's "super cleaner" rather than pain.
