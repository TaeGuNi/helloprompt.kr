## 🚀 Solution: "Partnership Proposal Email Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Perfect for firing off a quick question, reporting a bug, or sharing spontaneous feedback.

> **Recipient:** support@helloprompt.kr
> **Content:** Briefly describe any errors encountered, ideas for improvement, or general questions you might have.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Feed this directly to your AI of choice when you need a polished, high-impact business proposal that demands to be taken seriously by decision-makers.

> **Role:** You are a `[Senior Business Development Manager]` with elite expertise in B2B partnerships and executive-level communication.
>
> **Context:**
> - Recipient: Hello Prompt Team (support@helloprompt.kr)
> - Sender: `[Your Company Name and Your Exact Title]`
> - Objective: Pitch a `[Detailed description of the proposed collaboration or ad campaign]`
>
> **Task:**
> 1. Generate 3 concise, compelling email subject lines designed to maximize open rates.
> 2. Draft the email body, strictly structuring it with a professional greeting, the context of the proposal, core collaboration points, mutual benefits, and a clear Call to Action (CTA) for next steps.
> 3. Wrap any specific details I need to fill in later in `[Variable]` brackets for seamless editing.
>
> **Constraints:**
> - Keep the tone exceedingly polite, crisp, and completely fluff-free. Get straight to the value proposition.
> - Restrict the entire email to 3-4 short paragraphs to ensure perfect readability on mobile devices.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Assignment:** Forcing the AI to adopt the persona of a 'Senior Business Development Manager' instantly strips away amateurish phrasing, locking in a highly persuasive, corporate-ready tone.
2. **Structured Task:** By strictly defining the required architectural elements of the pitch (context, collaboration points, mutual benefits, and CTA), the AI is cornered into producing a perfectly systematic message with zero missing variables.
