_"I read it literally, but native speakers can't understand."_

English is a language of 'Stress' and 'Rhythm'. If you read honestly as spelled, you sound like a robot. Just like "Water" is not understood if said "Water" (Korean pronunciation style) but understood if said "Wa-ter". This prompt visualizes the invisible rules of sound into text, helping you practice pronunciation even without sound.
