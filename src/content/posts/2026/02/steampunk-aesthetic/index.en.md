## 💡 Author's Commentary (Insight & How to Use)

The most critical issue I discovered while running hundreds of tests to generate Steampunk images is the AI's **Temporal Misinterpretation**. Simply because Steampunk is classified as a subgenre of SF (Science Fiction), image generation models like Midjourney or DALL-E often make the fatal error of randomly mixing in elements unique to Cyberpunk. Visual disasters, like flashing LED neon signs, smooth white plastic panels, and cold, sharp chrome suddenly appearing in a Victorian-era workshop, are the result.

To block these phenomena at the source and maintain the perfect analog mood we want, we must exercise strong control starting from the prompt engineering stage. First is the **active use of Negative Prompts**. If you are using Midjourney, you must strictly prevent futuristic elements from invading the canvas by adding `--no neon, plastic, futuristic, chrome, led` to the end of your prompt. For DALL-E 3, it is essential to include explicit constraints in the instructions themselves, such as "Avoid any futuristic, cyberpunk, neon, or plastic elements."

Second is the **magic of Texture Keywords that weave a dense context**. Don't just give vague instructions like 'machine' or 'metal'. Instead, you must combine adjectives that specify the physical state of the material and the marks of time, such as `oxidized brass`, `polished copper`, `dark mahogany wood`, and `worn leather`. This serves to constantly remind the AI that "I currently exist in an old workshop in 19th-century London."

Third is the **delicate orchestration of Steam and Lighting**, the crowning touch of Steampunk. If you just write 'steam' or 'smoke' to capture the feel of a steam engine, it can result in the entire screen becoming dark and murky, like a fire scene. To create an atmosphere that elegantly envelops the subject, you must specify the density and origin of the smoke, such as `soft white smoke` or `faint steam emitting from exhaust pipes`. Combining this with `Cinematic lighting` and `volumetric rays shining through a dirty window` creates a dramatic scene where dust particles floating in the air tangle with steam to scatter light. The lighting temperature must be set to `deep sepia and amber color grading` to coat the entire screen with the deep nostalgia felt in 19th-century black-and-white photos or old parchment.

In the end, a great Steampunk prompt is not a combination of words scattered like pillars, but a single massive 'clockwork mechanism' where the four axes of **Subject-Texture-Background-Lighting** are perfectly interlocked and spinning. The prompt provided above is the result of combining these four axes in the most ideal ratio. Now, freely replace the `[variables]` area according to your imagination and complete your own unique parallel universe where the gears keep turning.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I use this prompt exactly as is in DALL-E 3 besides Midjourney?**
  - A: Yes, you can get excellent results. However, DALL-E 3 cannot directly recognize Midjourney-specific parameters like `--ar 16:9`. After deleting that part, if you add a natural language instruction like "Draw it in a realistic photo style with a landscape aspect ratio (16:9)" to the end of the prompt, it will be perfectly compatible.

- **Q: Are there any tips for increasing quality when describing people or characters in a Steampunk style?**
  - A: In character descriptions, costume and accessory keywords determine the overall quality. Try actively adding keywords where historical details are vividly alive, such as `Victorian corset`, `long trench coat`, `top hat with gears`, and `leather aviator cap`.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Hyper-realistic Specification of Texture (Material Specificity):** Instead of vaguely expressing 'mechanical parts', it specifies the physical state of the material, such as `oxidized brass` or `polished copper`. This guides the AI to render much more realistic and three-dimensional textures.
2. **Perfect Contextualization of Background (Contextual Background):** Instead of leaving the subject floating in mid-air, it presents a specific mise-en-scène like a `dusty Victorian workshop`, firmly anchoring the tone and manner (vibe) of the entire image to the 19th century.
3. **Equipped with High-end Rendering Keywords:** The combination of powerful keywords like `volumetric rays`, `hyper-realistic`, and `unreal engine 5 render` acts as a reliable quality controller, instructing the AI to "implement it like top-tier quality 3D rendering or macro photography."
