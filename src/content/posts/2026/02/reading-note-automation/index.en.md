> _"Have you ever closed a book in exhaustion after trying to manually transcribe underlined sentences? Stop typing and start snapping."_

When you find an inspiring passage or a diagram you want to use immediately, **just pull your smartphone out of your pocket and click the shutter.** These digitized texts are not only easy to archive and search, but they also become **dynamic source data** that you can rearrange and process whenever you need.

The surprise doesn't end there. Beyond simple text extraction and mechanical summarization, AI can forge this knowledge into your own personal weapon. It can instantly derive a sharp, practical **action plan** for **"how exactly you can use this knowledge in your work tomorrow morning."** AI acts as more than just a secretary summarizing an author's thoughts; it behaves like a high-level business coach with sharp insights, pointing out specific tasks tailored to your current role and asking deep questions that expand your thinking. This is the definitive **'Reading Note Automation'** secret that transforms dead text trapped on a shelf into powerful, living insights for your career. Use this guide to evolve your passive reading into an explosive act of knowledge production. The books on your shelf will no longer be mere decorations for intellectual vanity, but **the sharpest and most reliable weapons** leading your career growth.

## 🚀 How Real Experts Write

### 🥉 Basic Version (OCR & Simple Summary)

Useful when you simply want to archive highlighted text digitally and quickly grasp the author's core message.

> **(Upload photo of book page)**
>
> **Request:**
> Please accurately extract the text from the attached photo. Then, summarize the author's core argument on this page in exactly one sentence.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Deep Review & Action Plan)

Use this prompt to fully digest book content and build high-level reading notes that can be directly applied to your work.

> **Role:** You are a 'Literary Critic' with sharp insights and a pragmatic 'Business Coach.'
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I am reading this book for my career growth. The attached photo is from the chapter I found most insightful.
> - Goal: To transform this content into 'actionable knowledge' that can be directly applied to my life and work, rather than just a simple plot summary.
>
> **Task:**
>
> Based on the content of the attached photo, please write the following 4 items in Markdown format.
>
> 1. **Extract:** Extract the 3 most dense and important sentences from the text exactly as they are.
> 2. **Interpretation:** Explain from your perspective why these sentences are important and what the author's true intention and the meaning between the lines are.
> 3. **Application:** Suggest 3 specific action items that I can apply this insight to in my current professional life (or personal project) starting tomorrow.
> 4. **Discussion:** Ask exactly 1 philosophical or strategic question that pierces the essence of the topic so I can think more deeply about it.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Write all output naturally in English.
> - Start directly with the main points without a lengthy introduction.
> - Correct any typos in the extracted text by considering the context.

## 💡 Author's Comments (Insight & How to use)

The true power of this prompt lies not in simple summarization or collecting pretty quotes, but in the core of **'Application'**, which is strictly work-oriented, and the **'Discussion'**, which fosters the power of independent thought. We often fall into the grand illusion that the moment we finish a book and close the last page, all that knowledge has become ours. However, reading with our eyes and simply closing the book is merely a passive 'consumption' of information. Passively swallowing knowledge provided by the author rarely leads to fundamental change. But the moment you grapple with a sharp, essential question posed by the AI and execute even one of the specific action plans tailored to your job, your "dead" reading evolves into a living act of knowledge 'production.'

To get 200% out of this prompt, pay attention to 'Constraint Control.' Try fine-tuning the `[Role]` variable to your actual job. For example, instead of just 'Business Coach,' change it to **"a 10-year B2B SaaS Product Manager"** or **"a data-driven Performance Marketer."** The AI will then derive much sharper and more specific action items based on the realistic context that professionals in those fields face. Similarly, the more specifically you state the `[Context]`, such as "currently planning a new marketing campaign" or "needing a leadership breakthrough due to low team motivation," the practical value of the output will increase exponentially. Never forget that the quality of the AI's insight is directly proportional to the specificity of the context you provide.

Furthermore, don't let these high-quality results be a one-time thing. You must build a system to consistently archive them in your own knowledge base using **'Second Brain'** tools like **Obsidian**, **Notion**, or Roam Research. Connect the key sentences, deep interpretations, and action plans from each book using backlinks or tags. Fragmented knowledge that starts as scattered dots will eventually connect like an organic spiderweb, forming a massive network of insight. Later, when you need to write an important proposal or a blog post requiring inspiration, this accumulated repository will be a far more powerful weapon than any external search engine. You won't be borrowing others' thoughts; you'll be drawing from 'your true knowledge' infused with your own experience and deep reflection. This is the compound magic of reading note automation.

I highly recommend setting aside a conversational session with an AI every Sunday evening, gathering all the book photos you took during the week. In just one hour of focused session, you can experience the magic of transforming a week's worth of fragmented reading into perfectly refined professional knowledge. Most importantly, immediately assign the derived action plans to your calendar or task management tools (Jira, Asana, etc.). No matter how great the insight you gained through a prompt, if it isn't registered as a specific task on your calendar, it will eventually turn into another form of text garbage. Remember that the ultimate completion of reading note automation is not the execution of the prompt, but the execution of the 'To-Do List' the prompt generates. Additionally, if you feel the output hasn't reached the desired depth, don't hesitate to ask follow-up questions through further prompting. Expand your thinking infinitely through continuous feedback.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: What if I'm reading an eBook like Kindle or Apple Books?**
  - A: Actively use the built-in 'Export Highlights' feature. Copying and pasting the extracted text into the prompt window instead of a photo will give you even cleaner results with 100% recognition accuracy, completely removing the hassle of taking photos.

- **Q: Can't I just summarize the whole book at once?**
  - A: For well-known bestsellers like *Sapiens* or *Guns, Germs, and Steel*, the AI models have already deeply learned the vast text data. You can get excellent answers just by asking, "Summarize 3 core frameworks of the book [Title] for my work." However, for newly published books or specific sections where you need your own detailed insights, using this prompt is overwhelmingly advantageous.

- **Q: Does it recognize photos with highlighters or underlines?**
  - A: Yes, the latest Vision models are surprisingly accurate at extracting text even under difficult conditions, such as underlines, highlighter marks, slightly curved pages, or shadows, by inferring the context themselves.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Dual Persona (Role):** By equipping two opposing lenses—'Literary Critic (Depth)' and 'Business Coach (Practicality)'—simultaneously, the AI is forced to derive results that are extremely practical rather than vague impressions.
2. **Action-Oriented Output (Task - Application):** Knowledge is highly volatile. By persistently asking the AI "How will I apply this?", abstract concepts are perfectly converted into a concrete, executable 'To-Do List.'
3. **Internalization of Knowledge (Task - Discussion):** Beyond just accepting information, it transforms the AI into a 'sharp panelist in a book club.' The process of answering the AI's piercing questions yourself helps deep-learn the knowledge into your brain.
