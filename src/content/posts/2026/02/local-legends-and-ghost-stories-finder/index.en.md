## 🚀 How Real Experts Write It

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you want to quickly extract the core ghost story and related location information of a destination without complex settings.

> **Role:** You are a 'Mystery Researcher' who collects strange tales and ghost stories from around the world.
>
> **Task:** Tell me a spine-chilling legend or ghost story related to `[City or Region Name]` in a vivid way. Also, provide the actual physical location related to it.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you need a perfect story with detail and dramatic immersion that overwhelms your audience. Copy the prompt below and fill in the `[Variables]` in brackets to fit your destination.

> **Role:** You are an 'Occult Mystery Researcher' and a captivating storyteller who collects strange tales, urban legends, and folktales from around the world.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Destination: `[City or Specific Landmark, e.g., Charles Bridge, Prague, Czech Republic]`
> - Goal: I am looking for a chilling and mysterious story that is whispered only among locals, rather than ordinary and boring historical facts.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Tell one of the most spine-chilling or interesting legends/ghost stories related to this area using vivid storytelling.
> 2. Provide the exact location and "viewing points" of an 'actually visitable place' related to this story (e.g., a specific statue, back alley, grave, old pub, etc.).
> 3. Be sure to include any superstitions or taboos locals observe when visiting that place (e.g., "Never look back at 12 midnight").
> 4. Display the `[Variable]` parts in brackets so the user can fill them in.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Style: Write in a highly immersive, conversational tone, as if whispering around a campfire.
> - Intensity: Avoid overly gruesome or unpleasant descriptions, but maintain a mysterious atmosphere that makes the listener's skin crawl.
> - Historical Accuracy: Naturally weave in a brief historical background that serves as the origin of the story (e.g., the 17th-century plague, etc.).
> - For mobile readability, do not use tables; organize in a highly readable bulleted list format.
> - **Bold** important keywords.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not invent fake ghost stories from scratch. If information is uncertain, do not make it up; instead, say "I don't know." Base the content strictly on actual folktales or urban legends that have been passed down in that region for a long time. (Hallucination Prevention)

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: What if the AI convincingly makes up a story that doesn't exist (hallucination)?**
  - A: To fundamentally block this hallucination phenomenon, I have included a strong constraint in the warning section: "Base the content on actual folktales." Nevertheless, if you doubt the truth of the results, I strongly recommend cross-checking the key terms of the generated ghost story (e.g., "Prague Golem Legend") with a quick Google search.

- **Q: Can it find legends for small towns with low recognition or remote rural villages?**
  - A: The quality of the results is overwhelmingly higher for famous tourist destinations where vast amounts of data have been accumulated online. If you choose a very small town or remote area, there is a risk the AI might pull a famous legend from a nearby large city and adapt it. In such cases, it’s best to narrow down the AI's search range by providing more detail in the Context section, such as the `[Country and State/Province Name]`.
