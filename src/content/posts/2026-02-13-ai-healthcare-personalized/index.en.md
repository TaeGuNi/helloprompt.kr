Imagine going to the hospital for a cold medicine, and the doctor checks your genetic information to prescribe a drug perfectly tailored to you. This is the future of medicine that AI technology is realizing as of 2026.

## The Rise of Precision Medicine

Precision Medicine is an approach that comprehensively analyzes an individual's genomic information, environment, and lifestyle to prevent and treat diseases. AI acts as the core engine analyzing this vast amount of data.

### The Role of AI

- **Genomic Analysis**: Analyzes billions of sequence data points to identify disease-causing genes.
- **Drug Discovery**: Predicts clinical trial success rates and rapidly identifies candidate substances, drastically shortening drug development timelines.
- **Early Diagnosis**: Precisely analyzes medical imaging data to detect serious diseases like cancer at an early stage.

## Wearables Meet AI

Wearable devices like smartwatches and rings collect our vital signs 24/7. AI monitors this data in real-time, sending immediate alerts if anomalies are detected, and even booking hospital appointments.

## Remaining Challenges

Of course, challenges such as data privacy issues and legal liability for AI diagnoses remain to be solved. However, it is clear that AI is improving the quality of medical services and increasing accessibility.
