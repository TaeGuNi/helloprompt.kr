## üí° Writer's Insight (Insight)

The true value of this prompt lies not just in generating the "twist," but in the "foreshadowing" extraction. A twist without a proper setup is just lazy writing; a twist with perfect setup is pure genius. By explicitly demanding foreshadowing elements, the AI helps you reverse-engineer your narrative. I highly recommend running this prompt _before_ you finalize your second draft. It allows you to seamlessly weave the necessary clues into the early chapters, making the final reveal feel both inevitable and completely surprising.
