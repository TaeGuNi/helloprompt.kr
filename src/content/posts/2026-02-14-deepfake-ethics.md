## Prompting Non-Consent
From deepfakes of celebrities to "softcore" images of real acquaintances, Generative AI has a consent problem. It's not just about copyright; it's about bodily autonomy in a digital space.

### The Legal Lag
Laws are struggling to catch up. Currently, generating non-consensual imagery often falls into legal gray areas, but the moral verdict is clear: it's a violation.
