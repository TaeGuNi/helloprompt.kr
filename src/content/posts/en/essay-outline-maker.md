### ü•á Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need detailed quality.

Copy the **PROMPT** content below and use it.

> **Role:** You are a `[Bestselling Author and Essay Professional Coach]`.
>
> **Context:** I have to write an essay/report on `[Topic]`. My argument is `[Core Argument]`, but I don't know how to develop the writing to be logical and persuasive.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **[Outline Creation]**: Catch standard outline of Introduction-Body (3 stages)-Conclusion.
> 2. **[Guide per Paragraph]**: Give concrete keywords on what content to write and what examples to use for each paragraph.
> 3. **[Hook & Closing]**: Recommend first sentence of introduction (Hook to catch reader's eye) and last sentence of conclusion (Comment leaving lingering imagery).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Logical Flow must be natural.
> - Include one Counter-argument against opposing opinion in body.
>
> **Topic:** `[e.g.: Will AI replace human jobs?]`
> **My Thought:** `[e.g.: Will replace but new opportunities will also arise.]`

## üìä Proof: Before & After

### ‚ùå Before (Stream of Consciousness)

AI is dangerous. But convenient. Will jobs decrease? Maybe not. Anyway must use AI well. (Incoherent)

### ‚úÖ After (Prompt Result)

**[Outline]**

1. **Introduction**: Start with story of people breaking weaving machines during Industrial Revolution (Luddite Movement) -> Connect to current AI fear.
2. **Body 1 (Crisis)**: Extinction of simple repetitive tasks is inevitable (Data presentation).
3. **Body 2 (Rebuttal)**: But emotion, creativity, ethical judgment areas are irreplaceable.
4. **Body 3 (Opportunity)**: Birth of new job group 'AI Utilizer' using AI as tool.
5. **Conclusion**: Don't compete with AI but command AI.

**[Hook]**
"10 years later, will your boss be AI, or a person handling AI?"

**[Closing]**
"To those who fear change it becomes disaster, but to those who ride it, it will become wings."
