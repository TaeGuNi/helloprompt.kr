> 2. **8-Pixel Spatial Rhythm:** All margins/padding, widths, and heights must be strictly composed of multiples of 4px or 8px. Magic numbers (`15px`, `22px`) are punishable by death.
> 3. **Typography Modular Scale:** No eyeballing font sizes. Follow a system where line-height and letter-spacing are bound together as a 1:1 set.

> 11. **Dark Mode Encapsulation & Multilingual Expansion Tolerance:** Do not spam `dark:bg-gray-800` directly on components. Text containers must follow Intrinsic Sizing and embed `flex-wrap` so the layout doesn't break even in multilingual environments.
