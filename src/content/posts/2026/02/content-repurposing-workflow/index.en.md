# 📝 The Ultimate OSMU Prompt: Turn One Blog Post into Instagram & LinkedIn Gold

- **🎯 Target Audience:** Solopreneurs, creators, and marketers managing all channels alone
- **⏱️ Time Saved:** 2 hours → 5 minutes
- **🤖 Recommended Model:** GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet

- ⭐ **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆
- ⚡️ **Effectiveness:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- 🚀 **Utility:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

> _"Why spend hours pouring your soul into a blog post, only to use it once and let it gather dust?"_

It takes an average of 3-4 hours to write a high-quality blog post. Leaving that carefully crafted piece exclusively on your blog is a massive waste of potential. A well-written article is a goldmine—it can instantly become a compelling YouTube Shorts script, an engaging Instagram carousel, or a thought-provoking LinkedIn professional insight.

If you've been painfully rewriting and formatting your content from scratch for every single platform, it's time to stop. Build a flawless **'One-Source Multi-Use' (OSMU)** system with AI and explode your content reach by over 500%.

---

## ⚡️ TL;DR

1. **Craft the Masterpiece:** Write your core content first (blog post, article, or newsletter).
2. **Platform-Specific Transformation:** Command AI to adapt the content according to the unique 'grammar' and 'algorithmic traits' of each platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, X, etc.).
3. **Automate Visual Planning:** Extract AI image generation prompts for carousels or video assets simultaneously, reducing your planning time to absolute zero.

---

## 🚀 The Solution: "Content Transformer Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you need rapid-fire text to copy and paste across multiple social channels.

> **Role:** You are an expert `[Social Media Marketer]`.
> **Task:** Read my original text below and transform it into the following 3 formats:
>
> 1. Instagram Feed Caption (An attention-grabbing first line, with appropriate emojis and hashtags)
> 2. X (Twitter) Thread (Broken down into an engaging, hook-driven thread)
> 3. LinkedIn Post (Professional tone, focusing on business insights)
>
> **Original Text:** `[Paste your blog post content here]`

<br>

### 🥇 Pro Version

This goes beyond simple summarization. It generates a comprehensive content plan that perfectly reflects the latest algorithmic traits and user consumption patterns of each platform.

> **Role:** You are a top 1% `[Viral Content Strategist and Social Media Marketing Expert]`.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I have just published a highly polished article on my blog. I want to distribute the core value of this post across various social media platforms to boost brand awareness and drive traffic.
> - Goal: Do not simply summarize the text. 'Recreate' it using the most popular formats and tones for each platform to maximize reach and engagement.
>
> **Task:**
>
> Based on the provided original text, create optimized deliverables for the following 3 platforms:
>
> 1. **Instagram Carousel Plan:**
>    - Structure the content into 5 to 7 slides.
>    - Page 1 (Cover) must feature a powerful hook that stops the scroll.
>    - For each slide, provide the [Text] to be displayed and a matching [Midjourney Prompt for Image Generation].
>    - Provide a caption for the upload and 15 highly relevant hashtags.
> 2. **LinkedIn Insight Post:**
>    - The introduction must start with a relatable sentence that resonates with professionals and employees.
>    - Reframe the core message of the original text through the lens of a 'Lesson Learned in the Field' (Professional business tone).
>    - Conclude with a sharp, thought-provoking question designed to spark discussion in the comments.
> 3. **YouTube Shorts / Reels Script:**
>    - Write a script that can be read comfortably within exactly 60 seconds (approx. 130-150 words).
>    - Use a fast-paced, intuitive, and conversational tone to prevent viewer drop-off.
>    - Include instructions for text overlays (with subtitle effects) and visual directions (actions, BGM mood, etc.).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Absolutely do not compromise the core message and context of the original text.
> - Maximize readability by appropriately utilizing emojis tailored to each platform's culture.
> - Output the response beautifully structured in Markdown format.
>
> **Original Data (Input):**
> `[Paste the full text of your blog post here]`

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The true power of this prompt lies in the **'separation of platform-specific personas'**. The exact same core message requires "visual stimulation and brevity" on Instagram, but demands "practical insight and professional experience" on LinkedIn.

Especially when converting to a LinkedIn post, simply listing information will never get traction. By framing the content as **"What I learned while writing this"** or **"Failures I experienced in the field and how I overcame them"**—as instructed in the prompt—you will drive significantly more likes and shares. With this single prompt, you achieve the extreme efficiency of having three specialized content strategists sitting right next to you.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: If my original post is too long, will the AI cut it off or miss details?**
  - A: The latest models like GPT-4o and Claude 3.5 Sonnet can effortlessly handle thousands of words. However, if your article is extremely long (e.g., over 10 pages), the secret to getting a sharper output is to extract and input only the core 'body' section, leaving out the lengthy introduction and conclusion.

- **Q: Can I use the carousel image prompts directly in ChatGPT (DALL-E) instead of Midjourney?**
  - A: Yes, absolutely! Simply change the instruction from `[Midjourney Prompt for Image Generation]` to `[DALL-E 3 Image Generation Prompt]`. You can then generate both the text and the corresponding images seamlessly within a single ChatGPT conversation.

- **Q: Should I just read the Shorts script exactly as generated?**
  - A: While the AI writes in a conversational tone, everyone has a unique speaking rhythm. Read the generated script out loud once and slightly tweak the vocabulary and sentence endings to fit your natural breathing and speaking style. This ensures a much more authentic and high-quality video.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Clear Context Delivery:** Instead of just saying "change this," we explicitly explained the background and the goal of "driving traffic." This guides the AI to plan more strategic, conversion-focused copy.
2. **Platform-Specific Task Details:** By embedding platform-specific success formulas as constraints—like "ending with a question" for LinkedIn and adding "visual directions" for Shorts—we elevate the output quality to an expert level.
