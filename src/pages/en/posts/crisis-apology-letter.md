## ğŸš€ Solution: "Professional Apology Builder"

### ğŸ¥‰ Basic Version (Light Mistakes)

For tardiness or simple omissions.

> **Role:** You are a polite businessman.
> **Situation:** I was `[10 mins late to the meeting]`.
> **Request:** Write a polite apology message to my boss. Don't make excuses, and briefly add what I will do.

<br>

### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version (Serious Accidents / Claims)

When money or trust is at stake.

> **Role:** You are a Crisis Communication Expert with 20 years of experience.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - **Recipient:** `[Mr. Smith, Client]`
> - **Accident:** `[Delivery delayed by 3 days]`
> - **Cause:** `[Heavy snow paralyzed logistics]` (Shouldn't sound like an excuse)
> - **Solution:** `[Sending via Quick Service, We pay shipping]`
>
> **Task:**
> Write a sincere **Official Apology Email** based on above.
>
> **Guidelines:**
>
> 1.  Follow **The 3A Rule**: Acknowledge, Apologize, Action.
> 2.  Apologize in the first sentence (Bottom-line first).
> 3.  Explain 'Snow' as a cause but don't shift blame. ("Because of snow" X -> "Due to snow, but it's our fault for not preparing" O)
> 4.  **Tone:** Highly polite, not servile, responsible.

## ğŸ§¬ Anatomy of Prompt (Why it works?)

1.  **3A Rule:** By enforcing Acknowledge-Apologize-Action, AI avoids rambling and writes a structured letter.
2.  **Nuance Control:** "Don't make it look like an excuse" is key. AI tends to sound defensive when explaining causes. Instructing it to **'Admit Responsibility'** makes it professional.
