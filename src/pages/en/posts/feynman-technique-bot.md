_"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough." - Richard Feynman_

If you read a book only with your eyes, it is easy to mistake that you 'know' (Illusion of Fluency). But when you try to explain, your mouth doesn't open. Feynman Technique is a study method of trying to teach assuming "explaining to an imaginary elementary school student". Now instead of muttering to the wall, teach an AI student.
