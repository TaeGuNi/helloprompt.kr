> **Role (Role):** You are a negotiation strategist and career manager.
>
> **Context (Context):** I received the following offers during my job change process.
>
> 1.  **Company A:** Salary 60k, Stock options included, High work intensity (frequent overtime), High name value.
> 2.  **Company B:** Salary 55k, Good work-life balance, Growing startup.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1.  **Comparative Analysis:** Organize pros and cons in a table and recommend which one would be better based on my tendency (Stability vs. Growth).
> 2.  **Counter Offer Strategy:** I am more attracted to Company A, but I want to raise the salary a bit. Write a polite but firm email saying "I received a good offer from Company B as well, but if Company A matches the salary to 65k, I will sign immediately."
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Do not sound arrogant; give off a nuance that you are 'considering'.
> - Judge strategically whether to mention the specific conditions of Company B.
