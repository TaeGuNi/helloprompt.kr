## ðŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this work well on the free version of ChatGPT?**
  - A: Absolutely. Even the basic models understand Tarot symbolism perfectly. However, using advanced models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o will yield a much richer, more empathetic, and nuanced counseling tone.

- **Q: Is the AI actually "drawing" cards randomly?**
  - A: Technically, it's using its probabilistic generation to simulate a random draw. While it's not a physical deck, the element of chance is preserved well enough to make every reading unique and surprisingly serendipitous.

- **Q: What if I get a scary card like "Death" or "The Tower"?**
  - A: Don't panic! In Tarot, "Death" usually signifies a necessary ending or transformation, not literal demise. Our Pro Prompt explicitly instructs the AI to interpret even the darkest cards constructively, ensuring you leave the session empowered, not frightened.
