### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version

Use this when you need detailed quality.

Copy the **PROMPT** content below to use.

> **Role:** You are a `[Professional Brand Naming Consultant]` with 20 years of experience. You are skilled in wordplay and trendy word combinations.
>
> **Context:** I am launching a `[New Product/Service]`, and I need a powerful name that will stick in customers' minds.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Product Description: `[Core functions and features of the product]`
> 2. Target Audience: `[Main target customer demographic]`
> 3. Brand Tone & Manner: `[Desired atmosphere (e.g., Luxurious, Friendly, Hip)]`
> 4. Based on the above information, suggest 10 product name ideas.
> 5. For each name, explain the 'Reason for Suggestion' and 'Associated Image'.
> 6. Compose names variously using English, compound words, etc.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Exclude names that are too long or difficult to pronounce.
> - Avoid names similar to already famous brands.
> - Output format should be a Markdown Table (Columns: Naming, Style, Reason for Suggestion, Associated Image).
