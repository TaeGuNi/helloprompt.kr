Anthropic has successfully raised $30 billion in its Series G funding round, bringing the company's valuation to a staggering $380 billion.

This massive investment underscores the continued market confidence and demand for AI safety research and the secure scaling of foundation models. Anthropic plans to use this capital to accelerate the development of next-generation AI models and further expand its infrastructure for safe AI deployment.

As AI technology advances rapidly, Anthropic's commitment to building reliable and steerable AI systems remains a critical focal point for the industry.
