# The Agent Bubble: Smart Money vs. User Reality

## Follow the Money
In 2026, Venture Capitalists are pouring billions into "AI Agents"—autonomous software designed to execute tasks. The narrative is that everyone will have a personal Jarvis. But is this the next internet, or the next NFT crash?

### The Disconnect
While investors are hyper-bullish, user adoption tells a different story.
1.  **Trust Issues:** Users are hesitant to let AI handle money or sensitive data.
2.  **Reliability:** Agents still hallucinate and get stuck in loops.
3.  **Fatigue:** People are tired of configuring new tools.

### A Classic Bubble?
We are seeing classic bubble signs: valuations detached from revenue, a rush of "me-too" startups, and a disconnect from end-user utility. The "Agent Economy" is coming, but the crash will likely come first, wiping out the hype-driven players.
