This prompt isn't just a simple editing tool. It blocks artificial AI expressions at the source and completely deconstructs mechanical sentence structures that repeat at set lengths. It compresses the rigorous desking process of a 10-year senior editor into a single prompt, rewriting your draft into **vivid prose where a human pulse can be felt**.

The moment you apply this prompt to your workflow, an amazing transformation begins. Articles once bloated with obvious clichés are reborn as hooks that sharply pierce the **reader's actual Pain Points**. Mechanical lists of information become enriched with everyday, **specific metaphors and real-world examples**.

Above all, readers won't even suspect the text was written by an AI. Drawn in by the **natural rhythm and tension**—as if a close colleague is speaking right next to them—they will find themselves reading through to the final sentence in one breath. You will still complete your writing in 20 minutes, but the value and impact of that work will rival masterpieces written through 12 hours of human agony. Now, I reveal the secret of the **'Human Touch'** used secretly by true experts.

## 🚀 How True Experts Write

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this lightly when you want to quickly strip away the mechanical tone and refine it into a human speaking style.

> **Role:** You are a `[close colleague/editor]`.
>
> **Task:**
> Read the following `[Draft Text]` written by an AI and revise it into a natural human speaking style, removing all mechanical vibes (e.g., "In short," "In conclusion," or excessive emojis).

### 🥇 Pro Version (Expert Grade)

This is the master prompt deployed in professional fields when detailed quality and a perfect human touch are required. Completed after dozens of trials and errors, copy the prompt below and fill in the `[Variables]` inside the brackets to suit your situation.

> **Role:** You are a `[Content Director]` who is a 10-year senior editor and extremely sensitive to human psychology and trends.
>
> **Context:**
> - Background: As of 2026, the public is feeling extreme fatigue (Hype Score 92/100) toward soulless AI content (AI Slop).
> - Goal: Completely rewrite the input `[AI-Generated Draft]` into an authentic, human-smelling piece so that no one can ever guess it was written by an AI.
>
> **Task:**
> 1. Completely deconstruct the existing sentence structure of the provided `[AI-Generated Draft]`, keeping only the 'Core Value' that solves actual physical problems.
> 2. Start the intro with a hook based on the reader's actual `[Pain Point]` or a vivid personal anecdote.
> 3. Avoid simple lists of technical jargon in the body; instead, use everyday, specific metaphors or real-world examples.
> 4. Adjust the tone to match the level of the `[Target Audience]`.
>
> **Constraints:**
> - Forbidden Words: "In summary," "In conclusion," "Innovative," "Game-changing," "Shall we find out?", "Dive in"
> - Vary sentence lengths (use a mix of short and long sentences).
> - For mobile readability, NEVER use tables; organize information into high-readability lists.
> - **Bold** important keywords.
> - Output format must be Markdown.
>
> **Warning:**
> - Completely break away from the mechanical intro-body-conclusion structure typical of AI.
> - Never fabricate information or data (e.g., internal metric figures) that isn't certain. If you don't know, omit it. (Hallucination prevention)

## 💡 Author's Comments (Insight & How to use)

I am confident that this prompt will be the **most powerful and only survival weapon** for content creators over the next five years. The public already senses "AI-written text" in 0.1 seconds and heartlessly hits the back button. In the past, there was value in simply gathering information, but now information itself has become a common commodity that anyone can get in a second. The key to surviving the flood of content is not 'how fast or how much you write,' but **'how much human warmth can be felt.'**

Based on hundreds of A/B tests in my actual marketing and content planning roles, I've confirmed that applying this prompt to **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** mimics human nuances and rhythm most perfectly. To make a real difference, you must go beyond simply copying the prompt and perfectly understand the principles of **Constraint Control** and **Structural Deconstruction** to actively apply them to your work.

**First, you must understand the powerful control of Constraints.**
AI is essentially a model that predicts 'the next word with the highest probability.' Therefore, if left alone, it inevitably chooses the most obvious and cliché expressions. Words like "In conclusion," "Innovative," and "Shall we find out?" are the words with the highest weight in AI prediction models. The <b>'Forbidden Words List'</b> in this prompt serves to forcibly break this probabilistic bias. Just by setting forbidden words, the AI is forced to abandon familiar patterns and explore entirely new, creative, and human-like vocabulary. Keeping this list updated for your specific industry is the core cheat code.

**Second is the variation of sentence length (Pacing).**
The reason machine-written text feels particularly boring is that the sentence lengths are too uniform. Real human speech or writing catches its breath sometimes and leaves a long, lingering trail at others. The single instruction to <b>"use a mix of short and long sentences"</b> gives the output an amazing rhythm. It creates that 'distinctly human tension'—striking quickly and powerfully when delivering a core message, and flowing smoothly when further explanation is needed.

**Third is the complete destruction of the dual-ended structure.**
The 'Intro-Body-Conclusion' structure we learned in school—and which AI has as its default—provides the worst experience for modern internet readers. This is because they have to read an unnecessary intro just to get to the conclusion. By ordering the <b>'complete deconstruction of existing sentence structure,'</b> this prompt opens with a powerful hook or vivid anecdote that catches the reader off guard. The moment you veer off the expected path, the reader's brain reactivates, and they become deeply immersed in the text.

Ultimately, the true power of this prompt is not in making the AI 'smarter,' but in <b>'scarring the AI to be more human.'</b> Intentionally injecting human irregularities and emotional fluctuations into the perfectly tidy text of a machine. This is the essential insight for surviving as an irreplaceable 1% creator in the coming era of 100% AI content. I strongly encourage you to fine-tune the `[Variables]` of the prompt to find your own unique tone and manner. Finally, never forget the <b>'True Human Touch'</b>—adding a single line of a real-world episode that only you know to the generated result.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Specifying Forbidden Words (Constraints):** By blocking the cliché expressions that AI habitually overuses, it fundamentally eliminates the mechanical scent in the first stage.
2. **Varying Sentence Length (Pacing):** The instruction to intentionally alternate between short and long sentences gives the text a rhythmic tension, creating a natural breath as if a real person is speaking.
3. **Complete Deconstruction of Structure (Task):** By boldly breaking the cookie-cutter 'Intro-Body-Conclusion' template, it cleverly deviates from the reader's expected path and maximizes engagement.
