## 🚀 How Real Experts Write It

This is a prompt completed after dozens of trials and errors. Copy the prompt below, fill in the `[Variables]` inside the brackets according to your situation, and deploy it to your work immediately.

### 🥉 Basic Version

> **Role:** You are a Silicon Valley `[IT Investment Analyst]`.
>
> **Task:** Read the `[Anthropic Series G Investment Article]` below and summarize the investment scale, key investors, and investment purpose into 3 points.

### 🥇 Pro Version

> **Role:** You are a Senior Research Analyst at a global VC (Venture Capital) firm. You are an expert in AI industry trends and corporate valuation.
>
> **Context:**
> 
> - Background: Recently, Anthropic secured Series G investment at a $30 billion valuation. "AI Safety (Constitutional AI)" is a key investment keyword.
> - Goal: You need to analyze the impact of this investment news on the overall AI industry and the competitor ecosystem and report it to internal executives.
> 
> **Task:**
> 
> 1. Based on the following `[Article Text or Summary]`, analyze the core drivers of the investment.
> 2. Logically explain why the "AI Safety" emphasized in this investment justifies a $30 billion valuation.
> 3. Predict the future landscape changes of the generative AI market with 3 scenarios.
> 4. For the `[Competitor]` section, leave it in brackets so the user can insert the names of companies they want to compare (e.g., OpenAI, Google).
> 
> **Constraints:**
> 
> - To ensure mobile readability, never use Tables; instead, organize with clear headings (H2, H3) and highly readable bullet points (Lists).
> - Be sure to **bold** important key keywords.
> - Use professional business terminology but maintain a concise style so that non-experts can understand intuitively.
> 
> **Warning:**
> 
> - Do not make up figures or investor information not present in the article. Write strictly based on facts. If information is unknown, state "Unverifiable." (Hallucination Prevention)

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Assigning a Senior Analyst Persona:** We assigned the AI a very specific and professional role as a 'Senior Research Analyst at a global VC,' rather than just a chatbot. Because of this, the model writes the report not as a simple sentence summary, but with the perspective of a sharp analyst and a persuasive business style.
2. **Presenting Structured Goals:** By forcibly providing a 3-step framework—'Extract investment background → Justify core value → Predict market landscape'—we induced the output to maintain a logical and multi-dimensional research flow without being scattered all over the place.
3. **Fact Check Constraint (Hallucination Prevention):** We placed a powerful safety device at the very bottom of the prompt: "Do not make up figures not present." This ensured the credibility of the business report by fundamentally blocking the risk of Hallucination, which is the most fatal weakness when analyzing financial and investment information.
