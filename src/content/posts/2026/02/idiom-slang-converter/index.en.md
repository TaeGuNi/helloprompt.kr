## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this only work for American English?**
  - A: By default, the prompt specifies an "American Hollywood Screenwriter," but you can easily swap this out! Just change the role to "British TV Screenwriter" or "Australian Local Expert" to get regional slang like "knackered" or "chock-a-block."

- **Q: Will the AI ever hallucinate fake slang?**
  - A: It's rare with top-tier models like GPT-4 or Claude 3.5, but to be absolutely safe, the prompt's request for the "Origin" of the idiom acts as an anchor. This forces the AI to reference real, verifiable linguistic history rather than fabricating a phrase.

- **Q: Can I use this for professional business emails?**
  - A: Absolutely. Just tweak the context slightly: ask for "corporate jargon" or "professional idioms" instead of "trendy slang." The prompt structure works perfectly for mastering board-room English.
