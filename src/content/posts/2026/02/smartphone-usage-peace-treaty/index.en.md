>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not use overly complex vocabulary; keep it suitable for a 7-year-old's comprehension level. Do not add overly strict or harsh punishments.

## ğŸ™‹ FAQ

- **Q: Does this work for older kids or teenagers?**
  - A: Absolutely. For teenagers, simply modify the `Context` to reflect their age (e.g., "14-year-old teenager") and change the reward/penalty system to something more age-appropriate, like Wi-Fi access or allowance, rather than stickers.

- **Q: What if my child still refuses to turn off the phone after the 5-minute warning?**
  - A: Consistency is key. Gently but firmly enforce the penalty outlined in the contract immediately the next day. You can also ask the AI for "De-escalation scripts for when a child breaks the screen time contract."
