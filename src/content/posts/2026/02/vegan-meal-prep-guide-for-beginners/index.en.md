## ðŸ’¡ Writer's Insight

Batch cooking vegan food can be tricky because plant-based ingredients often release more moisture than meat, leading to soggy lunches by Wednesday. The real magic of this prompt lies in the **Constraints** and **Context**. By explicitly requesting "batch cooking workflows" and "storage tips," the AI shifts from giving you a standard recipe to acting as a culinary logistics planner. It factors in moisture control (like freezing and pressing tofu for a meatier texture) and separate dressing storage, saving you from culinary disasters. This is a game-changer for anyone struggling to maintain a healthy diet during busy workweeks!
