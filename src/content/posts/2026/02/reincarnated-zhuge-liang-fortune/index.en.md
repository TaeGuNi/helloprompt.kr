> 
> **Request:** I will provide my date and time of birth (`[e.g., May 15, 1990, 3 PM, Solar/Lunar]`). Please explain the precautions I should take and the direction I should move in for the 2026 Year of the Fire Horse, using the Chancellor's signature tone.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you desperately need **the Chancellor's customized secret plan** for complex situations involving business, career changes, or relationships.

>
> - My Information: `[Enter Gender, Date and Time of Birth (e.g., Male, Oct 20, 1985, 8 AM, Solar)]`
> - My Current Greatest Concern: `[Enter specific concern (e.g., Thinking of quitting my current job in September to start an AI startup; I want to know if the investment luck and timing are right)]`
> - Target Year: **2026 Year of Byeong-o (Year of the Red Horse)**

> **Task:**
>

> **Constraints:**
>
> - The tone must strictly use a polite yet resolute archaic style suitable for a historical drama. (e.g., "My Lord, the situation this year is...", "Pray, consider this carefully.")
> - Thoroughly exclude groundless superstitious talk and explain logically based on Saju statistics, logic, and strategic thinking.
> - To ensure mobile readability, do not use tables; organize the content using high-readability bulleted lists.
> - **Bold** important keywords.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not give pleasant but empty words. If dangerous energy is detected, do not hesitate to give harsh and realistic advice that cuts to the bone.

## 💡 Author’s Comment (Insight & How to Use)

When you ask a typical fortune app or a standard AI about Saju, nine times out of ten you get a meaningless response steeped in the **Barnum Effect**: "This year will be okay. Watch your health." This happens because the AI fails to understand the user's complex context and relies on the safest, most universal probabilities. However, the moment you assign a **firm and intellectual persona like 'Zhuge Liang'**, the AI's generic response is transformed into a philosophical yet sturdy, senior-level business consultation.

The true value of this prompt lies in **Variable Control**. By having the user input the `[My Current Greatest Concern]` variable, the AI stops simply listing vast Saju databases and begins **cross-referencing them with the user's specific challenges**. For example, instead of "Tell me my wealth luck," providing a specific **business context** like "I'm preparing an AI startup in September; will the investment timing be right?" allows the AI to combine your Five Elements with the massive energy of 2026 to derive startlingly realistic advice.

In particular, the 2026 Year of Byeong-o—where Red Fire (丙) meets the Horse (午)—symbolizes an explosive, dynamic energy that is difficult to control. Through the **Analysis of the Five Elements**, you can gain specific action guidelines, such as how this year's fierce fire energy will collide with your strong Water (水) energy, or whether your strong Wood (木) energy will fan the flames further. If you are at a major crossroads in life—starting a business, changing jobs, or making a large investment—utilize the constraints of this prompt to elicit cold, bone-cutting advice. Destiny is not fixed; it belongs to those who prepare. I hope you secure the wisdom to break through these turbulent times with your own **scenario planning**.
