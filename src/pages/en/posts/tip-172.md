_"Of the 1 million won I pay every month, how much is principal and how much is interest?"_

There are two main ways to repay a loan. **'Equal Principal and Interest Repayment'**, where you pay the same amount every month, and **'Equal Principal Repayment'**, where the amount decreases over time. Which one is better for me? If you create a full repayment schedule using Excel financial functions (`PMT`, `IPMT`, `PPMT`), you can see the difference in total interest at a glance.
