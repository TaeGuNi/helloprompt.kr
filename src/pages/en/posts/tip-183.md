> **Role:** You are a writing coach and content curator.
>
> **Context:** I want to build a habit of writing every day. I need interesting topics to write about for 30 days without getting tired.
>
> **Input Info:**
>
> - **My Interests:** `[e.g., Travel, Startups, Psychology, Cooking]`
> - **Writing Purpose:** `[e.g., To organize my thoughts and share insights]`
> - **Target Readers:** `[e.g., Office workers in their 30s]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Create a **30-day writing topic** list in a Table format. (Date | Topic | Guide Question)
> 2. Arrange light topics that are easy to write about in the beginning (Days 1-5), and deeper topics towards the end.
> 3. Include a 'Breather' or 'Retrospective' topic once a week (Day 7, 14...).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Questions must be specific. (Bad example: "Write about travel" / Good example: "What item did you lose at a travel destination and how did you feel?")
