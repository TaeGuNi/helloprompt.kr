This service does **not** collect, store, process, or analyze any images or visual data from users. All interactions are text-based.

Our system relies entirely on the information provided directly by the user via text input. We do not infer personal data from external sources or biometric sensors.

User inputs are used solely for the purpose of generating immediate responses and are processed in accordance with strict privacy standards. We do not sell or share user data with third parties for advertising purposes.
