>
> **Context:** I received a `[Strong Complaint about Delivery Delay]` email from a customer. I need to calm this situation and propose a reasonable alternative.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Based on the key points (Facts) I wrote roughly, write a reply email with a polite and empathetic attitude.
> 2. Validate the customer's angry emotions, but explain our side's situation focusing on facts, not excuses.
> 3. Finish by naturally proposing `[Compensation/Alternative]`.
>
> **Key points I want to convey:**
>
> - Sorry for being late. It was unavoidable due to the courier strike.
> - I just checked and it is scheduled to arrive tomorrow.
> - I will give you 5,000 points as a reserve with a sorry heart.
