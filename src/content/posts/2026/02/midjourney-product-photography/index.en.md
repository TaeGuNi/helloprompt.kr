## 🚀 Solution: "Studio Lighting Master"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you quickly need the basic shape of a product isolated on a clean white background. Simply copy and paste it into Midjourney.

> **/imagine prompt:** professional product photography of a **[Coffee Tumbler]**, studio lighting, white background, 8k, ultra detailed --v 7 --ar 3:4

<br>

### 🥇 Pro Version

This is the expert-level prompt used when you need to render luxury cosmetics, high-end textures, or a deep sense of spatial dimension.

> **/imagine prompt:** professional commercial product photography of a **[Minimalist Organic Skin Lotion Bottle]** sitting on a marble podium, soft cinematic lighting, ray tracing, bokeh, depth of field, **[pastel pink and white color palette]**, minimalist composition, shot on Hasselblad X2D 100C, 100mm lens, 8k resolution, hyper-realistic, --ar 4:5 --stylize 250 --v 7

**[Variable Input Guide]**

- `[Minimalist Organic Skin Lotion Bottle]`: Change this to the specific name or shape of the product you are selling. (e.g., `Luxury Leather Wallet`)
- `[pastel pink and white color palette]`: Change this to your brand's main theme colors. (e.g., `matte black and gold color palette`)

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The real reason this prompt works instantly in professional settings isn't because it vaguely asks the AI to "draw it beautifully." It works because it gives the AI precise instructions using the **physical characteristics of an ultra-high-end medium format camera ('Hasselblad X2D 100C') and a '100mm lens'.** Midjourney v7 recognizes this text and near-perfectly simulates the unique optical distortion, depth of field (bokeh), and distinct light reflectance that the actual camera lens would produce in real life.

**💡 Pro Tips for Practitioners:**

- **The `--stylize` Parameter Trap:** If you set the `--stylize` value too high (over 700), the shape of the product will warp unrealistically, or text and logos will get crushed. To safely maintain the original form of the product while bringing out a commercial aesthetic sensibility, a value between **250 and 300** is highly recommended.
- **When You Need a Perfect Cutout:** Try adding `--no shadow, reflection` to the end of your prompt. Shadows and reflections will vanish, making Photoshop background removal (magic wand tool) 10 times easier.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Will the result look 100% exactly like the actual product I'm selling?**
  - A: No. Since Midjourney generates virtual images based on text, it cannot be perfectly identical to your real-world product. We highly recommend using this prompt for "mood-setting" shots that establish the visual atmosphere of your product page, or as a "high-quality background/podium source" to seamlessly composite your actual product photos onto.

- **Q: Does this work exactly the same in the previous version, Midjourney v6?**
  - A: Yes, the prompt itself is compatible. However, the texture rendering and the expression of light reflection on object surfaces introduced in v7 are overwhelmingly superior. Therefore, we strongly advise using v7 when generating commercial product photography.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Gear (Specifying Equipment Specs):** By explicitly mentioning `Hasselblad X2D 100C` and `100mm lens`, we forced the AI to accurately mimic the optical output of actual physical lenses.
2. **Lighting & DoF (Depth of Field):** The powerful combination of `soft cinematic lighting`, `ray tracing`, `bokeh`, and `depth of field` keeps the main product crisp and vibrant while softly blurring out the background, driving dramatic focal attention.
3. **Setting (Spatial Stability):** We assigned a specific physical location by stating `sitting on a marble podium`, which prevents the product from awkwardly floating in mid-air or blending poorly into the environment.
