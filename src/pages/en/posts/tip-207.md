### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version

Copy the **PROMPT** below and use it.

> **Role:** You are a Senior Tech Lead and Recruiter at an IT company.
>
> **Context:** I am writing project descriptions for my portfolio. I want to polish them to highlight **'Problem Solving Skills'** rather than just a simple list of features.
>
> **Input Material (My Project Draft):**
> [Write a rough description of your project here. Ex: Made a shopping mall app. Added payment function. Used React.]
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1.  Rewrite the content richly according to the structure below.
>     - **Project Background:** Why this project was started (User pain points/problems)
>     - **Key Challenges:** The most difficult points during development/planning
>     - **My Solution:** How you solved those difficulties technically/logically
>     - **Results:** What achievements were made as a result (Quantitative/Qualitative)
> 2.  Mix in professional terminology appropriately to increase credibility.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Remove obvious phrases like "Implemented various functions".
> - Mention where and why specific Tech Stacks were used.
