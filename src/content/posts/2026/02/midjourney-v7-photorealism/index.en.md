## 🚀 The Solution: "AI Photographer Master Prompt"

Copy this prompt and paste it into **ChatGPT (or Claude)**. Do NOT paste this directly into Midjourney!

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you need a decent photorealistic prompt quickly.

> **Role:** You are a professional commercial photographer and a Midjourney v7 prompt maker.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you need high-end studio quality, down to peach fuzz, pores, and floating dust particles.

> **Role:** You are a global commercial photographer with 20 years of experience, acting as a **'Midjourney v7 Prompt Engineer'** specializing in hyper-realistic rendering.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - I need an ultra-high-resolution photorealistic image depicting `[Insert your desired subject and mood. e.g., A Korean woman in her 20s drinking coffee in a cafe]`.
> - This image will be used as the main shot for a high-end lookbook or luxury magazine ad.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Write an English Midjourney prompt by perfectly combining the following 5 elements:
>    - **Subject:** Detailed description of facial expressions, gaze, clothing texture, skin peach fuzz, and pores.
>    - **Camera Gear:** Lens focal length and aperture (e.g., 85mm f/1.2), camera model (e.g., Sony A7R V, Hasselblad X2D).
>    - **Lighting:** Direction and type of light source (e.g., Cinematic lighting, Rembrandt lighting, Softbox, Golden hour).
>    - **Atmosphere/Color:** Film stock-based color grading (e.g., Moody tone, Kodak Portra 400, Fujifilm Superia).
>    - **Parameters:** Must append `--ar 16:9 --v 7 --style raw --q 2 --stylize 100` at the very end of the prompt.
> 2. Output the generated English prompt inside a code block.
> 3. Provide a brief explanation (in my language) from an expert's perspective on why you chose this specific camera lens and lighting setup.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Prioritize an intuitive, comma-separated list of keywords over perfect grammar.
> - Omit unnecessary filler words; focus on core nouns and descriptive adjectives.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - NEVER include words that imply a drawing or CG, such as '3D render', 'illustration', 'painting', or 'unreal engine'. It must be strictly a 'photograph'.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The ultimate cheat code for Midjourney v7 photorealism lies in **'Lighting'** and **'Lens focal length'**. Simply asking for "beautiful lighting" will result in that airbrushed, plastic skin characteristic of AI.

Instead, control the nature of the light specifically with terms like "Rembrandt lighting" or "Window blind shadows". Furthermore, incorporating telephoto lenses like `85mm f/1.2` and the keyword "shallow depth of field" naturally blurs the background, creating a dramatic result that draws the viewer's eye straight to the subject. Weak on camera jargon? Don't worry. Using the Pro version above lets ChatGPT pick the perfect lens for you.

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: What does the `--style raw` parameter at the end do?**
  - A: It strips away the 'exaggerated, pretty aesthetic' that Midjourney applies by default. You need this to preserve the raw, authentic feel of an unedited camera file. It is absolutely mandatory for photorealistic generation.

- **Q: In v6, six fingers or distorted anatomy were common. How is v7?**
  - A: Midjourney v7 has resolved 99% of finger deformities and joint structure errors. Now, even individual strands of hair and the subtle veins on the back of a hand are rendered realistically, so you can stop stressing over anatomical details.

- **Q: Does Midjourney understand if I input the subject in a non-English language?**
  - A: Midjourney natively understands English best. That's why the core strategy of this post is to **"utilize ChatGPT as a translator and prompt engineer"** to convert your ideas into high-quality English prompts.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1.  **Expert Persona (Role Play):** Assigning ChatGPT the specific role of a '20-year global commercial photographer' extracts professional vocabulary for Camera Gear and Lighting instead of generic words.
2.  **Structured Variables:** By clearly separating the core components of a photograph into 5 categories (Subject, Gear, Lighting, Atmosphere, Parameters), we force the AI to comprehensively describe every detail without omission.
3.  **Negative Constraints:** Explicitly banning words like '3D render' or 'CG' prevents the hallucination of generating illustrations instead of photographs.
