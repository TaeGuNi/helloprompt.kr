With the release of macOS Tahoe, the most debated feature is undoubtedly "Intelligent Edge".

Instead of precise pixel-level cursor changes, AI predicts your intent when hovering near a window edge. While some find it intuitive, others argue it lacks precision.

Designers and developers, in particular, are complaining about accidental resizing and are demanding a toggle to revert to the old behavior.

Apple calls it the "future of computing," but the controversy seems far from over.
