### ü•á Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need detailed, high-quality output.

Copy the **PROMPT** content below and paste it into ChatGPT or Claude.

> **Role (Role):** You are a Senior HR Interviewer and Talent Acquisition Expert at a top-tier corporation.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I am preparing to interview an applicant for the `[Job Position]` role.
> - Target: The key highlights of the applicant's resume include: `[Resume Summary or Key Achievements]`.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Formulate 3 in-depth, scenario-based questions to rigorously assess their core job competencies.
> 2. Create 2 behavioral questions designed to evaluate their Culture Fit and soft skills.
> 3. Provide 1-2 probing questions (tail questions) for each main question to dig deeper into their responses.
> 4. Outline an Evaluation Guide detailing what constitutes a 'Strong Answer' versus a 'Red Flag' for each question.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - All questions must be open-ended; avoid any questions that can be answered with a simple "Yes" or "No".
> - Focus strictly on the Behavioral Event Interview (BEI) framework, asking about past experiences rather than hypothetical pressure scenarios.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not invent arbitrary criteria. Base the evaluation guide strictly on industry standards for the specified role to prevent hallucination.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I use this for entry-level positions or internships?**
  - A: Absolutely. Just add a constraint like "Focus on foundational skills, learning agility, and academic projects rather than extensive industry experience."

- **Q: Does this work well with the free version of ChatGPT?**
  - A: Yes, it works flawlessly. However, advanced models like GPT-4 or Claude 3.5 Sonnet will generally provide more nuanced probing questions and a more sophisticated evaluation guide.

- **Q: What if the generated questions are too technical for me to evaluate?**
  - A: You can instruct the AI to "Provide the evaluation guide in layman's terms" or ask it to generate questions that focus more on problem-solving methodologies rather than specific syntax or tools.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Playing:** By assigning the persona of a "Senior HR Interviewer," the AI adopts a professional, discerning, and analytical tone.
2. **Contextual Anchoring:** Feeding the `[Resume Summary]` ensures the questions are tailored specifically to the candidate, preventing generic output.
3. **Behavioral Focus (BEI):** Constraining the AI to use the BEI framework forces candidates to prove their skills through past actions, not theoretical knowledge.
4. **Evaluation Guide:** Asking for a rubric transforms the prompt from a simple question generator into a comprehensive interviewing tool, standardizing your assessment.
