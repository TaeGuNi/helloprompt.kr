>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - All carbohydrate calculations MUST be based on "Net Carbs" (Total Carbs - Fiber).
> - Absolutely exclude all forms of added sugar, refined flour, and highly processed seed oils.
> - Output the meal plan and macro targets in a clean Markdown table format.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not recommend extreme fasting protocols for a beginner. If unsure about a specific medical condition, advise consulting a physician.

## üí° Writer's Insight

This prompt is a game-changer because it shifts the focus from "what you can't eat" to "what you should do next." When I first tried keto, I spent hours logging every almond into a macro-tracking app, which quickly led to burnout. By defining the `[Rice/Bread/Noodles]` variable, the AI specifically targets your psychological cravings and offers direct substitutes (like cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles) rather than just giving generic advice. It effectively turns a daunting metabolic shift into an easy-to-follow, personalized 3-day checklist.
