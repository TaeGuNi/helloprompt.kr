## 📝 Writing Job Descriptions (JD) That Attract Applicants

- **🎯 Recommended For:** HR Managers, Startup Founders, Hiring Managers
- **⏱️ Time Required:** 60 minutes → 5 minutes
- **🤖 Recommended Model:** All AI Models (Claude 3.5 Sonnet highly recommended)

- ⭐ **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆
- ⚡️ **Effectiveness:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- 🚀 **Utility:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

> _"You’ve published a new job opening, but your inbox remains filled with irrelevant resumes or utterly empty... What's the real bottleneck?"_

Stiff, corporate, and painfully boring job postings are practically invisible to high-performing talent. To win the brutal war for talent, you must craft a narrative that showcases your company's unique culture and clearly defines the strategic impact of the role. A top-tier candidate doesn't just want a job; they want a mission. Let's transform your JD from a mundane, demanding checklist into a powerful, irresistible talent magnet. By leveraging AI to architect your job descriptions, you can position your startup as an industry leader and organically attract the exact A-players you need.

---

## ⚡️ 3-Line Summary (TL;DR)

1. **Ditch the Clichés:** Stop relying on lazy phrases like "family-like atmosphere" and focus instead on tangible growth trajectories.
2. **Structure Matters:** Leverage AI to organize Roles & Responsibilities (R&R) and core qualifications with undeniable clarity and persuasion.
3. **WIIFM (What's In It For Me):** Radically shift the perspective from what your company demands to the massive value the applicant stands to gain.

---

## 🚀 The Solution: "Startup Recruitment Branding Expert"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this prompt when you need a rapid, foundational draft for an urgent job posting.

> **Role:** You are an elite Startup Recruitment Branding Expert.
> **Request:** Write a highly attractive, modern Job Description for a `[Job Title]` position at a `[Industry]` startup. Focus on drawing in ambitious, top-tier talent.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Expert)

Use this advanced framework for high-quality, culturally aligned, and meticulously detailed job descriptions. 
Copy the **PROMPT** below and paste it directly into ChatGPT or Claude.

> **Role (Role):** You are a top-tier Startup Recruitment Branding Expert and elite Copywriter.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: We are a company that `[Brief Company Introduction / Mission]`.
> - Goal: We need to hire an outstanding `[Job Title / Position]`.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Brainstorm 3 captivating job posting titles that immediately pique top candidates' curiosity.
> 2. Write a compelling introduction explaining why this specific role is pivotal to our company's ultimate success, anchored by our core vision, and outlining the exact career growth the candidate will experience.
> 3. Clearly structure the core responsibilities (R&R) and the required qualifications using easily scannable bullet points.
> 4. Attractively package our organizational culture and core benefits to maximize appeal.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Strictly avoid corporate clichés (e.g., "family-like atmosphere", "fast-paced environment").
> - Emphasize the WIIFM (What's In It For Me) principle entirely from the applicant's perspective.
> - Maintain an engaging, professional, yet deeply approachable tone.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not invent arbitrary benefits, perks, or requirements that are not standard to the industry unless specified. If you are unsure, leave highly visible placeholders like `[Insert specific benefit here]`.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

Writing a brilliant Job Description is fundamentally identical to B2C copywriting—the "product" is the job itself, and your "customer" is the elite applicant. When executing this prompt, the quality of the `[Brief Company Introduction / Mission]` you provide will absolutely make or break the final output. Don't just settle for "We make an app." Instead, write: "We are building a revolutionary fintech app that saves independent freelancers 10 hours a week on accounting." The more radically specific you are about your mission, the better the AI can tailor its tone to attract candidates who genuinely resonate with your exact goals. I highly recommend using Claude for this specific task, as it naturally excels at nuanced, empathetic, and human-sounding copywriting compared to other models.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Should I use ChatGPT or Claude for this specific workflow?**
  - A: While both models work beautifully, Claude 3.5 Sonnet consistently produces more natural, less "robotic" copy for HR and employer branding purposes. It captures the subtle, human nuances of organizational culture exceptionally well.

- **Q: What if the AI generates technical skills that are far too advanced for the actual salary we are offering?**
  - A: AI models tend to aim extremely high by default. Simply add a firm constraint to the prompt like: _"Target mid-level professionals with 3-5 years of experience, keeping the required skills realistic and strictly focused on core competencies rather than a 'unicorn' wishlist."_

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Injection:** By defining the AI as a "Startup Recruitment Branding Expert," we instantly shift its default vocabulary from standard, dry HR jargon to modern, growth-oriented language.
2. **The WIIFM Constraint:** Forcing the AI to focus heavily on "What's In It For Me" fundamentally transforms the JD from a one-sided, demanding checklist into a highly persuasive value proposition.
3. **Banning Clichés:** Explicitly forbidding lazy, overused phrases forces the AI to articulate actual, meaningful cultural benefits that top-tier candidates truly care about.
