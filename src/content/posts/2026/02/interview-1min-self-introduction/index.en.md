- **Q: Does this work well for fresh graduates with no work experience?**
  - A: Absolutely. Instead of professional experience, input your academic projects, leadership roles in clubs, or specific coursework into the `[Strength]` variables. The AI will perfectly adapt the tone to highlight your potential and learnability.

- **Q: The generated script feels a bit too formal for my industry (e.g., a startup). How can I fix this?**
  - A: You can simply add a line to the **Constraints** section: _"Adopt a conversational, energetic, and slightly informal tone suitable for a dynamic tech startup environment."_ The AI will adjust the vocabulary accordingly.

- **Q: Can I use this for a 3-minute introduction?**
  - A: Yes, but you shouldn't just ask the AI to "make it 3 minutes." For longer intros, you need more substance. Add a `[Strength 3]` and include a new instruction in the **Task** section: _"Elaborate on one specific project where I applied these strengths to overcome a major challenge."_
