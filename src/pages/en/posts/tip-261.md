**[10-Minute Running Recovery Routine]**

**1. Standing Instant Cool-down (Using Park Bench)**

- **Calf Stretching:** Push against a bench or wall and press your back heel firmly to the ground. (30 seconds each side)
- **Hamstring Stretching:** Place one leg on the bench and bend your upper body forward. (30 seconds each side)

**2. At-Home Foam Roller Care (Focus on Outer Knee Pain)**

- **Iliotibial Band Massage:** Lie on your side, place the foam roller on the outside of your thigh, and roll up and down. (It can be very painful, but endure for 1 minute!)
- _Tip:_ If it hurts too much, support the floor with the opposite leg to distribute your weight.
