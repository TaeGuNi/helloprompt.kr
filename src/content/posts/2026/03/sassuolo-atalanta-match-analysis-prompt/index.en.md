## 🚀 Solution: "Perfect Sports Match Analyzer"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you need a rapid summary of key statistics and a concise match preview.

> **Role:** You are a `[Football Data Analyst]`.
> **Task:** Summarize the last 5 head-to-head matches, predicted lineups, and 3 key tactical points for the `[Sassuolo vs Atalanta]` match. You must reflect the latest information through web search.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you require a meticulously detailed, high-quality content draft for a blog post, marketing report, or premium newsletter.

> **Role:** You are a 10-year experienced `[Sports Data Analyst and Professional Copywriter]`.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: A football match between `[Sassuolo vs Atalanta]` is scheduled soon.
> - Goal: Write a professional and engaging match preview report that will capture the attention of football fans.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Deeply analyze the recent 5-match records and goal difference data of both teams.
> 2. Explain in detail 2 key tactical points that will determine the outcome of this match.
> 3. Check and list the key missing players (injuries and suspensions) for both teams via real-time web search.
> 4. Finally, complete an attractive preview draft that can be published immediately on blogs or social media.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - All data must be cross-verified based on the most recent web search results.
> - Adequately mix professional football tactical terms (e.g., build-up, half-space, beating the press, Gegenpressing, etc.) to enhance professionalism.
> - Format the output using a combination of bullet points and short paragraphs to ensure excellent readability on mobile environments. (Using Markdown tables is strictly prohibited)
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not make up information regarding the injury list or predicted lineups if facts cannot be checked or are uncertain. Explicitly state "Currently unconfirmed" instead. (Strictly prevent hallucinations)

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can this framework be applied to other sports, such as baseball or esports, instead of just football?**
  - A: Absolutely. Simply replace `[Sassuolo vs Atalanta]` with `[LA Dodgers vs San Diego Padres]` or `[T1 vs Gen.G]`, update the assigned role to reflect an expert in that specific field, and the prompt will work flawlessly.
- **Q: The AI keeps generating incorrect information about missing players or starting lineups. How can I fix this?**
  - A: Free-tier AI models are heavily reliant on outdated training data. To completely eliminate factual errors regarding the latest squad rotations and injury reports, you must use an engine that excels at real-time web scraping, such as Perplexity or ChatGPT Plus with the web search feature enabled.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Forcing Real-Time Data:** By explicitly instructing the AI to base its findings "on web search," we preemptively neutralize the risk of fatal hallucinations caused by outdated historical data.
2. **The Dual Persona:** Simultaneously assigning the roles of "Data Analyst" and "Professional Copywriter" forces the AI to strike the perfect balance between uncompromising data accuracy and a highly engaging, persuasive writing style.
3. **Mobile-First Optimization (Constraints):** We strictly prohibited the use of Markdown tables—which frequently break on smaller mobile screens—and mandated bullet points instead. This guarantees that your formatting remains visually flawless when copied directly to a blog or social media platform.
