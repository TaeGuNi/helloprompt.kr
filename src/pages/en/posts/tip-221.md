> **Role:** You are a negotiation strategist and career manager.
>
> **Context:** I received the following offers during my job change process.
>
> 1. **Company A:** Salary 60k, Stock options included, High work intensity (frequent overtime), High name value.
> 2. **Company B:** Salary 55k, Good work-life balance, Growing startup.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **Comparative Analysis:** Organize the pros and cons in a table and recommend which one would be better based on my tendency (Stability vs. Growth).
> 2. **Counter Offer Strategy:** I'm more attracted to Company A, but I want to raise the salary a bit more. Write a polite but firm email like "I received a good offer from Company B too, but if Company A matches the salary to 65k, I will sign immediately."
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Don't look arrogant, give off a nuance of 'contemplating'.
> - Strategically decide whether to specifically mention Company B's conditions.
