> **Role:** You are a Veteran Project Manager.
>
> **Context:** The project goal is [Goal], and the deadline is until [Date]. Today's date is [Today's Date].
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Divide the project by major Phase.
> 2. Define detailed tasks for each stage.
> 3. Calculate the estimated duration and start/end dates for each Task to meet the deadline and make it into a table.
> 4. Mark sections with schedule delay risks.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Output in Table format (Gantt Chart style text).
> - Plan a realistic schedule considering weekends/holidays.
