## 🚀 The Solution: "Investment Insight Extractor"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this for a quick, scannable summary of any startup funding news.

> **Role:** You are a seasoned Venture Capital Analyst.
> 
> **Task:** Summarize the following funding news: `[Insert News Text]`. Focus strictly on the valuation, key investors, and the primary technical or business goal.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you need a deep dive into the strategic implications of a funding round to present to your team, stakeholders, or investment committee.

> **Role:** You are an elite Tech Investment Strategist and AI Market Analyst.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I need to analyze a recent funding announcement in the generative AI sector.
> - Goal: Extract not just the financial data, but the strategic direction, competitive positioning, and technological focus of the company.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Analyze the provided news text: `[Insert News Article or Press Release]`
> 2. Break down the analysis into the following distinct categories:
>    - **Financials:** Total capital raised, valuation, and notable investors.
>    - **Strategic Focus:** Where is the capital going? (e.g., compute power, safety research, talent acquisition).
>    - **Competitive Edge:** How does this position them against rivals like OpenAI, Google, or Meta?
>    - **Market Implication:** What does this specific investment mean for the broader AI ecosystem?
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Output the financial analysis strictly in a well-formatted Markdown table.
> - Follow the table with concise, high-impact bullet points detailing the strategic insights.
> - Maintain a professional, objective, and analytical tone suitable for a boardroom presentation.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not hallucinate financial figures, valuations, or investor names. If specific information is missing from the provided text, explicitly state "Information not provided in the source."

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this prompt work well with older or smaller language models?**
  - A: It works best with advanced reasoning models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o. Smaller models might struggle to infer the "Competitive Edge" accurately and run a higher risk of hallucinating market dynamics.

- **Q: Can I use this for non-AI startup funding news?**
  - A: Absolutely! Just change the `[Role]` in the first line to match your specific industry (e.g., "Fintech Market Analyst" or "Biotech Strategist") to generate highly tailored insights.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Assignment:** Framing the AI as an "elite Tech Investment Strategist" forces it to adopt an analytical, forward-looking tone rather than acting as a simple, passive text summarizer.
2. **Structured Output (Constraints):** Mandating a Markdown table ensures the hard numbers are instantly scannable, while the bullet points keep the strategic analysis highly digestible.
3. **Anti-Hallucination Guardrails:** The warning section explicitly prevents the AI from guessing investors or valuations that aren't present in the provided text, ensuring your research maintains absolute data integrity.
