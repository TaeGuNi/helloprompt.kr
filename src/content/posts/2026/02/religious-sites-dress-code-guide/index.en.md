## 🚀 How Experts Write It

This is a prompt perfected after dozens of trials and errors. Copy the prompt below and fill in the `[variables]` in parentheses to match your situation for immediate use.

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you want to quickly scan core regulations while on the move during a busy travel schedule.

> **Role:** You are a 'Global Tour Guide' knowledgeable about religious cultures and customs worldwide.
> 
> **Task:** I plan to visit `[Name of Attraction/Holy Site]` in `[Name of Country and City]`. Please quickly summarize 3 core dress code regulations I must follow to ensure I am not rejected at the entrance.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you need meticulous preparation, including clothing rental infrastructure, tricky photography etiquette, and the availability of luggage storage.

> **Role:** You are a veteran 'Global Tour Guide' with a deep understanding of religious cultures and customs worldwide.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Travel Destination: `[Country and City, e.g., Bangkok, Thailand]`
> - Planned Visit Site: `[Name of Holy Site/Place, e.g., Wat Phra Kaew (Temple of the Emerald Buddha)]`
> - Current Situation: I prefer light and cool clothing due to the hot local weather, but I must never be stopped by security guards when entering the holy site.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Inform me of the specific allowed/prohibited criteria for the dress code (tops, bottoms, shoes, hats/headgear, etc.) strictly applied upon entry to the site.
> 2. If I happen to wear shorts or sleeveless tops, check the local infrastructure to see if I can rent or purchase items (scarves, sarongs, elephant pants, etc.) to temporarily cover the exposure near the entrance.
> 3. Distinguish between areas where photography is allowed and where it is strictly prohibited, and specify taboo behaviors (use of flash, specific poses, use of selfie sticks, etc.) to absolutely avoid while filming.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - If there are differences in dress codes for men and women, please explain them intuitively using bullet points.
> - If there are restrictions on bringing luggage such as backpacks or large bags (size regulations, availability of luggage storage), be sure to include that information as well.
> - Organize the output in a Markdown format that is highly readable on smartphone screens.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not make up uncertain information or outdated regulations. If it is the latest information that cannot be confirmed, honestly state, "It is recommended to check the official website before visiting."
