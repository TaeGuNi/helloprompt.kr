## 🚀 How Real Experts Write Them

This prompt was perfected after dozens of trials and errors. Copy the prompt below and replace the `[Variable]` parts with your specific details to use it immediately in your work.

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this lightly when you need a quick draft or reference.

> **Role:** You are a commercial product photographer with 10 years of experience.
>
> **Task:** Create a product photo of `[Product Name]`. Make the background a clean solid color and use studio lighting to make the product stand out as much as possible.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this for sophisticated reflections and overwhelming high-end quality that could be used on a billboard right away. Copy and paste the prompt below into your AI image generator (like Midjourney).

> **Role:** You are the Lead Commercial Photographer and Lighting Director for a global luxury brand.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I need a main thumbnail shot for an official website and Instagram for a new product launch.
> - Subject: `[Luxury perfume in a crystal glass bottle, gold cap, splashing water droplets around it]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Set the lighting delicately to maximize the texture of the subject (reflections, transparency, etc.).
> 2. Lighting: Professional studio lighting, three-point lighting, softbox, edge lighting, reflection on the floor.
> 3. Style: Commercial photography, 4k, hyper-detailed, clean sharp focus, minimal solid background, high-end advertisement.
>
> **Constraints (For Midjourney):**
>
> - Force maximum aspect ratio and quality: `--ar 16:9 --v 6.0 --style raw --q 2`

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The absolute core secret that beginners often overlook, but which causes the quality of AI image generation to skyrocket, is the **control of 'Lighting.'** Beginners often rely on abstract and vague instructions like "Draw it pretty and flashy," "Make it top quality," or "Masterpiece." However, AI—especially diffusion models like Midjourney or Stable Diffusion—has the ability to perfectly understand and depict the principles of light in the physical world at the pixel level. Therefore, when you specify <b>the physical direction and properties of light</b>—such as "Apply softbox lighting from a 45-degree side angle" or "Give it a strong rim light on the edges to separate it from the background"—the AI produces much more three-dimensional, realistic, and breathtaking results.

Particularly in <b>Commercial Photography</b> for e-commerce detail pages or social media feeds, <b>Reflection</b> and <b>Depth of Shadow</b> are the most important metrics that decide whether a product looks cheap or like a high-end luxury item. The `Studio lighting` and `Three-point lighting` keywords included in the prompt are <b>magic cheat codes</b> that transform a flat, plain 2D image into an overwhelming 3D object worthy of being the main feature in a luxury brand catalog. It transplants the actual studio setup formula—shaping the form with Key light, adding texture to shadows with Fill light, and clearly defining the silhouette with Back light—directly into the AI prompt.

To utilize this prompt 200% in practice, it is crucial to <b>describe the `[Subject]` variable to the absolute extreme.</b> Instead of just writing "perfume bottle," describe the texture and dynamic elements obsessively: "a heavy body made of crystal glass, a matte-textured gold cap, and fine water droplets splashing freshly around it." The lighting keywords shine brightest on top of these detailed textures. The transparency of the crystal will shatter brilliantly when it meets the back light, and the matte gold cap will hold a luxurious glow under the softbox lighting.

Also, never skip the background constraints like `Minimal solid background` or `Pure white background`. Generative AI has a natural tendency to fill empty spaces with various flashy elements it imagines (over-generation). By strictly limiting the background to a 'solid color,' you force the AI to <b>concentrate 100% of its computing power and descriptive capability on the fine details and lighting effects of the central subject.</b> This results in a double benefit: it pushes the quality of the product itself to the limit while reducing the post-production time for background removal (masking) in Photoshop to just one second. Those who master the flow of light and the physical laws of lenses through prompts will ultimately dominate the quality of commercial AI images.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I apply this lighting setup to generate portrait photos (ID cards, CEO profiles)?**
  - A: Absolutely! Just change the subject part to `Portrait of [Person description]`. You can instantly get high-quality body profiles or trustworthy professional CEO portraits that look like they were shot in a high-end studio for hundreds of dollars. Sophisticated lighting setups exert the same powerful influence on portraits as they do on products.

- **Q: Background removal is too tedious for use on a detail page.**
  - A: Try specifying `Pure white background` or `Solid hex color #FFFFFF background` in the prompt's style constraints. Because the brightness contrast between the subject and the background becomes extreme, you can extract a clean image—down to a single strand of hair—in just one second using one-click 'Background Remover' tools in Photoshop or Canva.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Targeting with Professional Lighting Terms (`Softbox`, `Edge lighting`):** By inserting standard equipment terms used in the actual commercial photography industry, we accurately target and retrieve the vast 'high-quality studio shooting dataset' that the AI has pre-learned.
2. **Background Noise Control (`Minimal solid background`):** We fundamentally blocked the AI from wasting computing power by describing unnecessary background elements. This ensures all performance is focused on the subject's unique textures and fine details.
