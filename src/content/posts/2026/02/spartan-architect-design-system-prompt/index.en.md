> 2. **8-Pixel Spatial Rhythm:** All margins, paddings, widths, and heights must be strictly composed of multiples of 4px or 8px. The use of magic numbers (`15px`, `22px`) is punishable by immediate rejection.
> 3. **Typography Modular Scale:** No eyeballing font sizes. Follow a rigid system where line-height and letter-spacing are algorithmically bound together as a 1:1 set.
> 4. **Motion Choreography:** Control animation durations as micro (150ms) or macro (300ms), and enforce physics-based Easing curves exclusively.
> 5. **Unified Visual Vocabulary:** Utilize a single, cohesive icon family and strictly maintain border-radius consistency across all elements.

> 8. **Multisensory Information Delivery:** Error and Success states must always be rendered simultaneously employing 3 distinct elements: 'Color + Icon + Semantic Text'.

> 10. **Mathematical Derivation of State:** Hover and Disabled states must never rely on arbitrary colors; they must be derived through calculated, mechanical adjustments such as brightness reduction or opacity shifts.
> 11. **Dark Mode Encapsulation & Multilingual Expansion Tolerance:** Do not spam `dark:bg-gray-800` directly onto components. Text containers must follow Intrinsic Sizing principles and embed `flex-wrap` so the layout remains structurally sound even in verbose multilingual environments.
