## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I use this prompt to analyze actual patient medical records?**
  - A: **Absolutely NOT.** Due to HIPAA, GDPR, and strict data privacy laws, you must never input personally identifiable information (PII) or real clinical patient records into public AI models. Use this strictly for personal wearable data (without your name) or mock data for content creation.
- **Q: Does this work well with standard smartwatch data (Apple Health, Garmin, Oura)?**
  - A: Yes! You can simply copy and paste your daily summary or week-in-review text from your wearable app directly into the `[Health Data]` variable. Modern AI models are exceptionally adept at parsing and contextualizing standard biometric metrics.

- **Q: Is the AI's advice medically safe?**
  - A: The prompt includes a strict constraint to focus on _lifestyle optimization_ rather than medical treatment, alongside a mandatory disclaimer. However, always use common sense and consult a real doctor before making drastic dietary or physical changes.
