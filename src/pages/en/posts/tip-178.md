> **Context:** The sentence I wrote is too dry and explanatory. Fix this so the reader can feel it vividly as if they are on the scene.
>

>

> **Atmosphere Options:**
>
> 1. **Horror/Thriller:** Tension, unpleasantness, danger
> 2. **Sentimental/Faint:** Traces of time, loneliness, memory
> 3. **Mysterious/Fantasy:** Bizarre, secret, magical atmosphere
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Instead of abstract emotion words like "scary", "sad", induce emotions with sensory descriptions.
> - Mix similes and metaphors appropriately to express literarily.
