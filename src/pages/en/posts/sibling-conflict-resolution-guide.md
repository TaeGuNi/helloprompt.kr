> **Role:** You are a fair judge and a counselor.
>
> **Context:** An older brother (8 years old) and a younger brother (5 years old) are fighting over the TV channel rights.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Write an intro for a 'Speaking Right Talk' that listens to both children's stories fairly.
> 2. Ask questions that encourage them to check what each other wants and find a compromise (Rock Paper Scissors, taking turns for 10 minutes, etc.).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Prohibition of hierarchy logic like 'You yield because you are older', 'He is younger'.
> - Finish with a mission to hug each other after reconciliation.
