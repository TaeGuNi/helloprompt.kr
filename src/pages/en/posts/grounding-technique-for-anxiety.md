_"Have you ever suddenly couldn't breathe and your mind went blank?"_

Anxiety separates us from reality and drives us into a vortex of negative thoughts. What is needed at this time is **'Grounding'**, lowering the anchor of the mind. It is a training to turn attention to this moment, here (Here & Now). AI becomes a guide in emergency situations.

3.  Ask questions that break the chain of anxious thoughts to regain reason.
