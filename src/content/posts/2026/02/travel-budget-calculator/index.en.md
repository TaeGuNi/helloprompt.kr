> **Context (Context):**

> 4. Suggest 2 **Local Money-Saving Tips** relevant to the destination.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Display all monetary amounts in both USD and the local currency (e.g., JPY).
> - Calculate food expenses based on realistic unit prices (e.g., 'Lunch $15, Dinner $30').
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not hallucinate prices. If you lack real-time data for a specific cost, clearly state that the figure is an estimate based on historical averages.
