## 🚀 The Solution: "Sleep Hygiene Consultant"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you need a quick, high-level overview.

> **Role:** You are a Sleep Medicine Expert and a Behavioral Psychologist specializing in CBT-I (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia).
> **Request:** Analyze my daily routine and sleep environment to provide a customized, actionable guide to overcoming my insomnia.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Expert)

Use this for a deep, personalized analysis. Copy the prompt below and fill in the brackets before pasting it into your AI.

> **Role:** You are a Sleep Medicine Expert and Behavioral Psychologist. You are highly skilled in Lifestyle Correction for Sound Sleep (CBT-I).
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Current Situation: For the past `[week]`, I haven't been able to fall asleep until `[2 AM]`, and I wake up exhausted at `[7 AM]`.
> - Daily Routine: `[Wake up at 9 AM, drink 1 Americano after lunch at 1 PM, eat dinner at 8 PM, use smartphone in bed for 1 hour from 11 PM]`
> - Sleep Environment: `[No blackout curtains, slight road noise from outside]`
> - Goal: `[To fall asleep by 11:30 PM and wake up refreshed]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Analyze my routine and identify the 3 biggest factors disrupting my sleep.
> 2. Design an 'Ideal Evening Routine' starting 2 hours before my target bedtime to help reset my circadian rhythm.
> 3. Provide 3 immediate, practical environmental tweaks to enhance my sleep quality.
> 4. Teach me a proven relaxation technique (like the 4-7-8 breathing method) to use when I can't fall asleep.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Output the analysis and routine in a clear Markdown structure.
> - Do not provide medical diagnoses or prescribe medication. Focus strictly on behavioral and environmental corrections.
> - Avoid cliché advice (e.g., "just go to bed earlier"). Give specific, actionable directives (e.g., "Switch to decaf or herbal tea after 2 PM").

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: Does this prompt work on the free versions of AI (like standard ChatGPT)?**
  - A: Yes, absolutely. Sleep hygiene and CBT-I principles are extensively documented within their core training data. Because of this, even free models like GPT-4o-mini or Claude 3 Haiku can generate excellent, highly accurate recommendations based on this prompt.

- **Q: What if my schedule changes every day, like with shift work?**
  - A: You can simply modify the `[Context]` variables to reflect your unique shift patterns. For instance: *"I work night shifts from 10 PM to 6 AM."* The AI will instantly adapt and generate a customized sleep schedule, optimal meal timings, and a strategic light exposure plan tailored entirely to your non-traditional hours.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role-Playing Expertise:** Assigning the specific persona of a CBT-I expert forces the AI to draw its answers from scientifically validated behavioral therapies, completely bypassing generic, surface-level wellness blog advice.
2. **Hyper-Personalized Context:** By providing precise timestamps and specific environmental details, the AI can identify exact biological bottlenecks—such as delayed melatonin release caused by late-night screen time—that are entirely unique to **you**.
3. **Actionable Constraints:** Explicitly banning cliché advice guarantees that you receive practical, immediate directives like "dim all overhead lights at 9 PM," rather than useless platitudes like "try to relax more."
