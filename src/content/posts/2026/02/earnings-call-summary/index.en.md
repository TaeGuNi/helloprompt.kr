## ğŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Where can I find the earnings call transcripts to paste?**
  - A: Websites like Seeking Alpha, The Motley Fool, or the "Investor Relations" section of the company's own website usually provide free transcripts within a few hours of the call ending.

- **Q: The transcript is too long and the AI gives an error. What should I do?**
  - A: If you are using a model with a smaller context window, you might need to split the transcript. I recommend feeding the "Prepared Remarks" first, asking for a summary, and then feeding the "Q&A Session" separately. Alternatively, upgrade to a model like Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o which easily handles 100k+ tokens.

- **Q: Can the AI predict the stock price movement based on this?**
  - A: No. AI cannot predict the future. It analyzes the _textual sentiment and facts_ presented in the call. The market may react unpredictably based on broader macroeconomic factors or valuation concerns not discussed in the call. Use this strictly as a research tool, not direct financial advice.
