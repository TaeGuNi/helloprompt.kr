## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Is it okay to drink as much decaf coffee as I want right before sleep?**
  - A: It’s not perfectly 0mg. Decaf coffee usually contains a trace amount of caffeine, about 2 to 10mg. Therefore, it's best to avoid consuming large quantities immediately before bed.

- **Q: Doesn't the rate of caffeine metabolism vary from person to person?**
  - A: Correct. Depending on genetics or liver function, the caffeine half-life can range from as short as 3 hours to as long as 10 hours. If you are generally sensitive to coffee, try adding the condition `- My caffeine sensitivity: Very high (Please calculate with an 8-hour half-life)` to the **Input** section. You will get much more accurate and personalized results.

- **Q: Can it calculate for green tea, black tea, or energy drinks besides coffee?**
  - A: Certainly. Just write the type and approximate volume of the drink in the input field, and the AI will infer and calculate based on the average caffeine content for that beverage. (e.g., 1 can of Monster Energy 355ml)
