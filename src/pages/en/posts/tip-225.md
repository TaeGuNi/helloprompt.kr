### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version

Use this when you need detailed quality.

Copy the **PROMPT** below and use it.

> **Role:** You are a LinkedIn influencer and content editor.
>
> **Context:** I am going to post on LinkedIn based on my work experience. I want to write an article that is professional yet has a human scent.
>
> **Material (Topic):**
>
> - **Event:** `[e.g., My opinion was rejected in today's meeting]`
> - **Thought/Learning:** `[e.g., Realized lack of preparation. Thought I should persuade with data]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **Hook:** Start the first sentence with a question or a shocking sentence that makes people stop scrolling.
> 2. **Storytelling:** Unfold my experience honestly and plainly. (Attitude admitting failure)
> 3. **Insight:** Give a lesson on what I learned and how I will apply it.
> 4. **Readability:** Divide paragraphs frequently for easy viewing on mobile, and use emojis appropriately.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Avoid trying to teach too much (condescending) or boasting tone.
> - End with a question asking for people's opinions (Induce comments).
