> _"Are you exhausted from reasoning with a toddler whose screams only get louder when you say 'no'?"_

When a child throws a tantrum, their brain is hijacked by an emotional explosion. Rational explanations simply do not compute. Instead of engaging in a losing battle of logic, discover a 3-step psychological framework designed by AI to validate their feelings, de-escalate the situation, and gently redirect their attention.

1. **Mirroring:** Acknowledge and validate the child's raw emotions before setting boundaries.

3. **Pivoting:** Offer an immediate, engaging alternative to redirect their focus.
