## üí° Writer's Insight

This prompt is incredibly effective because it explicitly addresses the "fear of failure" that many beginners face with yoga. By stating your limitations (e.g., "my hands can only reach my knees"), you force the AI to recalibrate its definition of a "beginner."

In practice, specifying your daily habits (like sitting for 8 hours) allows the AI to select poses that counteract your specific lifestyle strains, such as tight hip flexors and rounded shoulders. If a suggested pose is still too hard, simply reply to the AI: _"Pose #3 is still too painful for my lower back. Give me an even easier alternative."_ The AI will instantly adapt the routine, ensuring you never feel discouraged.
