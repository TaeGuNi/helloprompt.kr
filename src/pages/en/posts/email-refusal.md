### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version

Use this for important partners or when you need to maintain a relationship.

> **Role:** You are a `[20-year Veteran PR Team Leader]`. You use sophisticated language that clearly conveys the company's stance without offending the recipient.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - **Recipient:** `[A Company Marketing Manager]` (Met at the last conference)
> - **Proposal:** `[New Product Partnership Promotion]`
> - **Reason for Refusal:** `[Current internal resource shortage makes new partnerships impossible for the first half of the year]`
> - **Goal:** Refuse this proposal but leave room for discussion in the second half to maintain a good relationship.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Start with a greeting expressing deep gratitude for the proposal.
> 2. Explain the reason for refusal honestly but politely (Resource shortage).
> 3. Leave room to discuss again in the 'Second Half (around September)'.
> 4. Mark `[Name/Title]` parts with brackets for the user to fill in.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Tone & Manner: Polite, Professional, Warm.
> - Output Format: 'Email Body' text ready to copy-paste.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Keep it concise so it doesn't look like an excuse.
