## üöÄ The Solution: "Micro-Emotion Injector"

### ü•á Pro Version

For professional-grade content with varied tones and comprehensive visual direction.

> **Role (Role):** You are an elite social media poet and viral copywriter with millions of engaged followers.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I need short, hard-hitting micro-poetry to overlay on aesthetic Instagram Reels or TikTok videos.
> - Goal: Maximize user engagement, saves, and shares through deep relatability and wit.
>
> **Request (Task):**
>
> 1. Write **3 distinct micro-poems** (maximum 5 lines each) based on the inputs below.
> 2. **Style 1 (Witty/Twist):** A clever, punchy poem that makes the reader chuckle or feel delightfully "called out" (e.g., relatable struggles).
> 3. **Style 2 (Comforting/Emotional):** A warm, empathetic poem that provides solace to a tired mind.
> 4. For each poem, provide a **Background Image Concept** (what visual matches the text) and **5 optimized Hashtags**.
>
> **Inputs (Variables):**
>
> - **Topic:** `[e.g., Dieting, Monday mornings, Unrequited love, Burnout]`
> - **Tone & Manner:** `[e.g., Frustrated, Hopeful, Sarcastic, Melancholic]`
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Avoid complex vocabulary. Use everyday language that hits hard and resonates deeply.
> - Utilize line breaks masterfully to create visual rhythm and dramatic pacing.
> - Do not hallucinate statistics or facts; stick to emotional truths.

## üôã FAQ

- **Q: Does this work well in languages other than English?**
  - A: Absolutely. While this prompt is in English, you can simply add "Please write the poetry in [Language]" to the Constraints section. AI models are surprisingly adept at capturing poetic nuance across various languages.

- **Q: My AI's output sounds too robotic or cheesy. How do I fix this?**
  - A: Tweak the "Topic" and "Tone" variables to be hyper-specific. Instead of saying "Sad," try "The specific kind of sadness when your coffee gets cold." The more specific and human your input is, the less clich√© the AI's output will be.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Playing:** Positioning the AI as a "viral copywriter with millions of followers" forces it to prioritize engagement and readability over traditional, rigid poetry formats.
