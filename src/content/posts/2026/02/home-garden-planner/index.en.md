## 🚀 The Solution: "AI Custom Plant Butler Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version (Entry-Level Plant Matching)

Use this prompt to find a companion plant that perfectly matches your living space and daily lifestyle.

> **Role:** You are an elite `[Lead Planterior Designer]` with exceptional botanical knowledge and a keen eye for spatial design.
>
> **Task:**
> Analyze my living environment and personality below to recommend 3 resilient plants that will thrive in my home and beautifully enhance the space.
>
> **My Environment:**
> - Housing Type: `[Apartment balcony (extended living room window area)]`
> - Lighting Conditions: `[South-east facing (direct sunlight from 9 AM to 1 PM)]`
> - Ventilation: `[Poor cross-ventilation, aired out for less than 1 hour a day]`
> - My Personality: `[Busy professional (frequently forgets to water, can only manage maintenance once every 2 weeks)]`
>
> **Output Format:**
> Include the 'Reason for Recommendation', 'Language of Flowers', 'Watering Difficulty', and 'Interior Styling Tips' for each plant.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Plant Emergency Diagnosis & Recovery Solution)

Use this with a photo when a plant you are already growing is withering or showing unknown lesions.

> **Role:** You are a **'Senior Plant Pathologist'** who saves dying companion plants through precise analysis.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Goal: Accurately diagnose the condition of the plant in the photo and establish an emergency treatment plan to revive it within 14 days.
> - Plant Type: `[Albo Monstera]`
> - Current Status: `[Leaves are turning yellow from the bottom and drooping. The soil surface is wet and attracting fungus gnats.]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **Precise Diagnosis:** Based on the attached photo and symptom description, analyze the most likely causes. (Provide the top 2 probabilities, e.g., overwatering, underwatering, over-fertilization, fungal infection)
> 2. **Golden Time First Aid:** Detail the physical actions I must take within the next 24 hours, step-by-step. (e.g., cutting rotted roots, drying soil, repotting)
> 3. **2-Week Recovery Roadmap:** Create a 14-day intensive care guide to follow until the plant stabilizes.
> 4. **Environment Reset:** Re-establish the correct light, temperature, humidity, and ventilation conditions suited for this plant's characteristics.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Absolutely no abstract advice (e.g., "Give it appropriate water"). Provide specific, quantitative instructions like "When the soil is dry up to 3cm deep (test with a wooden chopstick), water thoroughly until it drains out the bottom of the pot."
> - Format the output in highly readable Markdown.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - If poor photo quality prevents a clear judgment, do not guess hastily; instead, ask questions about the parts that need further clarification to prevent hallucinations.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The most common reason beginner "plant parents" kill their greenery isn't a lack of love, but an excess of it—specifically, **overwatering**. Blindly following the generic internet formula of "watering once a week" inevitably leads to root rot. The true value of this prompt shines when you leverage the Vision capabilities of advanced AI models. 

Take about 3 photos from different angles: one showing the overall shape of the plant, one capturing the discoloration patterns on both sides of the leaves, and one detailing the condition of the soil surface. Upload these alongside the Pro prompt. The AI can clearly distinguish between **thrip damage**, **root rot from fungus gnat larvae**, or simply the **natural shedding of old leaves**—issues that are incredibly difficult to diagnose through simple text searches. Because early intervention dictates the survival rate when dealing with plant pests and diseases, I highly recommend utilizing this AI Emergency Room prompt at the very first sign of trouble.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: What if I don't know the exact or scientific name of my plant?**
  - A: That's not a problem at all. Just upload a clear photo of the entire plant and add this sentence to the beginning of your prompt: "First, identify the exact species and scientific name of this plant, then proceed with the diagnosis." The AI will pinpoint the common name (e.g., Ficus Audrey) and scientific name to deliver a highly tailored diagnosis.

- **Q: Can it recommend plants for a windowless studio bathroom with zero natural light, rather than a balcony?**
  - A: Absolutely. If you specify your conditions—for example, "No windows, artificial fluorescent lighting turned on for only 4 hours a day, high humidity"—the AI will recommend plants uniquely adapted to extreme shade and highly humid environments, such as the Golden Pothos or Boston Fern.

- **Q: I want to maintain a plant growth record. Can the AI create a tracking format for me?**
  - A: Yes! Simply add this request to the end of your prompt: "Create a 'Plant Growth Observation Log' template in Markdown format that I can fill out weekly. Please include fields for the date, watering status, new sprouts, and changes in leaf size." You will receive a perfectly structured template that you can easily copy and paste right into Notion.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Controlling Microclimate Variables:** Instead of vaguely describing your space as "my house," inputting precise data for the three critical factors of plant survival—sunlight duration, light direction, and ventilation level—maximizes the chances of your plant thriving.
2. **Segmented Expert Personas:** By distinctly splitting the AI's role into a **Planterior Designer** (focused on aesthetic satisfaction) or a **Plant Pathologist** (focused on survival and treatment), you accurately guide the AI to deliver highly specialized responses based on your current needs.
3. **Quantitative Directives (Constraints):** Eliminating ambiguous instructions like "water moderately" or "sometimes," and instead enforcing objective, measurable standards (e.g., "wait until the soil is dry 3cm deep"), dramatically improves your execution and the plant's health.
