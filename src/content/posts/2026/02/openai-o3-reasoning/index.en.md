## 🚀 Solution: "O3 Deep Reasoning Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Deploy this prompt when you need a swift, transparent breakdown of logical steps.

> **Role:** You are a top-tier analyst in `[Specific Field]`.
> **Task:** To solve `[Complex Problem]`, do not jump to hasty conclusions. Instead, detail your entire thought process step-by-step.

### 🥇 Pro Version

Push the reasoning engine to its absolute limits. Leverage this framework for rigorous hypothesis testing, intricate coding challenges, and high-stakes strategic planning.

> **Role:** You are the core cognitive brain of an autonomous agent, acting as a `[Specific Role, e.g., Lead Data Scientist]`.
>
> **Context:**
> - Background: We are currently facing `[Current Problem or Bottleneck]`.
> - Objective: We need to flawlessly resolve `[Logical Conclusion to Deduce or Hypothesis to Verify]`.
>
> **Task:**
> 1. Deconstruct the primary problem into at least 3 distinct sub-problems.
> 2. For each sub-problem, construct your logic using a rigorous 'Chain of Thought' methodology.
> 3. Critically evaluate (Self-Correct) the deduced hypothesis or code, and propose highly targeted revisions.
> 4. You must incorporate `[Key Variable or Data]` to formulate the final conclusion.
>
> **Constraints:**
> - Structure your entire reasoning process using Markdown bullet points and numbered lists.
> - Intuitive guessing or skipping logical steps is strictly prohibited; you must explicitly state the causal relationship for every single deduction.
>
> **Warning:**
> - If you encounter a logical leap or lack verifiable information, instantly halt the reasoning process at that exact step and request the missing data. Do not hallucinate.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works)

1. **Problem Deconstruction:** By shattering a monumental challenge into manageable, bite-sized components, we prevent cognitive overload and keep the AI strictly within its optimal processing parameters.
2. **Enforcing Self-Correction:** We forcibly activate the model's most potent capability: its innate drive to critique, debug, and revise its own logical pathways before finalizing an output.
3. **Blocking Logical Leaps (Constraints):** By explicitly mandating the AI to halt when it encounters an information deficit, we aggressively neutralize the risk of hallucinations, guaranteeing a structurally sound final deliverable.
