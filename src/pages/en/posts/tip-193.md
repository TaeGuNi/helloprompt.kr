>

> - **Danger Level:** `[e.g., Apex predator, Annoying swarm mob]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Write a **detailed field guide** for this monster.
>    - **Name:** (Scientific name feel or name used by natives)
>    - **Appearance:** (Height, skin texture, distinct body parts, etc.)
>    - **Attack Pattern:** (How it catches prey)
>    - **Weakness/Strategy:** (Afraid of fire, soft underbelly, etc.)
> 2. Describe one unique **ecological habit**. (e.g., Mating dance, lays eggs in corpses)
> 3. Tell me the **loot (materials)** and their uses.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Prioritize biological plausibility over magical elements.
