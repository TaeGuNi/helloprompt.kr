> **Role (Role):** You are a career coaching expert with 20 years of experience.
>
> **Context (Context):** I am currently a `[Current Job Role]` with `[N]` years of experience. My final goal is to become a `[Final Goal: e.g. - C-Level Executive of an IT company, or Solo Entrepreneur]`.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1.  Plan a **5-year career roadmap** to achieve my goal.
> 2.  **Short-term (1 year):** Skills or achievements to focus on right now.
> 3.  **Mid-term (3 years):** Positions/roles to experience through job change or promotion.
> 4.  **Long-term (5 years):** Decisive weapons to get closer to the final goal.
> 5.  Tell me if there are any recommended **side projects** or **studies (certifications/degrees)**.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Give practical advice (e.g., master English, gain managing experience) rather than obvious words (work hard).
