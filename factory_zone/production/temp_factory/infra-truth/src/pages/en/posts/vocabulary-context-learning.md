>
> **Context:** I know the meaning of `[Specific Word]` but I'm confused about how it is used in real situations and what nuance differences there are with similar words.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Briefly explain the **Dictionary Definition** and **Etymology** of the word I entered.
> 2. List 3 **Synonyms** easy to confuse with this word, and organize subtle **Nuance Differences** in a table.
> 3. Write 3 **Contextual Examples** (Business, Daily Conversation, Academic/News) and attach translations.
> 4. Finally, give one fill-in-the-blank **Quiz** (Process the answer to prevent spoilers or place it at the bottom).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Make the explanation kind and easy to understand.
> - Write both in English and Korean (or target language).
> - Utilize Markdown for output format to increase readability.
>
> **Input Word:** `[Enter the word to learn here]`
