## 🚀 How the Real Pros Write

This prompt was perfected after dozens of trials and errors. Copy the prompt below and fill in the `[Variables]` inside the brackets to fit your situation for immediate professional use.

### 🥉 Basic Version

A lightweight prompt for quickly generating idea sketches.

> **Role:** You are a genius rapper in the hip-hop scene known for being the trendiest and best lyricist.
>
> **Task:** Write an 8-bar rap verse according to the theme and rhyme I provide.
>
> - Theme: `[My bank balance is at the bottom, but my dreams reach the sky]`
> - Target Rhyme: `[AI/AY (e.g., Sky, High, Die, etc.)]`
> - Condition: Include at least one brilliant punchline that will make the listener go "wow."

### 🥇 Pro Version (Expert)

A professional-grade prompt for when you want to control everything from the overall atmosphere of the track to the precise details of the punchlines beyond simple rhyming.

> **Role:** You are a genius rapper and lyricist with top-tier skills. You are a master of wordplay and double entendres.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Beat Style: `[Boom Bap, BPM 85]`
> - Mood: `[Aggressive & Confident, with a sharp edge]`
> - Theme: `[My ambition and success, rising from the bottom to the top]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Write a 16-bar rap verse using the **Target Rhymes** provided below.
> 2. Ensure it’s not just a one-dimensional rhyme matching vowels, but a densely designed structure where the meaning and context of the words are organically connected.
> 3. Include at least two **Punchlines (double entendres, wordplay, punchline metaphors)** that make the listener react, and provide an explanation of the exact meaning of those punchlines below the lyrics.
>
> **Input Information:**
>
> - **Target Vowel (Rhyme):** `[AI/AY (e.g., High, Sky, Tie, Die, Dice, Price)]`
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - For mobile readability, never use tables; organize in a highly readable bulleted list format.
> - Mix English and local slang in a trendy, natural 8:2 ratio.
> - Avoid awkward translation styles or textbook literary tones. Use raw slang or trendy expressions currently used in the real hip-hop scene.
> - Adjust the syllable count of each bar tightly so that the rhythm hits the beat accurately when recited over the track. **Bold** important keywords.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not make up information you are unsure of; answer "I don't know" if necessary. (Hallucination prevention)

## 💡 Author's Comment (Insight & How to use)

The true value of this prompt lies in delegating the sophisticated **'Rhyme Design'** to the AI, moving beyond simple 'songwriting.' In rap, the impact of vowel rhymes (e.g., AH-EE, OH-AH, etc.) determines over 80% of the overall flow quality. The human brain consumes an enormous amount of energy searching for words in a rhyme dictionary and connecting them contextually. However, AI scans a vast vocabulary database that exceeds human limits, weaving together brilliant word combinations and rhyme schemes we might never have thought of in just a second. Because the AI explores words in parallel that would take us tens of minutes to find, the tempo of your work isn't interrupted, allowing you to maintain full immersion.

The key know-how for utilizing this prompt 100% in a real-world environment is to treat the AI's output as your personal **'Inspiration Bank'** rather than copying and recording it as-is. Keenly catch **one sharp punchline** or two or three catchy rhyme words the AI casually tossed out and naturally melt them into your own authentic narrative. That is the secret to creating a truly high-quality, non-artificial verse. Rather than expecting perfect lyrics from the start, I recommend running the prompt several times, extracting the phrases you like, and 'assembling' them into one completed track.

There are also tips for controlling variables. If the text feels a bit stiff, try modifying the `[Mood]` variable in the Context section of the prompt to something more detailed like <b>"a bit more arrogant and laid-back"</b> or <b>"a gloomy and cynical murmuring tone."</b> For target rhymes, instead of filling all 16 bars with one vowel structure, creating variations like `[First 8 bars use 'AI', last 8 bars use 'OH-AH']` makes the flow much more diverse. Furthermore, adding a specific rapper's style (e.g., "Use Tablo's unique poetic metaphors" or "Write in Vince Staples' blunt and realistic style") will completely change the texture of the vocabulary the model outputs.

Also, the **requirement for punchline explanations (Task 3)** at the bottom of the prompt isn't just a courtesy. This device is a powerful <b>self-censorship mechanism</b> that prevents the AI from spitting out meaningless, forced wordplay. Since the AI must explain the logic of its own metaphors in text, it helps in completing much deeper and harder-hitting double entendres. While reading the explanations, you might even have a new linguistic epiphany, thinking, "Ah, I didn't know this word had such a double meaning." I hope this prompt fully explodes your potential, which was once trapped in the pain of creation.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: The rhyme quality is too one-dimensional or sounds childish like nursery rhymes.**
  - A: The depth of understanding linguistic wordplay varies depending on the type of AI model. In this case, try giving a detailed instruction in the constraints like *"Avoid one-dimensional rhymes focused on simple nouns, and place multi-dimensional, sophisticated rhymes by crossing various forms of verbs and adjectives."* Particularly in literary creative fields where metaphors and rhythm are important, the **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** model generates overwhelmingly "sticky" results.

- **Q: It looks great as text, but the timing keeps going off when I try to rap it over an actual beat.**
  - A: Even if you limit the syllable count, current AI text generation logic doesn't perfectly calculate 'auditory rhythm' like grooves or off-beats. Therefore, you should use the AI-generated text as a draft and go through the process of reciting it while playing the actual beat. Finalizing the **'Flow Design'**—boldly cutting unnecessary particles and tightly compressing long words—is the creator's responsibility.

## 🧬 Anatomy of the Prompt (Why it works?)

1. **Precise Beat and Mood Context:** We injected very specific genre backgrounds like 'BPM 85' and 'Boom Bap.' This forces the AI to prioritize words with the heavy, raw vibe unique to authentic hip-hop rather than light vocabulary suited for Trap or Singing Rap.
2. **Self-Censorship Mechanism Through Explanation (Task):** We assigned a task to directly explain the hidden meaning of the derived punchlines. This device prevents the AI from outputting meaningless wordplay and helps it verify the logic internally once more to complete deeper double-entendre expressions.
3. **Textualizing Auditory Rhythm (Constraints):** We placed a strong constraint on 'syllable count control.' This ensures the output takes the form of a practical 'lyric' that forms a groove when a rapper actually recites it with real breath, rather than being just a block of text.
