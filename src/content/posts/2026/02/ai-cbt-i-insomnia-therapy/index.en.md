## ðŸ™‹ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Isn't 'Sleep Restriction Therapy' just sleeping less? Won't I be more tired?**
  - A: Initially, yes. The goal is to consolidate fragmented sleep. By severely restricting your time in bed to match your _actual_ sleep time, you build up a massive "sleep debt" (sleep drive) that eventually forces your brain to sleep deeply and continuously. As your sleep efficiency improves, the AI will gradually extend your time in bed.

- **Q: Can I use this prompt every day?**
  - A: Absolutely. In clinical CBT-I, patients keep a daily sleep diary. You can feed your exact sleep hours into this prompt each morning, and the AI will recalculate your sleep efficiency and adjust your bedtime schedule for that specific night.

- **Q: What if I have a medical condition causing my insomnia?**
  - A: This AI prompt is strictly for _primary insomnia_ caused by psychological factors (stress, anxiety, poor sleep habits). If you suspect your insomnia is due to sleep apnea, restless leg syndrome, or a severe medical condition, please consult a healthcare professional.
