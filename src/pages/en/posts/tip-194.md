### ğŸ¥‡ Pro Version

> **Role:** You are a political science professor and architect of a civilization simulation game.
>
> **Context:** I'm setting up a fictional nation for a novel. I need a political system that is historically plausible yet unique.
>
> **Nation Basic Info:**
>
> - **Geography/Environment:** `[e.g., Oasis cities in the middle of a desert, Barren northern mountains]`
> - **Major Resources:** `[e.g., Magic stones, Spices, Strong iron ore]`
> - **Cultural Features:** `[e.g., Reveres martial arts, Developed commerce]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Design the **Political System** of this nation. (e.g., Military dictatorship, Merchant council, Theocratic rule, etc.)
> 2. Set the **ruling class** holding power and the **opposing forces** challenging them.
> 3. What is the **biggest political/social problem** this nation is currently facing?
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Avoid simple structures like "The King does everything." Describe checks and balances (or imbalances) of power.
