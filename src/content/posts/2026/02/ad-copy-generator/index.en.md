## 🚀 The Solution: "ROAS Booster Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this lightweight prompt when you need a quick, solid draft on the fly without complex setups.

> **Role:** You are an elite `[Performance Marketing Expert]` specializing in crafting ad copy that yields exceptionally high Click-Through Rates (CTR) and Conversion Rates (CVR).
>
> **Task:** Write an engaging, scroll-stopping primary text for a social media ad promoting `[Product/Service]`.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Expert)

Deploy this advanced prompt when you need highly targeted, professional-grade variations for rigorous A/B testing.

Copy the **PROMPT** block below and paste it directly into ChatGPT or Claude.

> **Role:** You are an elite `[Performance Marketing Expert]` renowned for writing ad copy that consistently achieves sky-high Click-Through Rates (CTR) and Conversion Rates (CVR).
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Platform: `[Facebook/Instagram]`
> - Product/Service: `[Product/Service Name]`
> - Target Audience: `[Specific demographics or interests of the target audience]`
> - Core Offer/USP: `[Discount rate, unique features, social proof, etc.]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Based on the provided context, write 3 distinct versions of the Ad Primary Text.
> 2. Ensure each version rigorously follows these specific frameworks:
>    - **Version A (Storytelling):** Empathize deeply with a specific customer pain point and position our product as the ultimate, life-changing relief.
>    - **Version B (Direct Response/Benefit-Driven):** Keep it punchy and aggressive. Highlight the core features and the immediate, tangible benefits (e.g., discounts) right upfront.
>    - **Version C (Social Proof/Review):** Write it in a highly authentic, UGC (User-Generated Content) style, as if a genuine, thrilled customer just left a glowing 5-star review.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - The most critical hook MUST be placed within the first two lines (before the "See More" truncation kicks in).
> - Enhance readability by using appropriate, visually appealing emojis (but do not overdo it to the point of looking spammy).
> - Conclude every single version with a clear, urgent, and unmistakable Call To Action (CTA).
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Absolutely do not invent or hallucinate features, statistics, or promotions that are not explicitly mentioned in the context. If crucial details are missing, ask for clarification instead of making assumptions.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this prompt work well with the free version of ChatGPT?**
  - A: Yes. The structural constraints built into the Pro version are robust enough to guide even standard, free models to produce highly usable results. However, utilizing premium models like GPT-4 or Claude 3.5 Sonnet will drastically improve the natural flow and nuanced tone, especially when generating the highly empathetic Storytelling variant.

- **Q: How do I prevent the generated copy from sounding too "robotic" or obviously "AI-generated"?**
  - A: The secret lies entirely in the quality of the variables you provide. The more hyper-specific and human-centric your inputs are for the `[Target Audience]` and `[Core Offer/USP]` fields, the more authentic the output becomes. Always give the AI's final output a quick human polish to ensure the vocabulary perfectly aligns with your brand's unique voice and tone guidelines.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Playing:** By explicitly assigning the persona of an "elite Performance Marketing Expert," the AI immediately discards its default, dry, informational tone and adopts a highly persuasive, conversion-focused psychological stance.
2. **Constraint Management:** Mandating that the primary hook must appear before the Facebook/Instagram "See More" cutoff forces the AI to front-load the most compelling, scroll-stopping information, perfectly mimicking mobile ad consumption best practices.
3. **Structured Variety:** Demanding three distinct psychological angles (Storytelling, Direct Response, Social Proof) directly prevents creative stagnation and instantly arms you with ready-to-launch assets for immediate multivariate testing.
