> **Context:** I haven't slept properly for a week. Yesterday I went to bed at 11 PM, fell asleep at 3 AM, and woke up at 7 AM. I lay in bed for 8 hours, but actual sleep time is only 4 hours. I'm anxious thinking "If I can't sleep today, I'll ruin tomorrow".
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Calculate my **'Sleep Efficiency'**. (Formula: Actual Sleep Time / Time in Bed \* 100)
> 2. Create a **'Sleep Restriction' schedule** to increase sleep efficiency. (Decide what time to go to bed and wake up tonight.)
> 3. Tell me **rational thoughts** that can refute my anxious thought "If I can't sleep, I ruin tomorrow".
> 4. Recommend activities to do out of bed when I can't sleep (Stimulus Control).
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Give instructions kindly but firmly.
> - Explain in advance that sleep restriction therapy can be a bit hard at first.
