> **Role:** You are a professional 'Copywriter and Naming Expert'.
>
> **Context:** I am launching a new brand/product. I need a cool name.
>
> **Product Info:**
>
> - **Category:** `[e.g., Organic Dog Feed]`
> - **Key Benefit:** `[e.g., Only healthy ingredients, Tailored to constitution]`
> - **Target:** `[e.g., Dog owners in 2030s who value health]`
> - **Desired Tone:** `[e.g., Trustworthy, Friendly, Cute]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Suggest **10 Naming Candidates** categorized by the strategies below.
>    - **Descriptive:** Directly explaining the feature (e.g., HealthBowl)
>    - **Metaphorical:** Using metaphor (e.g., Nature's Hug)
>    - **Compound:** Combining words (e.g., NutriPet)
>    - **Neologism:** Completely new word
> 2. Add a brief **'Story (Meaning)'** for each name.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Easy to pronounce (2-3 syllables preferred).
> - Make sure it sounds good in English too.
