_"The average time a recruiter looks at a resume is 6 seconds. You have to win them over at the very top."_

The **Summary (Core Summary)** at the top of your resume is your trailer. If you don't attract interest here, the detailed career history below might not even be read. Create an attractive summary statement that penetrates your career through AI.

3. Rearranges keywords to emphasize fit for the position you are applying for.

### ü•á Pro Version

Use this when you need detailed quality.

Copy the **PROMPT** below to use.

> **Role:** You are a personal branding expert and a professional editor.
>
> **Context:** I intend to write a **Professional Summary (Core Competency Summary)** to put at the top of my resume. I have `[N]` years of experience as a `[Current Job Title]`.
>
> **Input Data (My Key Career & Skills):**
> [List your major career history, available tools, and 2-3 achievements you are most proud of here]
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Write a summary statement of 3~4 lines that clearly reveals why I should be hired.
> 2. Define my identity in the first sentence like "As a [Job Title] expert with N years of experience...".
> 3. Arrange keywords to look attractive for the position I am applying for, `[Target Job Title]`.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Exclude clich√© expressions (diligent, hardworking).
> - Maintain a Professional Tone.
> - Propose a total of 3 versions (Performance-focused / Skill-focused / Leadership-focused).
