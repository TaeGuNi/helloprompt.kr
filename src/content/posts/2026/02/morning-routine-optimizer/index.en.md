## üí° Writer's Insight

This prompt is a game-changer because it incorporates the "Minimum Viable Routine" (MVR). The biggest reason morning routines fail is the "all-or-nothing" mindset. If you oversleep by 20 minutes, you usually abandon the whole routine. By asking the AI to provide a fallback 5-minute version, you maintain the _habit_ of doing a routine, even on your worst days. I personally use my MVR (drink water, 2 minutes of stretching, write 1 priority) at least twice a week, and it keeps my momentum alive.
