## 🩺 Developer Burnout Self-Test: Am I Lazy, or Am I Sick?

- **🎯 Target Audience:** Developers blaming themselves for a lack of motivation, or professionals spending entire weekends stuck in bed
- **⏱️ Time Required:** 3 minutes (Diagnosis & Actionable Prescription)
- **🤖 Recommended AI:** All conversational AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.)

- ⭐ **Difficulty:** ⭐☆☆☆☆
- ⚡️ **Effectiveness:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- 🚀 **Utility:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

> _"I used to love pulling all-nighters to code... Now, just launching my IDE fills me with absolute dread. Have I just gotten lazy?"_

No, you haven't become lazy—**your mental battery is simply depleted.** Burnout isn't just everyday fatigue; it's a physiological state where your brain essentially goes on strike. Instead of blaming yourself and forcing down yet another cup of coffee, confide your honest feelings to an AI counselor and receive an objective diagnosis. Rather than hollow words of encouragement like "cheer up," this prompt delivers a precise, actionable recovery prescription grounded in rigorous psychological scales.

## 🚀 Solution: "Burnout Doctor Prompt"

### 🥉 Basic Version

Use this when you want a quick gauge of how severe your current condition is.

> **Role:** You are an `[IT-specialized psychological counselor]`.
> **Task:** "I hate coding lately, and I feel incredibly lethargic. Ask me 5 core questions to diagnose whether this is just a temporary slump or actual burnout."

### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you need a systematic analysis and highly practical, actionable solutions.

> **Role:** You are a `[professional psychological counselor]` who has exclusively handled mental healthcare for IT industry professionals for over 10 years.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: I am a `[5]`-year `[backend developer]`. Recently, I've been physically and mentally exhausted due to `[frequent overtime and unreasonable project deadlines]`.
> - Symptoms: Even when I write code, I feel zero sense of accomplishment, and even replying to colleagues on Slack feels overwhelming. On weekends, I just lie in bed doing absolutely nothing.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **In-depth Analysis:** Analyze my symptoms based on the 3 core dimensions of the Maslach Burnout Inventory (MBI), a premier psychological burnout scale: emotional exhaustion, depersonalization, and reduced personal accomplishment.
> 2. **Objective Diagnosis:** Quantify and evaluate my current burnout risk level on a scale of 1 to 10.
> 3. **Customized Prescription:** Do not give unrealistic advice like "just rest unconditionally." Instead, provide 3 highly realistic, "developer-tailored" recovery routines that I can execute immediately tomorrow at work or right after clocking out, formulated as concrete micro-action plans.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Maintain an analytical and highly practical tone, strictly avoiding overly sentimental or hollow condolences.
> - Briefly add a medical disclaimer at the very end stating that this AI assessment cannot replace a professional psychiatric diagnosis.

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I really trust an AI's psychological diagnosis?**
  - A: An AI cannot replace a licensed psychiatrist. However, because this prompt is engineered to utilize the Maslach Burnout Inventory (MBI) framework—which is actively used in clinical psychology—it serves as an excellent "first-line screening tool" to objectively gauge the severity of your condition.

- **Q: The routines I received in my prescription seem too trivial. Is this correct?**
  - A: When you're in a state of burnout, your sheer willpower is entirely depleted. Grand goals like "go to the gym for 1 hour every day" will only act as poison. Starting with incredibly small, frictionless micro-routines like "lowering your monitor brightness" or "getting 10 minutes of sunlight during lunch" is the absolute core of the burnout recovery process.

- **Q: What should I do if the results indicate my condition is severe?**
  - A: If your risk level comes back exceptionally high (e.g., an 8 or above out of 10) and is actively disrupting your daily life, do not stop at the AI's advice. You must immediately seek out your company's Employee Assistance Program (EAP) or consult a professional therapist. You can even leverage the AI's objective analysis as supporting documentation when requesting time off or a reduced workload from your manager.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1.  **Applying a Scientific Framework (MBI):** Instead of asking for a generic diagnosis, we forced the AI to evaluate based on clear psychological metrics: 'emotional exhaustion', 'depersonalization', and 'reduced personal accomplishment'. This compels the AI to deliver a logical, multi-dimensional analysis rather than cliché words of comfort.
2.  **Realistic Constraints:** Acknowledging the grueling reality of developers, we proactively blocked impossible advice like "just take a month-long vacation" and strictly demanded immediately executable "micro-action plans," maximizing real-world utility.
