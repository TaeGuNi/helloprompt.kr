## üí° Writer's Insight (Insight)

Creating a compelling mystery isn't just about the murder itself; it's about the dance of misdirection. What makes this prompt exceptionally powerful is its insistence on _logical constraints_. By explicitly banning clich√©s like the "secret twin" trope, we force the AI to think harder and rely on structural brilliance‚Äîlike thermal expansion, psychological blind spots, or mechanical time-delays.

I highly recommend iterating on the **Decisive Clue** section. If the AI suggests a clue that feels too obvious, simply reply with: _"Make the clue more subtle. It should be something the police overlook during the initial investigation, but the detective realizes its importance later."_ This refines the 'Aha!' moment and drastically improves reader satisfaction.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this prompt work well with standard (free) AI models?**
  - A: Yes, it works decently. However, for highly complex physical tricks or airtight logical consistency, premium models like GPT-4 or Claude 3.5 Sonnet perform significantly better. They are less prone to creating logical loopholes in their trick designs.

- **Q: The tricks generated are sometimes too scientifically complex. What should I do?**
  - A: You can easily adjust the prompt! Add a constraint like: _"The trick must be understandable to a middle-school student and rely on everyday household objects rather than complex chemistry."_

- **Q: Can I use this for tabletop role-playing games (TRPGs) like Call of Cthulhu or Murder Mystery games?**
  - A: Absolutely. This is a game-changer for Game Masters (GMs). Just change the 'Context' line to _"I am designing a Murder Mystery TRPG scenario"_ to get interactive clues suited for players to discover through investigation rolls.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Dual Persona Injection:** Combining the names of two distinct masters (Agatha Christie for classic whodunit structure, Keigo Higashino for psychological depth) guides the AI's tone and creativity simultaneously.
2. **Strict Constraints:** Explicitly banning lazy tropes forces the AI to dig deeper into its latent knowledge of physics, psychology, and logic to construct a genuine puzzle.
3. **Structured Output (Trick -> Alibi -> Clue):** By breaking the mystery down into these three mandatory pillars, the output immediately becomes a usable, structurally sound plot rather than a rambling story summary.
