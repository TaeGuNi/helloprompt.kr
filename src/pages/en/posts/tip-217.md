The weakness question is not intended to fail you, but to see **'if you have self-objectification'** and **'if you have the will to improve'**. You need 'sophisticated honesty' by choosing a non-fatal weakness and presenting it with your efforts to overcome it.

2. Briefly mention past problem situations caused by the weakness.
3. Emphasize what efforts you are currently making to compensate for this (Solution).

> **My Actual Personality/Weakness Candidates:**
>

> 2. `[e.g.: Get very nervous]`
> 3. `[e.g.: A bit slow with hands]`
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Choose one subject from the candidates above that is least fatal to the job but can buy empathy. (Explain the reason too)
> 2. Write an answer script that shows this weakness is **'honestly admitted but being improved professionally'**.
> 3. Structure: [Admit Weakness] -> [Past Experience/Problem] -> [Current Effort to Overcome/Mechanism]
>
> **Constraints:**
