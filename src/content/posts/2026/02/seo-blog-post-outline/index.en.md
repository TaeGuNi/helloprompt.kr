### ü•á Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need an exhaustive, highly-converting content strategy. Copy the prompt below and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

> **Role (Role):** You are an `[SEO Content Marketing Expert]`.
>
> **Situation (Context):**
>
> - Target Keyword: `[Insert Keyword, e.g., iPad Drawing App Recommendations]`
> - Goal: Achieve top-tier exposure on Google search results and maximize click-through rates.
>
> **Request (Task):**
>
> 1. Analyze the search intent behind the target keyword and identify the core problems readers want to solve.
> 2. Create an SEO-optimized blog post outline, strictly organizing it with H1, H2, and H3 tags.
> 3. Provide exactly 5 highly engaging, click-worthy title (H1) candidates.
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Format the output using clean Markdown.
> - Ensure the structure flows logically, naturally incorporating LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keywords related to the main topic.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not generate actual blog content; provide only the structural outline, search intent analysis, and title suggestions.

## üí° Writer's Insight (Insight)

Creating content without an SEO outline is like building a house without a blueprint. As a content marketer, I always use this prompt before writing a single paragraph. By letting AI define the H2 and H3 structures based on search intent, you prevent "writer's block" and ensure your article covers all the semantic keywords Google expects to see. For maximum impact, take the generated outline and tweak the headings to sound slightly more conversational and human-like before you start drafting the main text!

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this work for niche or highly technical keywords?**
  - A: Absolutely. In fact, it excels there. For highly technical keywords, AI will structure the outline to answer the specific, complex questions your target audience is searching for. Just make sure to define the target audience in the `[Context]` section.

- **Q: Should I use ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini for this?**
  - A: All three perform exceptionally well for structural tasks. However, Claude 3.5 Sonnet tends to generate slightly more logical and human-like document structures, while Gemini is excellent if you need real-time search trends integrated into the outline.

## üß¨ Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Role Definition:** Assigning the "SEO Content Marketing Expert" persona forces the AI to prioritize search engine guidelines over creative but unoptimized structures.
2. **Search Intent Analysis:** It commands the AI to first understand _why_ the user is searching, ensuring the outline directly answers their underlying questions.
3. **Structured Hierarchy:** Mandating H1, H2, and H3 tags ensures the resulting outline is ready to be directly pasted into your CMS (WordPress, Ghost, Astro, etc.) with perfect semantic structure.
