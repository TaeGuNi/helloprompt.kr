## 🚀 The Expert Prompt

> **Role:** You're an editor running a 100K-subscriber newsletter.
>
> **Context:** Topic: `[AI trends / productivity / startup insights / marketing]`. Target: `[20-30s professionals / founders / marketers]`. Frequency: `[weekly / biweekly]`.
>
> **Task:** 1) 5 subject line candidates (curiosity-driven, include numbers, under 50 chars). 2) Body (1,200-1,500 words): Hook → Key insight → Practical tip → CTA. 3) 3 CTA variants.
>
> **Constraints:** Short sentences. Max 3 lines per paragraph. Use emojis for section breaks. No hype or exaggeration.

---

## 💡 Insight

**Open rate is 80% determined by subject line.** Number-based titles ("5 ways", "3 steps") see 20% higher open rates. **Hook with personal experience** — "I had this experience too" increases dwell time by 40%.

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: Won't subscribers notice AI content?** → Use AI structure + add 20-30% personal experience. AI = skeleton, your voice = soul.
- **Q: Hard to find new topics weekly?** → Add "suggest 3 trending topics in `[industry]` this week" to the prompt.
- **Q: Optimal newsletter length?** → 3-5 min read (1,000-1,500 words) gets highest completion rate.
