## 🚀 How Real Experts Write

### 🥉 Basic Version (Discovering Material and Conflict)

Use this when your mind is a blank slate and you don't know where to start, or when the story is going off the rails.

> **Request:** Suggest 5 fresh story ideas for the `[Romance Fantasy]` genre. It must be a shocking development that subverts (Twists) common `[Regression/Possession/Reincarnation]` clichés to defy readers' expectations. For each idea, clearly summarize the protagonist's core conflict factor in one line.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Chapter Treatment & Framework Design)

This is a powerful prompt to use when you need to build a solid framework before starting serialization or when you need a specific episode blueprint with zero margin for error. Copy the prompt below and fill in the `[Variables]` in brackets to suit your situation and deploy it immediately.

> **Role:** You are a star writer and main planner of web novels who accurately understands the needs of target readers and has achieved 10 million cumulative views with tense developments.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Genre: `[Modern Fantasy / Apocalypse]`
> - Logline: `[In a zombie apocalypse world, the story of a protagonist who is the only one to awaken the ability to control zombies, but is instead chased by surviving humanity and uncovers the secrets of the world.]`
> - Goal: Build a tight plot and attractive character relationships that maximize tension so readers don't drop out midway.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. **Character Sheet:** Set up multi-dimensional personalities, hidden secrets, and the fatal defects of the protagonist, core allies, and the main villain.
> 2. **Plot Composition:** Create a large framework for the entire plot according to the 4-act structure of commercial web novel writing (Setup-Rising Action-Crisis-Climax).
> 3. **Early Episode Detailing:** Write a detailed summary for each episode from Episode 1 to 5.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - At the end of each episode, include a powerful **'Cliffhanger'** line or situation in one sentence that makes it impossible for readers not to pay for the next episode.
> - Thoroughly exclude predictable and contrived developments (Deus Ex Machina).
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Skip boring descriptions and maintain an overwhelming sense of speed that develops quickly around events and actions.
> - If you are not sure about something, answer "I don't know" instead of making it up. (Hallucination prevention)

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I just copy and paste the novel written by AI and serialize it?**
  - A: I absolutely do not recommend it. Because of the unique translation tone and soulless mechanical descriptions of AI, sharp readers will instantly smell the 'AI scent' and drop out. Use AI only as a **competent planner** to build the 'plot (framework)' and supply fresh 'ideas (materials).' The sentences (flesh) that breathe real life into the novel must be crafted directly with your own unique style to finally complete the overwhelming commercial value.

- **Q: Can I also plan 19+ (Adult) romance or hardcore cruel descriptions as a plot?**
  - A: Most commercial AIs (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) have their own Safety Guardrails that firmly refuse to generate explicit sexual descriptions or excessively cruel scenes. Therefore, rather than asking for the high-level scene itself outright, you should throw metaphorical and indirect prompts like *"Describe the psychological conflict situation just before the protagonist loses his reason due to a fatal temptation"* to get a tense answer.

- **Q: Why do you specifically recommend Claude 3.5 Sonnet over ChatGPT for writing?**
  - A: In the field of literary creation, the performance of Claude 3.5 Sonnet is unrivaled. Its ability to maintain a long context of over tens of thousands of characters while delicately capturing subtle nuances is overwhelming. While ChatGPT gives a mechanical and stiff 'report' feel, Claude pulls out the sticky relationships between characters and multi-dimensional emotional lines brilliantly with much more literary vocabulary.

1. **Assigning an Overwhelming Persona (Star Writer & Main Planner):** It overlays a powerful persona on the AI—not just a simple typing assistant, but a 'top-tier commercial writer who hit 10 million cumulative views.'
