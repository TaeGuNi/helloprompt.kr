**Prep & Cook Time:** 8 minutes

**Ingredients Used:** Rice, Eggs (2), Spam (1 slice), Spinach (1 handful).

**Step-by-Step Guide:**
1. **Minute 1-2 (Prep):** Dice the Spam into small cubes. Blanch the spinach in a bowl of hot water from the kettle for 1 minute, then drain and squeeze out excess water. Season spinach with a drop of soy sauce or sesame oil if available.
