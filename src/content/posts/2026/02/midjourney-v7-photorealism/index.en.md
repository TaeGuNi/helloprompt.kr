### 🥇 Pro Version

Use this when you demand high-end studio quality, capturing everything down to the microscopic pores, peach fuzz, and floating dust particles.

> **Role:** You are a world-class commercial photographer with 20 years of experience, acting as a **'Midjourney v7 Prompt Engineer'** specializing in hyper-realistic rendering.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - I need an ultra-high-resolution photorealistic image depicting `[Insert your desired subject and mood, e.g., a woman in her 20s drinking coffee in a sunlit cafe]`.
> - This image will be used as the hero shot for a high-end lookbook or luxury magazine ad.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Write an English Midjourney prompt flawlessly combining the following 5 elements:
>    - **Subject:** Detailed description of facial expressions, gaze, clothing texture, skin peach fuzz, and pores.

>    - **Lighting:** Direction and type of light source (e.g., Cinematic lighting, Rembrandt lighting, Softbox, Golden hour).
>    - **Atmosphere/Color:** Film stock-based color grading (e.g., Moody tone, Kodak Portra 400, Fujifilm Superia).
>    - **Parameters:** You must append `--ar 16:9 --v 7 --style raw --q 2 --stylize 100` at the very end of the prompt.

> 3. Provide a brief explanation from an expert's perspective on why you chose this specific camera lens and lighting setup.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Prioritize an intuitive, comma-separated list of keywords over perfect, grammatically correct sentences.
> - Omit unnecessary filler words; focus strictly on core nouns and descriptive adjectives.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - NEVER include words that imply an illustration or CG render, such as '3D render', 'illustration', 'painting', or 'unreal engine'. It must strictly be a 'photograph'.

## 💡 Writer's Insight

The ultimate cheat code for unlocking Midjourney v7's true photorealistic potential lies in mastering **lighting** and **lens focal lengths**. Merely asking for "beautiful lighting" guarantees you'll be left with the heavily airbrushed, plastic-textured skin that screams "amateur AI generation."

To achieve commercial-grade output, you must dictate the precise nature of the light using specialized terms like **"Rembrandt lighting"** or **"window blind shadows."** Furthermore, pairing a telephoto lens parameter like `85mm f/1.2` with the keyword **"shallow depth of field"** authentically blurs the background. This creates a dramatic, cinematic depth of field that instantly anchors the viewer's focus on the subject. 

Unfamiliar with camera jargon? Don't worry. Executing the Pro Version above empowers ChatGPT to mathematically calculate the perfect lens and lighting equation for your specific scenario.

## 🙋 FAQ

- **Q: What exactly does the `--style raw` parameter do?**
  - A: It strips away the exaggerated, overly polished aesthetic that Midjourney applies by default. You absolutely need this parameter to preserve the authentic, slightly gritty texture of an unedited camera RAW file. It is entirely non-negotiable for true photorealistic output.

- **Q: In v6, six fingers and distorted limbs were a constant headache. Is v7 actually better?**
  - A: Yes. Midjourney v7 has eradicated 99% of finger deformities and joint structure anomalies. The engine now renders individual strands of hair and the subtle veins on the back of a hand flawlessly, meaning you can finally stop wasting hours on anatomical touch-ups in Photoshop.

- **Q: Can I write my prompt in a language other than English?**
  - A: While Midjourney supports multiple languages, its core training data makes English the most effective. This is precisely why our strategy **utilizes ChatGPT as an intermediary prompt engineer**—effortlessly translating your raw concepts into perfectly structured, highly optimized English parameters.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **The Expert Persona (Role Play):** Injecting ChatGPT with the persona of a "20-year global commercial photographer" forces the LLM to access highly specialized vocabulary regarding camera bodies, lenses, and lighting, completely bypassing generic adjectives.
