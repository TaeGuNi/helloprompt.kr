Charlie Puth consistently drops global hits inspired by the most mundane sounds—a passing car horn, a squeaky mattress, or even a shattering cup. The essence of high-converting copywriting works exactly the same way. So, what happens if we infuse an AI with Charlie Puth’s signature "sense of rhythm" and "art of variation"? The results are staggering. ## 🚀 Solution: "Charlie Puth-Style Hook Maker"

### 🥉 Basic Version (Basic)

Use this when you need quick, punchy results on the fly.

> **Role:** You are `[Genius Pop Artist Charlie Puth]`.
> **Task:** Based on `[Ordinary Product Feature]`, create 3 short, impactful, and instantly catchy marketing hooks optimized for TikTok or Reels.

### 🥇 Pro Version (Expert)

Use this when you need top-tier, production-ready creative direction.

> **Role:** You are `[Charlie Puth]`, a genius producer and marketer who creates worldwide viral hit songs by remixing ordinary, everyday materials.
>
> **Context:**
>
> - Background: You need to turn `[The Most Boring and Ordinary Feature of a Product or Service]` into irresistible marketing material engineered to go viral on TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
> - Goal: To extract powerful, scroll-stopping text hooks and sensational video direction ideas that embed themselves in the consumer's mind within exactly 3 seconds.
>
> **Task:**
>
> 1. Completely dismantle the provided product features and reconstruct them as intuitive visual and auditory stimuli.
> 2. Write 3 magnetic hooking phrases that will force the `[Target Audience]` to unconsciously stop scrolling.
> 3. Pair each phrase with a specific background music (BGM) style or sound effect idea that maximizes its emotional impact.
>
> **Constraints:**
>
> - Output your response strictly as a Markdown bulleted list using emojis. Never use a table format.
> - Do not use a single word of corporate jargon. Craft bouncy, rhythmic sentences that even an elementary school student can instantly understand and vibe with.
>
> **Warning:**
>
> - Do not invent exaggerated benefits or fabricate facts. You must strictly adhere to the actual, proven specs of the product. (Zero hallucinations allowed).

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can this actually be applied to rigid B2B software or intangible services?**
  - A: Absolutely. In fact, the more boring and rigid a product appears (like B2B SaaS), the exponentially higher Click-Through Rate (CTR) you can achieve. The sheer contrast between a dry corporate tool and a sensational, rhythmic hook creates massive intrigue.
- **Q: How am I supposed to use the AI's suggested BGM ideas in my actual workflow?**
  - A: Hand the text verbatim to your video editor or producer. AI-generated descriptions like "ASMR-style mechanical keyboard typing" or "a heavy bass drum accelerating like a panicked heartbeat" function as flawless, highly intuitive audio references.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

1. **Persona Injection:** Instead of assigning a flat, generic "marketer" role, injecting the highly specific, rhythmic identity of a real genius like "Charlie Puth" elevates the AI's creative spectrum to an entirely different dimension.
2. **Visualizing the Auditory:** By forcing the AI to treat the copy not just as text, but as a synesthetic experience paired with BGM and sound effects, we instantly unlock production-ready scripts tailored perfectly for short-form video.
3. **Ruthless Constraints:** By establishing iron-clad rules like "No industry jargon" and "No tables," we force the AI to generate highly intuitive, digestible outputs completely optimized for mobile consumption.
