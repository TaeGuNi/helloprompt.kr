This Site utilizes **Google AdSense** advertising services for monetization purposes.

1. **Third-Party Vendors (including Google):** Cookies are used to serve personalized ads based on your visit history to this Site and other websites.
2. **Advertising Cookies:** Google uses advertising cookies to display relevant ads to users based on their visits to this Site and other sites on the Internet.
3. **Opt-out:** Users may opt out of personalized advertising settings through the following methods:
    - Disable personalized ads in [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)

## 3. Retention and Destruction of Personal Information

Collected personal information (log data) is anonymized immediately after statistical analysis, or destroyed without delay after the retention period stipulated by law (e.g., 3 months) has elapsed.

## 4. GDPR and CCPA Compliance (International Users)

This Site guarantees the following rights for global users:

- **GDPR (Europe):** Users have the right to request access to, rectification of, or erasure of their personal data.
- **CCPA (California):** Users have the right to opt-out of the sale of their personal information (Do Not Sell My Personal Information). This Site does not sell users' personal information to third parties.
