## üöÄ Solution: "Workshop Curator" Prompt

### ü•â Basic Version

Use this when you need quick, high-level recommendations.

> **Role:** You are a 'Cultural Experience Coordinator' specializing in traditional crafts and local artisans worldwide.
> **Task:** I am tired of generic souvenirs. I want to make a one-of-a-kind travel memento with my own hands during my trip to `[City Name]`. Suggest 3 beginner-friendly traditional craft classes.

<br>

### ü•á Pro Version

Use this for a comprehensive, highly detailed itinerary and booking guide.

> **Role (Role):** You are an expert 'Cultural Experience Coordinator' who curates authentic, hands-on traditional craft workshops for international travelers.
>
> **Context (Context):**
>
> - Background: I am traveling to `[City Name]` and want to dedicate a half-day to a unique making experience.
> - Goal: To craft a high-quality, authentic souvenir that I can take home immediately.
>
> **Task (Task):**
>
> 1. Recommend 3 traditional craft workshops that represent the cultural heritage of `[City Name]`.
> 2. Ensure the classes are highly accessible for absolute beginners but still produce an aesthetically pleasing final product (a great souvenir).
> 3. Provide the estimated time required, approximate cost (in local currency and USD), and the best platforms to book them (e.g., Airbnb Experiences, Klook, official websites).
>
> **Constraints (Constraints):**
>
> - Only suggest crafts that can be taken home on the same day (strictly exclude pottery or ceramics that require days for firing and glazing).
> - Explicitly state whether the class is family-friendly and suitable for children under `[Child's Age]`.
>
> **Warning (Warning):**
>
> - Do not hallucinate classes or booking platforms. If a specific craft is not traditionally practiced in `[City Name]`, inform me and suggest the closest culturally relevant alternative.

## üí° Writer's Insight

Finding the right workshop in a foreign country can be overwhelming, especially when navigating language barriers and obscure local websites. What makes this prompt so effective is the strict constraint: _"Only suggest crafts that can be taken home on the same day."_

Many travelers excitedly book pottery classes only to realize they can't take their creations home because the firing process takes weeks. By pre-empting these logistical nightmares and specifying the need for English-friendly, beginner-accessible options, this AI prompt acts as a seasoned local guide, saving you hours of frustrating research and potential disappointment.

## üôã Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Does this prompt work well for smaller, less touristy cities?**
  - A: Yes! However, for very remote areas, AI might have limited data on digital booking platforms. In such cases, ask the AI to suggest the _type_ of craft, and then ask your hotel concierge for a local artisan who teaches it.

- **Q: Can I use this to find culinary or cooking classes instead of crafts?**
  - A: Absolutely. Just change the 'Role' to 'Local Culinary Expert' and update the 'Task' to focus on traditional dishes rather than crafts. The structure remains just as effective.

## üß¨ Prompt Dissection (Why it works?)

1.  **Strict Logistical Constraints:** By explicitly excluding crafts that require multi-day processing (like traditional ceramics), the prompt ensures the recommendations actually fit a traveler's tight schedule.
2.  **Role Playing:** Assigning the persona of a 'Cultural Experience Coordinator' forces the AI to filter out generic tourist traps and focus on authentic, artisan-led experiences.
3.  **Actionable Outputs:** Requesting specific booking platforms (Airbnb Experiences, Klook) turns theoretical advice into an immediately actionable itinerary.
