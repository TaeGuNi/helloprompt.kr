## 💡 Writer's Insight

When we hear "Terms of Service," we usually picture a massive, boring wall of legal text. However, Hello Prompt's terms are more like a brief mutual promise on **"How we will protect and nurture this space of knowledge."**

In particular, **Article 3 (Limitation of Liability)** is something we all must keep in mind in the AI era. AI is a fantastic, tireless Copilot, but never forget that you are the Pilot—the final decision-maker who bears the responsibility. Rather than blindly trusting the provided prompts, developing the habit of tweaking and validating them to fit your specific context is the true beginning of 'Prompt Engineering.'

## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: Can I use the prompts here for my company's work?**
  - A: Absolutely! We highly encourage you to use them to boost your productivity at work, as this falls under 'personal/professional use'. (However, compiling these prompts to resell them as a paid e-book or course is strictly prohibited.)

- **Q: What if the AI model misunderstands the prompt and gives a weird answer?**
  - A: Outputs can vary depending on the characteristics and versions of the AI models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, etc.). Try adjusting the variables slightly by referring to the 'Writer's Insight' and 'Prompt Anatomy' sections in each post. If the error persists, it might be an AI hallucination, so fact-checking is mandatory.

- **Q: How will I know if the terms are updated?**
  - A: If there are significant changes to the terms, we will notify you well in advance of the effective date via announcements on the blog's main page or through our email newsletter.

## 🧬 Prompt Anatomy (Why it works?)

The secret to maximizing the readability of what is technically a legal document lies in the following:

1.  **Clarity:** We stripped away unnecessary legal jargon and archaic terms, rewriting them in everyday language that anyone can intuitively understand.
2.  **Structure:** By layering the information into Basic (Summary) and Pro (Detailed) sections, we designed it so readers can choose the level of detail that fits their current situation and available time.
